§147-97  Disposition of fees.  All fees collected under this part shall be paid into a special fund established by the department of agriculture and shall be expended for the purposes of this part. [L 1957, c 172, §1(f); Supp, §22-96; HRS §147-97; am L 2003, c 49, §7]

§147-78  Seller's invoice.  Every person, in selling eggs to a retailer, shall furnish to the retailer an invoice showing the exact grade or quality and the size or weight of the eggs according to the standards prescribed by the department of agriculture.  A copy of the invoice shall be kept on file by the person selling and by the retailer at their respective places of business for a period of thirty days, and shall be available for inspection at all reasonable times by accredited inspectors or representatives of the department.

§438-10  Temporary permits; apprentice permits.  (a)  A temporary permit may be issued upon application for examination and payment of the required fees.  The temporary permit shall allow the qualified applicant to practice barbering under the supervision of a licensed barber or beauty operator with hairdresser qualifications and shall be effective for the period of time covering four consecutive examinations offered by the board after the permit's date of issuance.  If the applicant has not obtained licensure after four consecutive examinations offered by the board, the applicant shall be required to apply and train as an apprentice for six months before qualifying for another series of examinations.

     (b)  An apprentice permit shall be used by the apprentice to obtain the hours of training needed to qualify for a barber's license.  An apprentice shall obtain an apprentice permit before beginning apprenticeship training.

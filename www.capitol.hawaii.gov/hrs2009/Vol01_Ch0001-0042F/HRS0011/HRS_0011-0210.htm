§11-210  Study and recommendation.  At least one year prior to a primary, special primary, or general election, the commission shall submit to the legislature:

     (1)  A study and recommendation of reasonable campaign expenditure and contribution limits and the factors which may be relevant in their establishment; and

     (2)  A report concerning the status of the Hawaii election campaign fund, and shall request an appropriation if the total amount of revenues comprising the fund is insufficient to partially finance all candidates for a particular primary, special primary, or general election as set forth in section 11-218. [L 1979, c 224, pt of §2]

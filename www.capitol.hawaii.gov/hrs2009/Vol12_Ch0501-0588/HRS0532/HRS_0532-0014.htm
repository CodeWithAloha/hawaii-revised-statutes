§532-14  Disposition by State.  If the intestate leaves no kindred, the intestate's estate shall be disposed of as provided in chapter 523A. [CC 1859, §1451; RL 1925, §3315; RL 1935, §4823; RL 1945, §12083; RL 1955, §318-14; HRS §532-14; am L 1974, c 75, §6; am L 1985, c 68, §25]

[§302D-2]  Existing charter schools.  Any charter school holding a charter to operate under part IV, subpart D, of chapter 302A, as that subpart existed before July 11, 2006, and any charter school holding a charter to operate under chapter 302B as it existed before June 19, 2012, shall be considered a charter school for the purposes of this chapter under a charter contract with the commission unless the charter contract is revoked, transferred to another authorizer, or not renewed, or the charter school voluntarily closes. [L 2012, c 130, pt of §2]

 

Revision Note

 

  "June 19, 2012" substituted for "the enactment of this chapter".

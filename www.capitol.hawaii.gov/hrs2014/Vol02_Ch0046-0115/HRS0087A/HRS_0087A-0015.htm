PART III.  BOARD POWERS AND DUTIES

 

     [§87A-15]  Administration of the fund.  The board shall administer and carry out the purpose of the fund.  Health and other benefit plans shall be provided at a cost affordable to both the public employers and the public employees. [L 2001, c 88, pt of §1]

§88-58  Prior service credit while per diem employee.  Under such rules as are adopted by board of trustees, all members who formerly filled per diem positions after December 31, 1927, in the various counties as road maintenance workers, shall be allowed full prior service credit in the system for their per diem service. [L 1959, c 173, §§1, 2; am L 1960, c 22, §§2, 3; am L 1964, c 62, §2; Supp, §6-36.8; HRS §88-58; am L 1969, c 110, pt of §1; am L 1982, c 165, §2(11)]

 

Attorney General Opinions

 

  Section (1969) neither required nor permitted ERS to include entirety of judges' per diem terms in computation of their retirement benefits.  Att. Gen. Op. 97-11.

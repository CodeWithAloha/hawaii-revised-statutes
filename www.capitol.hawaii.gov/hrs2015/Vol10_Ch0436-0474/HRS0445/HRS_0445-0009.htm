§445-9  Canceled on transferring, etc., business; penalty.  If any licensee closes out, transfers, or assigns the business for which a license is held, during the term for which the same was issued, the licensee shall within thirty days from the date of such closing out, transfer, or assignment, notify the treasurer of such fact in writing and return the license to the treasurer for cancellation, under a penalty not to exceed $100 for failure to do so.

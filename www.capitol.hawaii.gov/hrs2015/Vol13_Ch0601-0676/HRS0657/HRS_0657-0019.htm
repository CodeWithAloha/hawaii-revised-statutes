§657-19  Extension by injunction.  Whenever the commencement of any action is stayed by an injunction of any court, the time during which the injunction is in force shall not be deemed any portion of the time limited in this part or section 663-3 for the commencement of the action. [CC 1859, §1043; RL 1925, §2653; RL 1935, §3924; RL 1945, §10435; RL 1955, §241-18; HRS §657-19; am L 1972, c 105, §1(k)]

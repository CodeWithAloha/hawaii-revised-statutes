[§514E-14]  Preexisting time share units and plans.  Time share units and time share plans for existing units which were created in a project prior to June 29, 1980, shall, within six months after June 29, 1980, comply with this chapter, except as to section 514E-5. [L 1980, c 186, pt of §1]

 

Revision Note

 

  "June 29, 1980" substituted for "the effective date of this Act" and "the effective date".

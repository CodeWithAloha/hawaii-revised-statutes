§373-14 Revocation and cancellation. In addition to any other actions authorized by law, after affording all interested parties reasonable opportunity for a fair hearing, the director may revoke or cancel any license for cause. In addition to any other grounds for disciplinary action authorized by law, cause means violation of this chapter or rule of the director. [L 1959, c 264, pt of §3; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §27; Supp, §88A-14; HRS §373-14; am L 1980, c 302, pt of §2; am L 1992, c 202, §3]

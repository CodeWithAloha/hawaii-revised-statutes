§373-4 Bond. Each licensed employment agency shall give and keep in force a bond with the director in the penal sum of $5,000 with good and sufficient surety or sureties approved by the director, conditioned:

(1) That the licensee shall not violate this chapter.

(2) That the licensee shall faithfully, promptly, and truly refund all fees illegally or incorrectly obtained from applicants to the director. [L 1959, c 264, pt of §3; am L 1961, c 45, §1; Supp, §88A-4; HRS §373-4; am L 1980, c 302, pt of §2]

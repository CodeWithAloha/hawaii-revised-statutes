Case Notes

"Availability for work" - picket duty in itself does not affect availability, nor does refusal of own job. 46 H. 140, 377 P.2d 715.

Subsection (b) creates limited exception to "any week of unemployment". 56 H. 590, 546 P.2d 1.

Adoption of circuit court decisions for use by departmental referees without following requirements of chapter 91 appears to be contrary to intent and purposes of HAPA. 62 H. 286, 614 P.2d 380.

Registration waiver for strikers pursuant to agency rule was valid waiver "by regulation"; waiver of work registration not preempted by federal law. 68 H. 316, 713 P.2d 943.

Cited: 44 H. 93, 94, 352 P.2d 856; 46 H. 164, 173, 377 P.2d 932.

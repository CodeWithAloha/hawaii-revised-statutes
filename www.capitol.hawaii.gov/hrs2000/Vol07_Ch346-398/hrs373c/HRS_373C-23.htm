§373C-23 Responsibilities. In consultation with the Hawaii state occupational information coordinating committee and other potential users, the department of labor and industrial relations shall be responsible for the preparation of occupational employment demand information concerning projected needs for occupational skills in Hawaii's labor market. The department of education and University of Hawaii systems shall be responsible for the production of uniform public and private educational supply data concerning students in courses related to each occupational category in consultation with the committee and other potential users.

The state employment and training council in consultation with the committee and other potential users shall be responsible for the production of federal training supply data concerning trainees enrolled and completing training programs in each occupational category. The department of human services shall be responsible for preparing information concerning training activities and other rehabilitative data required by the occupational information system in consultation with the committee and other potential users.

All of the identified entities in this section shall jointly develop and produce, to the extent feasible, other data required of the occupational information system. [L 1983, c 193, pt of §1; am L 1987, c 339, §4]

[§453-32.6] Delegation to committee of practicing emergency physicians and emergency ambulance personnel. The board of medical examiners shall establish a committee consisting of practicing emergency physicians and emergency ambulance personnel to assist the board in the performance of duties under this part.

The board, by written order, may delegate to the committee any of its powers and duties for administration of this part, except that the board shall not delegate any authority to adopt, amend, or repeal rules, take disciplinary action against a certificate holder, or restore a certificate which has been revoked. [L 1984, c 168, pt of §2]

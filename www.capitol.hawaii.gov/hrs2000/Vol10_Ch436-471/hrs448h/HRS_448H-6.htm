§448H-6 Qualifications for license. No person shall be licensed as an elevator mechanic unless the person has satisfactorily passed the examination administered by the board, and satisfactorily completed at least two years of training under the supervision of a licensed, registered, or journey worker elevator mechanic. [L 1971, c 190, pt of §1; am L 1973, c 44, §1; gen ch 1985, 1993]

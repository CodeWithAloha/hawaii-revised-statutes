§440-25 Buildings to conform to legal requirements. All buildings or structures used or intended to be used for conducting, holding, or giving boxing contests shall in all respects conform to all governmental laws, ordinances, or regulations applicable thereto. [L 1929, c 216, §28; RL 1935, §7015; RL 1945, §7576; RL 1955, §165-27; HRS §440-25; am L 1983, c 241, §19]

§440-29 Limit of weight difference between contestants. No boxing contest shall be allowed in which the difference in weight between the respective contestants exceeds the limits which the boxing commission prescribes in its rules. [L 1929, c 216, §32; RL 1935, §7019; RL 1945, §7580; RL 1955, §165-31; HRS §440-29; am L 1983, c 241, §22]

§440-22 Sham boxing contest; forfeiture of license. Any person, club, corporation, organization, or association who conducts, holds, or gives or participates in any sham or fake boxing contest, knowing the same to be a sham or fake, shall forfeit the license issued in accordance with this chapter, and the same shall thereupon be canceled and declared void by the boxing commission. The person, club, corporation, organization, or association shall not thereafter be entitled to receive and shall not be given another license. [L 1929, c 216, §25; RL 1935, §7012; RL 1945, §7573; RL 1955, §165-24; HRS §440-22]

§441-45 Penalty. In addition to the penalties otherwise provided by law, any licensee who violates, or omits to comply with any of the provisions of this chapter or rules adopted pursuant thereto shall be fined not more than $1,000 for each violation. [L 1967, c 199, pt of §1; HRS §441-45; am L 1981, c 221, §22; am L 1985, c 101, §24; am L 1988, c 73, §3]

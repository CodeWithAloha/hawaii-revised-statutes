Note

L 1997, c 235, §12 provides:

"SECTION 12. All rules, guidelines, and other material adopted or developed by the board of nursing home administrators shall remain in full force and effect until amended or repealed by the department of commerce and consumer affairs pursuant to chapter 91, Hawaii Revised Statutes. In the interim, every reference to the board of nursing home administrators in those rules, guidelines, and other material is amended to refer to the director or department of commerce and consumer affairs as appropriate."

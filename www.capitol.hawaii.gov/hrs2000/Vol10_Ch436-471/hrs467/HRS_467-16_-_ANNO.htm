Case Notes

Appellants’ contention that phrase "damages sustained", as used in this section, includes punitive damages was erroneous. 76 H. 39, 868 P.2d 457.

"Any person aggrieved" means any person-claimant involved in one transaction. 4 H. App. 552, 670 P.2d 459.

§471-13 Violations; penalties. Any person who violates or fails to comply with any provision of this chapter shall be fined not more than $5,000 for each violation. Each day that the person is in violation of section 471-2 shall be a separate violation for the purposes of this section. [L 1949, c 280, pt of §1; RL 1955, §73-13; HRS §471-13; am L 1986, c 153, §4]

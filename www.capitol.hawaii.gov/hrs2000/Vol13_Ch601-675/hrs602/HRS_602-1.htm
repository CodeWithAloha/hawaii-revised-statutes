PART I. SUPREME COURT

Rules of Court

See generally Supreme Court Rules; Hawaii Rules of Appellate Procedure.

§602-1 How constituted. The supreme court, pursuant to section 2 of Article VI of the Constitution, shall consist of a chief justice and four associate justices.

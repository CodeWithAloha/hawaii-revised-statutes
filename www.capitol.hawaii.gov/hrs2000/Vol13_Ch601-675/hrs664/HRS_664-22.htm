§664-22 Jurisdiction. The circuit courts shall have jurisdiction within their respective circuits to hear and determine without the intervention of a jury all controversies respecting the fencing of land and the maintenance of fences in accordance with this part. [L 1931, c 101, §2; RL 1935, §3672; RL 1945, §10213; RL 1955, §234-21; HRS §664-22; am L 1972, c 90, §6(f)]

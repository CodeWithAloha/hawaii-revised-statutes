§651-36 Execution, courts of record; form. The forms of executions to be issued from courts of record shall be the same as have been established by the usage and practice of such courts; but alterations therein may, from time to time, be made or allowed by the supreme court, when necessary to adapt them to changes in the law, or for other sufficient reasons. [CC 1859, §1019; RL 1925, §2440; RL 1935, §4139; RL 1945, §10166; RL 1955, §233-35; HRS §651-36]

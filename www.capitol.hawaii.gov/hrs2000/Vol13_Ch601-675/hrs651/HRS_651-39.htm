§651-39 Available in all circuits. Any writ of execution or other writ issued for the purpose of carrying into effect any final order, judgment, or decree of any circuit court or circuit judge at chambers shall be available against the property of the person against whom the writ shall issue in whatever circuit the property is situated and the writ shall be as effective for all purposes in each of the circuits as in the circuit where it is issued. [L 1905, c 10, §1; RL 1925, §2443; RL 1935, §4142; RL 1945, §10169; RL 1955, §233-38; HRS §651-39]

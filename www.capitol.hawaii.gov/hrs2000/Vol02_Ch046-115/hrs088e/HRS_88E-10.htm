[§88E-10] Custodian of the funds. [Section repealed July 1, 2002. L 2000, c 253, §137.] The state director of finance shall be the custodian of the funds created under the plan. All payments from the fund shall be made by the director only upon vouchers signed by the chairperson of the board and countersigned by other persons designated by the board. [L Sp 1981 1st, c 7, pt of §1]

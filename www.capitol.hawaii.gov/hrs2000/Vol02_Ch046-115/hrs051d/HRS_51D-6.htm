[§51D-6] Transit capital development fund; appropriation. Each year beginning with fiscal year 1992 through fiscal year 2008, the legislature shall appropriate to the transit capital development fund created by section 51D-3 the sum of $53,000,000 or so much as may be deemed necessary for the purpose of financially assisting counties for capital costs in developing mass transportation. [L 1990, c 183, pt of §2]

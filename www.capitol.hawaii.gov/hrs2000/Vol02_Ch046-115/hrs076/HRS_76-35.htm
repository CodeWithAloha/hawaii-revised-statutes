§76-35 Intragovernmental transfers. [Section repealed July 1, 2002, L 2000, c 253, §56.] A transfer of an employee in the civil service from one position to another position within a department or between two departments may be made without a reduction in pay and with the approval of the department head or heads and the director of human resources development as provided by rule.

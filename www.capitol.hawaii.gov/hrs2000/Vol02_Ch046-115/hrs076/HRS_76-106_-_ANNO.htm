Attorney General Opinions

A full-time public employee is not ipso facto precluded from engaging in other governmental employment; additional public employment might come within scope of "outside employment". Att. Gen. Op. 65-17.

Eligibility of state civil service employee to serve on advisory commission. Att. Gen. Op. 69-5.

Dual public positions are permissible, if both positions are compatible. Att. Gen. Op. 81-13.

"Outside employment" refers to private employment. Att. Gen. Op. 81-13.

§76-44 Racial, sex, age, religious, color, ancestry, marital status, or political consideration barred. [Section repealed July 1, 2002, L 2000, c 253, §60.] No person holding any position in the civil service shall be suspended, demoted, or dismissed from the person's position on racial, sex, age, religious, color, ancestry, marital status, or political grounds. [L 1955, c 274, pt of §1; RL 1955, §3-22; HRS §76-44; am L 1973, c 177, §1(2); am L 1975, c 109, §2; gen ch 1985]

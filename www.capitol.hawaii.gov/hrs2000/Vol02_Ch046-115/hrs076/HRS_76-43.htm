§76-43 Layoff. [Section effective until June 30, 2002. For section effective July 1, 2002, see below.] Rules and regulations shall be promulgated by the director of human resources development to govern the conditions under which an employee is to be released from the employee's position due to lack of work or lack of funds. [L 1965, c 146, §1; Supp, §3-21(x); HRS §76-43; gen ch 1985; am L 1994, c 56, §21]

§76-43 Layoff. [Section effective July 1, 2002. For section effective until June 30, 2002, see above.] When it is necessary to release employees due to lack of work, lack of funds, or other legitimate reasons, employees with permanent appointments in civil service positions shall have layoff rights. Layoffs shall be made in accordance with procedures negotiated under chapter 89 or established under chapter 89C, as applicable. [L 1965, c 146, §1; Supp, §3-21(x); HRS §76-43; gen ch 1985; am L 1994, c 56, §21; am L 2000, c 253, §25]

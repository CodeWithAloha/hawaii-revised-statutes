§76-56 Annual report. [Section repealed July 1, 2002. L 2000, c 253, §66.] The state director of human resources development shall make a report to the governor and to the members of the legislature not later than February 15 of each year, which report shall review the operations of each respective department and the administration of the personnel system for the preceding year. The report shall also contain recommendations in the laws relating to the personnel system, deemed by the director to be necessary or desirable to further promote the merit system for public employment. [L 1955, c 274, pt of §1; RL 1955, §3-15; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §11; HRS §76-56; am L 1994, c 56, §21; am L 1998, c 53, §1]

§76-73 Expenses. [Section repealed July 1, 2002. L 2000, c 253, §68.] When any member of the commission is required to travel to and from the site of a commission meeting or from any island to another island in the State in the performance of the commission's duties, the commissioner shall be allowed reasonable traveling expenses. [L 1955, c 274, pt of §1; RL 1955, pt of §3-13; am L 1957, c 252, §1; HRS §76-73; am L 1977, c 61, pt of §1]

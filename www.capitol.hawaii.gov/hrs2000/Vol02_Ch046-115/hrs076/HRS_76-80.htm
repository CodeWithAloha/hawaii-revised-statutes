§76-80 Additional duties of county director. [Section repealed July 1, 2002. L 2000, c 253, §72.] In addition to the duties prescribed in section 76-13, the county personnel director shall report to the commission the director's findings and recommendations resulting from the investigations under section 76-13(7).

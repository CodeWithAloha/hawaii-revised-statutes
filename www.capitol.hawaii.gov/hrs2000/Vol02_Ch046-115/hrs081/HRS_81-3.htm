§81-3 Financing of operations. Funds necessary for the development and operation of in-service training programs and activities shall be provided by general fund appropriations. Additional revenues may be provided through the charging of fees as may be necessary to conduct programs for participating agencies and shall be made part of a revolving fund. [L 1978, c 48, pt of §2(3)]

§81-1 In-service training. The director of human resources development shall initiate and provide suitable in-service training programs so that the quality of service rendered by government employees may be continually improved. The director shall be responsible for the coordination of the in-service training activities of all departments of government with related activities conducted by the University of Hawaii or the department of education.

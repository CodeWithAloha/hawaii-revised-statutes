Attorney General Opinions

Employee covered by this section continues to earn vacation credits during employee's absence, any rule to the contrary notwithstanding. Att. Gen. Op. 68-12.

To earn vacation credits, not necessary that employee be on leave-with-pay status; provisions not applicable to period before July 1, 1967. Att. Gen. Op. 68-29.

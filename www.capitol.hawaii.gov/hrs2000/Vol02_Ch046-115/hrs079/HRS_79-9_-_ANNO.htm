Attorney General Opinions

Payment of portion of benefits that is made up from employee's sick leave allowance is mandatory and not subject to employee's election. (Prior law.) Att. Gen. Op. 79-6.

(Under prior law requiring supplementation from sick leave credits,) where wage loss benefits are payable by a no-fault insurer, sick leave credits are charged only if the sum of the no-fault benefits and the workers' compensation benefits is less than the regular salary. Att. Gen. Op. 81-3.

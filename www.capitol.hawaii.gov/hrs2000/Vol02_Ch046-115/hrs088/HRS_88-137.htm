§88-137 Ordinary death benefit. If any service member dies, the ordinary death benefit provided in section 88-84 shall be paid to the service member's estate or the service member's designated beneficiary. [L 1951, c 262, pt of §3; RL 1955, §6-116; HRS §88-127; am L 1969, c 110, pt of §1; gen ch 1985]

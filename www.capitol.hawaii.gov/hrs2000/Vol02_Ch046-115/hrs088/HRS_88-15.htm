§88-15 Bonus; waiver by veteran. Any veteran who may qualify for a nonservice-connected pension through the veterans administration may waive any portion or all of the benefits that the veteran may receive under sections 88-11 to 88-16. [L 1961, c 175, pt of §12; Supp, §6-254; HRS §88-15; am L 1969, c 110, pt of §1; gen ch 1985]

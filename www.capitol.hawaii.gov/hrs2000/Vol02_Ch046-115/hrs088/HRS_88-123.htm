§88-123 Amount of annual contributions by the State and counties. The contribution payable in each year to the pension accumulation fund by the State and by each county, respectively, shall be determined by allocating the sum of the normal cost and the accrued liability contribution for (1) police officers, firefighters, and corrections officers, the latter after the actual transfer of all county jails pursuant to executive order of the governor, and (2) all other employees in the same proportion as the aggregate annual compensation of each group employed by the State and by each county, respectively, as of March 31 of the valuation year.

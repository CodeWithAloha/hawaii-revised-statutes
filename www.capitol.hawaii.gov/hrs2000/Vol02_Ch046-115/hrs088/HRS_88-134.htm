§88-134 Service retirement benefit. If a service member has terminated the service member's service with the armed forces and otherwise complies with the requirements of section 88-73, the service member shall be entitled to a service retirement benefit to be computed as provided in section 88-74, including and taking into consideration the service credit preserved and allowed to the service member by the Servicemen's Act. [L 1951, c 262, pt of §3; RL 1955, §6-113; HRS §88-124; am L 1969, c 110, pt of §1; gen ch 1985]

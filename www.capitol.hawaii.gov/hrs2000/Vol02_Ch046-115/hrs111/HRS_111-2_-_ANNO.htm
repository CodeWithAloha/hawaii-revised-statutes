Case Notes

City building department and the city department of housing and community development are state agencies. 61 H. 144, 598 P.2d 161.

Tenants forced to vacate when city building department ordered landlord to demolish building deemed to be in substandard condition were persons displaced "as a result of a governmental program of...code enforcement activities". 61 H. 144, 598 P.2d 161.

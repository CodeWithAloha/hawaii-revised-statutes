§87-5 Reimbursement for state contributions. All state and county agencies having control of special funds shall reimburse the State for contributions made by the State pursuant to section 87-4 on account of the employees in the agencies whose compensation is paid in whole or part from the special funds. All state and county agencies receiving federal funds, which may be expended for the purpose of absorbing the contributions payable by the State to the fund, shall set aside a portion of the federal funds sufficient to reimburse the State for contributions made by the State pursuant to section 87-4 on account of the employees in the agencies whose compensation is paid in whole or part from federal funds. [L 1964, c 43, §1; Supp, §5A-4.1; HRS §87-5]

§325-3 Others to report. The director of health shall have the authority to determine which other persons shall report to the department of health communicable diseases or conditions dangerous to the public health. The director of health may assess an administrative fine not to exceed $1,000 per violation against persons who refuse or neglect to report immediately such diseases or conditions to the department of health. Persons assessed an administrative fine under this section shall not be subject to other sanctions provided by this chapter.

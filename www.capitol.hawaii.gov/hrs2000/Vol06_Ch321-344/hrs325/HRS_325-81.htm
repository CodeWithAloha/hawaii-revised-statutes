§325-81 Right, private physician, etc. Any person who has been ordered to be isolated and hospitalized under section 325-79 shall be permitted to be examined and treated at any time by the person's private physician or physicians if the person is isolated and hospitalized in a hospital other than a tuberculosis sanatorium, subject to the reasonable rules of the hospital in which the person is hospitalized. [L 1949, c 307, §3; RL 1955, §49-74; HRS §325-81; gen ch 1985]

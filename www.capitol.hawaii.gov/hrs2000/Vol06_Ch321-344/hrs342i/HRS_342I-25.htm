[§342I-25] Motor vehicle tire wholesalers. Any person selling new motor vehicle tires at wholesale shall accept at the point of transfer, in a quantity at least equal to the number of new tires purchased by a customer, used motor vehicle tires offered by the customer. A person accepting tires in transfer from a motor vehicle tire retailer shall be allowed a period not to exceed ninety days to remove tires from the retail point of collection.

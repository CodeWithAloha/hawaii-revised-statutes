§342G-88 Penalties. Any person who violates any provision of this part, or any rule adopted thereunder, shall be fined not more than $10,000 for each separate offense. Each day of each violation shall constitute a separate offense. Any action taken to impose or collect the penalty provided for in this section shall be considered an administrative action. [L 1994, c 201, pt of §2; am L 2000, c 128, §4]

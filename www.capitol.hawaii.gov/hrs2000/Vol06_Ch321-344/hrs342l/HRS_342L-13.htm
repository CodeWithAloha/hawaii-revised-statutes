§342L-13 Appeal. If any party is aggrieved by an order of the director, the party may appeal in the manner provided in chapter 91 to the circuit court of the circuit in which the party resides or has the party's principal place of business or in which the action in question occurred; provided that the operation of an order shall not be stayed on appeal unless specifically ordered by a court of competent jurisdiction. [L 1989, c 212, pt of §6; am L 1992, c 259, §15]

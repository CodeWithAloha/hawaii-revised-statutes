§37D-4 Inclusion in budget request. For each fiscal period, there shall be included in the executive and judiciary budget requests to the legislature, amounts sufficient to permit the payment of all amounts that will be due under financing agreements during that fiscal period, including any expenses and replenishment of any reserve funds up to the balances required by the respective financing agreements. [L 1996, c 119, pt of §2; am L 1998, c 41, §8 and c 263, §2]

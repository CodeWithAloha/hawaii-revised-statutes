§10-13.5 Use of public land trust proceeds. [Validity of 1990 amendment and retroactivity to June 16, 1980. L 1990, c 304, §§16, 18.] Twenty per cent of all revenue derived from the public land trust shall be expended by the office for the betterment of the conditions of native Hawaiians. [L 1980, c 273, §1; am L 1990, c 304, §7]

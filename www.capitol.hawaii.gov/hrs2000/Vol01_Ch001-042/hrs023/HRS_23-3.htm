§23-3 Salary of the auditor and appropriations. The salary of the auditor shall be fixed by the legislature and shall not be diminished during the auditor's term of office. Effective January 1, 1989, and January 1, 1990, the salary of the auditor shall be $81,629 and $85,302 a year, respectively.

The funds for the support of the auditor's office shall be provided for in the act providing for the expenses of the legislature.

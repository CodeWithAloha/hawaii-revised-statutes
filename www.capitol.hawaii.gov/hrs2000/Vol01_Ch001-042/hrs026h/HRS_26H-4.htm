§26H-4 Repeal dates for newly enacted professional and vocational regulatory programs. (a) Any professional or vocational regulatory program enacted after January 1, 1994, and listed in this section shall be repealed on the date indicated in subsection (b). The auditor shall perform an evaluation of the program, pursuant to section 26H-5, prior to its repeal date.

(b) (1) Chapter 451J (marriage and family therapists) shall be repealed on December 31, 2002; and

(2) Chapter 457G (occupational therapy practice) shall be repealed on December 31, 2003.

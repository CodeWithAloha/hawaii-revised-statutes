[§13-1.1] Reapportionment. Upon the implementation of a new apportionment plan, the chief election officer, by proclamation issued no later than the tenth day prior to the close of filing in elections, shall designate the representative districts that comprise the departmental school districts and the school board districts described in section 13-1 to comply with the new districting scheme of such plan; provided that the departmental school districts designated shall cover areas similar to those described in section 13-1. [L 1984, c 63, §1]

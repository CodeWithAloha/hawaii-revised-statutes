PART VI. [MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS]

§425-191 Merger of domestic general and limited liability partnerships. [Section effective July 1, 2000. For section effective until June 30, 2000, see above.] One [or] more domestic general and limited liability partnerships may be merged with a domestic limited liability company pursuant to sections 428-904 to 428-906. [L 1996, c 92, §3; am L 1999, c 280, §18]

LIMITED LIABILITY PARTNERSHIPS

[§425-151] Name. The name of a limited liability partnership shall end with "Registered Limited Liability Partnership", "Limited Liability Partnership", "R.L.L.P.", "L.L.P.", "RLLP", or "LLP". [L 2000, c 218, pt of §1]

§412:11-103 Use of federal examinations. The commissioner may accept, adopt, or use in lieu of an examination prescribed by section 412:11-102 or otherwise, all or any part of the results of an examination conducted by an appropriate federal regulatory agency of a financial institution or a financial institution holding company for the same period or subject matter that would be covered by an examination required or permitted under this article. [L 1993, c 350, pt of §1]

§412:13-222 Relocation of office; written application necessary. (a) No foreign bank that is licensed to establish and maintain a Hawaii state branch, Hawaii state agency, or Hawaii representative office shall relocate any Hawaii office unless the foreign bank files a written application with the commissioner and the commissioner approves the relocation.

(b) The written application filed by a foreign bank under this section shall be in a form and contain any information as the commissioner shall require and shall be accompanied by a fee, the amount of which shall be established by rule. [L 1996, c 155, pt of §2; am L 1999, c 245, §6]

§412:10-101 Necessity for credit union charter. Except as expressly permitted by federal law or this chapter or section 415-106(c), no person shall engage in the business of a credit union, represent itself as a credit union, use a name or title containing the phrase "credit union" or any derivation thereof, or control any other person engaging in the business of a credit union. [L 1993, c 350, pt of §1]

§412:7-406 Meetings. A mutual savings and loan association shall hold meetings of its members when and as required by its bylaws, or by any law applicable to financial institutions. A quorum for the conduct of business at any regular or special meeting of its members shall be the presence of at least two members, and when not inconsistent with law, a majority of all votes cast at any duly constituted meeting shall determine any question. [L 1993, c 350, pt of §1]

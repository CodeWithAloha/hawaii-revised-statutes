§420-17 Limitation on effect of chapter. If a corporation organized pursuant to this chapter fails to begin business within two years from the effective date of its articles, then the articles shall become void. [L 1957, c 288, §17; am L 1961, c 44, §1; am L 1964, c 41, §8; Supp, §176A-17; am L 1967, c 289, §3; HRS §420-17; am L 1996, c 26, §2]

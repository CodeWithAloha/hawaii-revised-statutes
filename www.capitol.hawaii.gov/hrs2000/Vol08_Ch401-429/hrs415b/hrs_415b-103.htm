§415B-103 Discontinuance of liquidation proceedings. The liquidation of the assets and affairs of a corporation may be discontinued at any time during the liquidation proceedings when it is established that cause for liquidation no longer exists. In that event the court shall dismiss the proceedings and direct the receiver to redeliver to the corporation all of its remaining property and assets. [L 1985, c 270, pt of §1; am L 1987, c 135, §177]

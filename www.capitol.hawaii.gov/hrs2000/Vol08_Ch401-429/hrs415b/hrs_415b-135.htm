§415B-135 Certificate of withdrawal. After the filing of the application for withdrawal, the director shall issue a certificate of withdrawal which shall be effective as of the date of the filing of the application of withdrawal, and the authority of the foreign corporation to conduct affairs in this State shall cease. [L 1985, c 270, pt of §1; am L 1987, c 135, §195; am L 1995, c 198, §8]

PART VII. ENFORCEMENT, FEES, AND PENALTIES

§415B-151 Powers of director. The director shall have the power and authority reasonably necessary to administer this chapter efficiently, to perform the duties imposed upon the director by this chapter, and to adopt, amend, and repeal rules under this chapter subject to chapter 91. [L 1985, c 270, pt of §1; am L 1987, c 135, §198]

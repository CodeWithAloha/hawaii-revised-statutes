§415B-157 Penalties imposed upon corporation. Any domestic or foreign corporation that fails or refuses to file its annual report for any year within the time prescribed by this chapter shall be subject to a penalty of $25 to be assessed by the director. The director may waive the imposition of the penalty upon a showing of good cause.

Any domestic or foreign corporation that fails or refuses to truthfully and fully answer, within the time prescribed by this chapter, interrogatories propounded by the director pursuant to this chapter shall be subject to a class C felony. [L 1985, c 270, pt of §1; am L 1987, c 135, §203; am L 1988, c 141, §46]

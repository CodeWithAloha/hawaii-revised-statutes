§415B-128 Powers of foreign corporation. A foreign corporation which has received a certificate of authority under this chapter and until a certificate of revocation or certificate of withdrawal has been issued pursuant to this chapter, shall enjoy the same, but no greater, rights and privileges as a domestic corporation organized for the purposes set forth in the application pursuant to which the certificate of authority is issued. Except as otherwise provided by this chapter, a foreign corporation shall be subject to the same duties, restrictions, penalties, and liabilities now or hereafter imposed upon a domestic corporation of like character. [L 1985, c 270, pt of §1; am L 1987, c 135, §188]

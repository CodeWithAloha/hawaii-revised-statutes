[§481L-3] Trade-in vehicle. A trade-in vehicle used to reduce the gross capitalized cost shall be identified as a trade- in vehicle in the lease agreement and identified by year, make, and model. The lease agreement shall also state, in an amount distinct from the capitalized cost reduction, the total credit value of the trade-in vehicle, including a good-faith estimate of any liability against the trade-in vehicle to be discharged by the retail lessor.

§490:9-409 Liability of filing officers. No officer or employee of the department of land and natural resources, including the registrar of conveyances and any deputy registrar, and no officer or employee of the land court, including the registrar of the land court and any assistant registrar, shall be subject to personal liability by reason of any error or omission in the performance of any duty under this Article 9 of the Uniform Commercial Code except in case of wilful negligence. [L 1967, c 77, §1; HRS §490:9-409]

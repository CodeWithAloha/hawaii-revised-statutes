§490:9-302 Law governing perfection and priority of agricultural liens. [Section effective July 1, 2001. For section effective until June 30, 2001, see the main volume and 1999 Supplement of the Hawaii Revised Statutes.] While farm products are located in a jurisdiction, the local law of that jurisdiction governs perfection, the effect of perfection or nonperfection, and the priority of an agricultural lien on the farm products. [L 2000, c 241, pt of §1]

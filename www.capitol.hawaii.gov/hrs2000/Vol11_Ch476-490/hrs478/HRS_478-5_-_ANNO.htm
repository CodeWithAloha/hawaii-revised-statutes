Case Notes

Recovery of principal may be had where principal and usurious interest are separable even though they are lumped together in note sued on; payments made on account of such note should be applied on principal. 11 H. 747. Usury voluntarily paid not recoverable. 36 H. 107; 37 H. 295.

Statute applied and payments made under usurious contract applied on principal. 34 H. 639, modified 34 H. 685.

This section docks interest in action brought by lender, but does not render contract entirely void as to interest. 49 H. 160, 183-86, 413 P.2d 221, reh'g den. 49 H. 255, 413 P.2d 221.

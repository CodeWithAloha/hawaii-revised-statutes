[§486H-4] Exceptions. No action may be brought under section 486H-2 in connection with the termination, cancellation, or nonrenewal of a franchise if the franchise agreement provides for the binding arbitration of disputes arising under the agreement, including disputes related to the termination, cancellation, or nonrenewal of the franchise, in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association. [L 1975, c 133, pt of §1]

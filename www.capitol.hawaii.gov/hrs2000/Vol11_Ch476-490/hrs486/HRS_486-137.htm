[§486-137] Suspension and revocation of license. The board is authorized to suspend or revoke the license of any public measuremaster:

(1) When it is satisfied, after a hearing pursuant to chapter 91, that the licensee has violated any provision of this part or of any valid rule of the board affecting public measuremasters; or
  1. In accordance with section 831-3.1. [L 1991, c 153, pt of §7]

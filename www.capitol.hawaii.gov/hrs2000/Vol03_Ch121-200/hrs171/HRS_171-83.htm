§171-83 Costs of, and realization from, residential leasing. The board of land and natural resources is authorized to expend from the special land and development fund sufficient moneys to meet all costs of the planning, development, and subdivision of public lands for residential leasing, the sale of residential leases and otherwise to effectuate the purposes of this part, and all realizations from residential leases and the selling of any improvements purchased from lessees shall be paid into the fund. [L 1962, c 32, pt of §2; Supp, §103A-79; HRS §171-83]

§132D-4 Permissible uses of special fireworks and aerial common fireworks. Special fireworks and aerial common fireworks may be purchased, set off, ignited, or otherwise caused to explode in the State only if for public display and permitted in writing pursuant to sections 132D-10 and 132D-16. [L 1994, c 180, pt of §1; am L 2000, c 233, §6]

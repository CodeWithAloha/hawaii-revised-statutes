§142-10 Importing prohibited, when. The governor, notwithstanding anything in this chapter, may, from time to time by proclamation declaring any port or country to be infected, absolutely prohibit the introduction of any animals therefrom until the restriction is removed. [L 1882, c 34, §6; RL 1925, §616; RL 1935, §211; RL 1945, §1061; RL 1955, §20-11; HRS §142-10]

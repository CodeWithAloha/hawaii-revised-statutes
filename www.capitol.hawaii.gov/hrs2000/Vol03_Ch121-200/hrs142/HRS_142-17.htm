§142-17 Branding of tuberculous cattle. All cattle found to be tuberculous either upon physical examination or by means of the tuberculin test shall be branded upon the left cheek with the registered brand "T." [L 1923, c 124, §2; RL 1925, §627; am L Sp 1932 2d, c 39, §2; RL 1935, §223; RL 1945, §1077; RL 1955, §20-18; HRS §142-17]

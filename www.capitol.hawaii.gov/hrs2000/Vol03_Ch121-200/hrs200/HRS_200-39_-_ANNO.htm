Note

L 1998, c 129, §6 as amended by L 2000, c 110, §2 provides:

"SECTION 6. Notwithstanding any law to the contrary, the department of land and natural resources shall not implement any provision relating to the locations of the commercial operation of ocean use activities in Kaneohe Bay recommended in the Kaneohe Bay master plan developed pursuant to Act 208, Session Laws of Hawaii 1990, until the department adopts rules relating to these activities pursuant to chapter 91, Hawaii Revised Statutes, and in accordance with section 200-39(e), Hawaii Revised Statutes."

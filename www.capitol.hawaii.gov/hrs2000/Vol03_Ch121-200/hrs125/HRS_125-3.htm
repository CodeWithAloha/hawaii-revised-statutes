§125-3 Charges. Whenever under this chapter any transportation or services are furnished or commodities sold by the State, such charges shall be made and collected therefor as will compensate the State for its expenditures in connection therewith. Any moneys realized are reappropriated for the purposes of this chapter. [L Sp 1949, c 21, §3; RL 1955, §357-3; HRS §125-3]

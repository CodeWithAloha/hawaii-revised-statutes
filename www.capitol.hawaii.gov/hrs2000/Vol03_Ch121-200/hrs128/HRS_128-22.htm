§128-22 Notice of requisition. The governor may requisition and take over any materials, facilities, real property or improvements, required for the purposes of this chapter, or requisition and take over the temporary use thereof. The requisition shall be made by serving notice thereof which notice may be served upon any person found in occupation of the premises or having the property in the person's custody, possession, or control; provided that a like notice shall also be served upon any person who has filed with the governor, or with such person as the governor may designate for the purpose, a request for notice with respect to the property; provided further that whenever all persons entitled to compensation for the property have not been served in the manner aforesaid, the governor shall publish a notice of the requisition at the earliest practicable date. [L 1951, c 268, pt of §2; RL 1955, pt of §359-21; HRS §128-22; gen ch 1985]

§188-68 Permits for coral and rock with marine life attached. [Repeal and reenactment on June 30, 2002. L 1999, c 85, §17.] The department may issue permits, as authorized by this section, section 187A-6, chapter 183C, or under rules adopted pursuant to chapter 91 necessary for collecting live stony corals or marine life visibly attached to rocks placed in the water for a commercial purpose.

§432D-23 Required provisions and benefits. [Section effective until June 30, 2001. For section effective on July 1, 2001, see below.] Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, each policy, contract, plan, or agreement issued in the State after January 1, 1995, by health maintenance organizations pursuant to this chapter, shall include benefits provided in sections 431:10-212, 431:10A-115, 431:10A-115.5, 431:10A-116, 431:10A-116.5, 431:10A-116.6, and 431:10A-119, and 431:10A-120 and chapter 431M. [L 1995, c 179, pt of §1; am L 1999, c 77, §8 and c 86, §4]

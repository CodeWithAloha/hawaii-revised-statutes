§431:9-234 Temporary licenses, duration and powers. (a) No temporary license shall be effective for more than six months, and the commissioner may refuse so to license again any person who has previously been so licensed. The commissioner may in the commissioner's discretion renew such license for any period not to exceed a total period of twelve months.

(b) Any temporary license is permitted to be issued only for a class of insurance for which the applicant's predecessor was licensed. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2]

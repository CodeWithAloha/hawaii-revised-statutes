ARTICLE 9

LICENSING OF AGENTS, BROKERS, SOLICITORS,

ADJUSTERS, AND BILL REVIEWERS

Note

Article heading amended by L 2000, c 288, §2.

PART I. GENERAL PROVISIONS

§431:9-101 Scope. This article shall govern the qualifications and procedures for granting licenses to all insurance agents, brokers, surplus lines brokers, nonresident agents or brokers, subagents, solicitors, adjusters, independent bill reviewers, and limited service representatives. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2; am L 2000, c 288, §6]

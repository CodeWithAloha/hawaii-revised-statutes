[PART VI.] WESTERN GOVERNORS UNIVERSITY

§304-111 Participation and membership in the Western Governors University and other educational consortia. The board of regents of the University of Hawaii may participate in and perform any act related to membership in an educational consortium involving other institutions or jurisdictions that advance the instruction, research, or service mission of the university, including the Western Governors University. [L 1997, c 228, pt of §2; am L 1998, c 115, §23]

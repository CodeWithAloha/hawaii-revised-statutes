[§304-7.9] Management of special and revolving funds of the University of Hawaii. In managing special and revolving funds under its control, the university board of regents shall ensure that:

(1) The purposes for which special and revolving funds are expended, encumbered, or transferred, benefit those upon whom fees and charges are imposed through these funds; and

(2) Fees and charges are fair and equitable with respect to the level and quality of services provided to those upon whom fees and charges are imposed. [L 1998, c 115, pt of §2]

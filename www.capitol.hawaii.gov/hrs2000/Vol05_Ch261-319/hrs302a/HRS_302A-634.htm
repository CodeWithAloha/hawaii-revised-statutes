[§302A-634] Noncertificated personnel. [Section repealed July 1, 2002. L 2000, c 253, §139.] (a) All noncertificated administrative, professional, and technical personnel not engaged in instructional work shall be placed by the department in the appropriate salary ranges within the educational officers' schedule.

(b) Beginning with the 1997-1998 school year, this section shall be interpreted as though the term "certificate" read "license" or "credential", as the latter terms are used in subpart D, and as circumstances require. [L 1996, c 89, pt of §2]

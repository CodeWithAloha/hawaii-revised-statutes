Note

L 1993, c 128, §5 provides:

"SECTION 5. This Act shall take effect upon its approval [May 21, 1993]; provided that section 2 [amending section 291-4(b)] shall be retroactive for all pending first-offense cases for driving under the influence of intoxicating liquor."

Application of 1999 amendment. L 1999, c 78, §4.

§291-52 Issuance of removable windshield placard. [Section effective until June 30, 2000. For section effective July 1, 2000, see below.] Each county may issue one distinguishing placard to each disabled person who so requests and presents a certificate of disability. The removable windshield placard shall be designed, fabricated, and sold at cost to the counties by the department of transportation. The county may charge a fee to cover its costs. [L 1984, c 46, pt of §2; am L 1992, c 46, §4; am L 1997, c 308, §9]

§291-52 Issuance of removable windshield placard. [Section effective July 1, 2000. For section effective until June 30, 2000, see above.] Each county may issue one distinguishing placard to each disabled person who so requests and presents a certificate of disability. The removable windshield placard shall be designed, fabricated, and sold to the counties at a rate negotiated by the disability and communication access board. The county may charge a fee to cover its costs. [L 1984, c 46, pt of §2; am L 1992, c 46, §4; am L 1997, c 308, §9; am L 1999, c 282, §6]

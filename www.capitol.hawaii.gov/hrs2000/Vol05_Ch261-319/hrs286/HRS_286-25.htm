§286-25 Operation of a vehicle without a certificate of inspection. Whoever operates, permits the operation of, causes to be operated, or parks any vehicle on a public highway without a current official certificate of inspection, issued under section 286-26, shall be fined not more than $100. [L 1967, c 214, pt of §2; HRS §286-25; am L 1972, c 18, §1; am L 1974, c 106, §1; am L 1993, c 214, §3]

§286-252 Notice of administrative revocation; effect. As used in this part, the notice of administrative revocation:

(1) Establishes that the arrestee's driving privilege in this State shall be terminated thirty days after the date of arrest or a later date as is established by the director under section 286-259, if the director administratively revokes the arrestee's license;

(2) Establishes that the registrations of all motor vehicles registered to an arrestee who is a repeat intoxicated driver shall be terminated thirty days after the date of an arrest pursuant to section 286-255(b);

(3) Establishes the date on which administrative revocation proceedings against the arrestee were initiated; and

(4) Serves as a temporary driver's permit and temporary motor vehicle registration as provided in section 286-255. [L 1990, c 188, pt of §3; am L Sp 1991, c 1, §4; am L 2000, c 189, §8]

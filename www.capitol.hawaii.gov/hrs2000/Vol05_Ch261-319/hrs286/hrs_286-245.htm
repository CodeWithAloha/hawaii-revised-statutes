§286-245 Driving record information to be furnished. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the state judiciary or the city and county of Honolulu shall furnish full information regarding the driving record of any person:

(1) To the driver's license administrator of any other state, Mexico, or province or territory of Canada, requesting that information; and

(2) To the person's employer or prospective employer. [L 1989, c 320, pt of §2; am L 1990, c 342, §15; am L 1993, c 268, §8; am L 1995, c 114, §8]

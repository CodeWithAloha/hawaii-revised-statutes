§281-62 Reduction or increase in area of licensed premises. The liquor commission may, in its discretion, permit the reduction or the increase in the area of the licensed premises of any licensee. Whenever any reduction or increase is permitted, the same shall be endorsed in some appropriate manner upon the license. [L 1937, c 211, §17; am L 1939, c 205, pt of §1; RL 1945, §7258; RL 1955, §159-61; HRS §281-62]

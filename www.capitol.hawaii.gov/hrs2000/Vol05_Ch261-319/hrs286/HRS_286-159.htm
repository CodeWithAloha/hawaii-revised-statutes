§286-159 Proof of refusal; admissibility. If a legally arrested person refuses to submit to a test of the person's breath or blood, evidence of refusal shall be admissible only in a hearing under part XIV of this chapter and shall not be admissible in any other action or proceeding, whether civil or criminal. [L 1967, c 214, pt of §2; HRS §286-159; am L 1973, c 135, §4; gen ch 1985; am L 1990, c 188, §6; am L Sp 1991, c 1, §18]

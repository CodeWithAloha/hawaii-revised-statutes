§245-15 Disposition of revenues. [Repeal and reenactment on June 30, 2006. L 2000, c 249, §20(2).] All moneys collected pursuant to this chapter shall be paid into the state treasury as state realizations to be kept and accounted for as provided by law, except for the amounts designated by section 245-41 for distribution to the attorney general. [L 1939, c 220, §16; RL 1945, §5765; RL 1955, §125-15; HRS §245-15; am L 2000, c 249, §10]

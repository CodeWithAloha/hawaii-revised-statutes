1. Housing for Elders

Note

Subpart heading amended by L 1998, c 212, §12.

§201G-151 Definitions. The following terms, wherever used or referred to in this subpart, shall have the following respective meanings, unless a different meaning clearly appears from the context:

"Elder" means a person who is a resident of the State and who has attained the age of sixty-two. [L 1997, c 350, pt of §2; am L 1998, c 212, §27]

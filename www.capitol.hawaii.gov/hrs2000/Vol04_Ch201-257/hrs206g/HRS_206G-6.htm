§206G-6 District established; boundaries. The Kalaeloa community development district is established and shall include all lands within tax map key numbers 9-1-31:28, 9-1-13:09, and 9-1-13:01, excluding those areas to be retained by the federal government. [L 1997, c 359, pt of §2; am L 1998, c 13, §3]

[§201-15] Hawaii film facility special fund. There is established in the state treasury a fund to be known as the Hawaii film facility special fund, into which shall be deposited all moneys and fees from tenants or other users of the department's film facility, and appropriations from the legislature. All moneys in the fund are hereby appropriated for the purposes of and shall be expended by the department for the operation, maintenance, and management of its film facility. [L 1991, c 156, §2]

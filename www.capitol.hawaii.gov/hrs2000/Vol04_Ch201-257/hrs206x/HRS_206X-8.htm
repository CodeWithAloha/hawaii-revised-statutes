§206X-8 Use of public lands. Any provision of chapter 171 to the contrary notwithstanding, the governor may set aside, transfer, convey, or lease public lands wherever situated and whether or not set aside to any department or agency of the State or any political subdivision of the State, to the authority for its use. [L 1988, c 96, pt of §1; am L Sp 1993, c 7, §6]

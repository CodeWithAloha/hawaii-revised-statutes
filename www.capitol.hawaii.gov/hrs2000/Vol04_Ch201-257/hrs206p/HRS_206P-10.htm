[§206P-10] Charges and term for services. (a) Access and information use fees and other charges by the corporation shall be adopted in accordance with chapter 91.

(b) Information and access fee and charges for public information provided through the information network shall be established by the corporation by agreement with the department of budget and finance and shall be adopted in accordance with chapter 91.

(c) The term for which services are to be rendered shall be by agreement between the corporation and the responsible agency or other data base provider. [L Sp 1988, c 1, pt of §2]

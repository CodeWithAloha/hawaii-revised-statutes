§205A-32 Penalties. (a) Any person who violates any provision of part II or part III shall be liable for a civil fine not to exceed $10,000.

(b) In addition to any other penalties, any person who is violating any provision of part II or part III shall be liable for a civil fine not to exceed $1,000 a day for each day in which such violation persists.

(c) Any civil fine provided under this section may be imposed by the circuit court or may be imposed by the department after an opportunity for a hearing under chapter 91. Imposition of a civil fine shall not be a prerequisite to any civil fine or other injunctive relief ordered by the circuit court. [L 1975, c 176, pt of §1; am L 1989, c 356, §9]

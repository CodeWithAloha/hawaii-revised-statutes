Case Notes

In robbery, the terms "from the person" and "from the custody and in the presence" are synonymous and may be alleged in alternative in an indictment. 26 H. 725.

Indictment alleging four alternative non-synonymous subjects for inquiry is defective for uncertainty. 37 H. 552.

Indictment alleging several acts in the alternative not duplicitous. 39 H. 574.

Cited: 27 H. 65, 102; 36 H. 550, 552, aff'd 150 F.2d 545.

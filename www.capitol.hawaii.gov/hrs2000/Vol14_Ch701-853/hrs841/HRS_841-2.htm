§841-2 Appointment of deputies. The coroner may appoint as many deputy coroners as the coroner may deem necessary to aid the coroner in the discharge of the coroner's duties as coroner from amongst the subordinates in the coroner's department. [L 1935, c 90, §2; RL 1945, §10902; RL 1955, §260-2; HRS §715-2; ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1; gen ch 1985]

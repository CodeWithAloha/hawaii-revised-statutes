§846-30 Identification certificates; form. The department of the attorney general, after taking the fingerprints of each registrant as provided in this part (except as otherwise provided in the case of children under three years of age), and after securing the information required by or pursuant to this part, shall issue to each registrant a certificate of identification in such form, and with such information, as the attorney general deems necessary and practicable. [L 1983, c 78, pt of §3(6); am L 1987, c 146, §4; am L 1988, c 183, §2; am L 1993, c 249, §5; am L 1999, c 15, §3]

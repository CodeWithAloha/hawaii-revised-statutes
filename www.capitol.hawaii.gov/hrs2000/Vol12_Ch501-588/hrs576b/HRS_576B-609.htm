PART III. REGISTRATION AND MODIFICATION

OF CHILD SUPPORT ORDER

[§576B-609] Procedure to register child support order of another state for modification. A party or support enforcement agency seeking to modify, or to modify and enforce, a child support order issued in another state shall register that order in this State in the same manner provided in part I if the order has not been registered. A petition for modification may be filed at the same time as a request for registration, or later. The pleading must specify the grounds for modification. [L 1997, c 295, pt of §1]

Rules of Court

Guardians ad litem, see HFCR rule 17(c).

Parties, see Hawaii Family Court Rules, part A(IV).

Case Notes

No requirement under subsection (a) that the child must be made a party to the paternity proceeding or that a guardian ad litem must be appointed to represent the child’s interests in a paternity proceeding. 88 H. 159 (App.), 963 P.2d 1135.

Cited: 9 H. App. 623, 859 P.2d 922.

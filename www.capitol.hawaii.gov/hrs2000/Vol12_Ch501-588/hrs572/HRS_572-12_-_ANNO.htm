Case Notes

In adultery, testimony of clergy who was authorized to solemnize and perform marriage is admissible. 10 H. 440. If record introduced, not necessary to prove that celebrant had authority to solemnize. 10 H. 442.

No ceremony necessary, but license is. 25 H. 397 reversing 16 H. 377.

Cited: 74 H. 530, 852 P.2d 44.

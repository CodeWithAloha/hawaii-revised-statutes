§514A-70 Warranty against structural and appliance defects; notice of expiration required. The developer of a condominium property regime subject to this chapter shall give notice by certified mail at the appropriate time to all members of the association of apartment owners and all members of the board of directors that the normal one-year warranty period will expire in ninety days. The notice shall set forth specific methods which apartment owners may pursue in seeking remedies for defects, if any, prior to expiration. [L 1977, c 98, pt of §2; am L 1989, c 261, §16]

§560:5-403 Venue. Venue for proceedings under this Part is:

(1) In the place in this State where the person to be protected resides whether or not a guardian of the person has been appointed in another place; or

(2) If the person to be protected does not reside in this State, in any place where the person has property. [L 1976, c 200, pt of §1; gen ch 1985]

[§534A-3] Requirement of survival by one hundred twenty hours under governing instruments. Except as provided in section 534A-6, for purposes of a provision of a governing instrument that relates to an individual surviving an event, including the death of another individual, an individual who is not established by clear and convincing evidence to have survived the event by one hundred twenty hours is deemed to have predeceased the event. [L 1993, c 122, pt of §1]

§517-3 Effect on existing and subsequent trusts. The legislature declares that if this chapter is held invalid or unconstitutional with respect to trusts and estates existing prior to April 24, 1957, it would have enacted this chapter as applicable to estates and trusts arising subsequent to April 24, 1957. [L 1957, c 13, pt of §4; Supp, §345A-4; HRS §517-3]

[§329-73]  Pseudoephedrine permit.  (a)  Beginning January 1, 2006, any person transporting by any means more than three packages of any product the sale of which is restricted by section 329-75 shall obtain a pseudoephedrine permit.

     (b)  The requirements imposed by [subsection] (a) shall not apply to persons registered with the department under section 329-67.  A pseudoephedrine permit shall be issued by the department in a form and manner as prescribed by the department by rule.  A pseudoephedrine permit shall be valid for one year and renewable annually. [L 2005, c 193, pt of §1]

[§327-13]  Delivery of document of gift not required; right to examine.  (a)  A document of gift need not be delivered during the donor's lifetime to be effective.

     (b)  Upon or after an individual's death, a person in possession of a document of gift or a refusal to make an anatomical gift with respect to the individual shall allow examination and copying of the document of gift or refusal by a person authorized to make or object to the making of an anatomical gift with respect to the individual or by a person to which the gift could pass under section 327-11. [L 2008, c 122, pt of §1]

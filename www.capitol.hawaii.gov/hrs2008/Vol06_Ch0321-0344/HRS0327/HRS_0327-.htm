Revision Note

 

  To incorporate the 2008 revised version of the uniform anatomical gift act as part I of this chapter, repealed references for former parts I and II are omitted. The two former part I's were repealed by L 1988, c 267, §2 and L 2008, c 122, §4, respectively. The former part II was repealed by L 1969, c 81, §2. The current parts III and IV are renumbered as parts II and III, respectively.

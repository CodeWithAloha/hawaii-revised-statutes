[§328C-6]  Donated drugs repository.  [Section repealed July 1, 2010.  L 2004, c 190, §11.]  (a)  Prescription drugs that meet the requirements of section 461-11.5, but are otherwise not appropriate for return-for-credit-and-reuse as defined in section 328B-2, may be donated to repository entities.  Prescription drugs from individual members of the public shall not be accepted for donation.

     (b)  Donations to repository entities shall be made without credit to the original payer for the cost of the drugs.

     (c)  Prescription drugs donated to a repository shall have the patient's name removed or redacted from the product, including the label.

     (d)  Drugs donated under this section that are not used or accepted by a repository in the State of Hawaii may be distributed to repositories for use outside of the State. [L 2004, c 190, pt of §3]

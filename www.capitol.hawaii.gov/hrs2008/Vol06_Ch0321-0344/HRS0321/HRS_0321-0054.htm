§321-54  Application of part.  The services provided by this part shall be for the benefit of and apply only to persons not over twenty-one years of age, who or whose estate, or whose parents or guardians are without sufficient means to pay for the services.  The department of health shall have the final administrative authority in determining the acceptance of cases for the care and services provided in this part.  [L 1939, c 179, §7; RL 1945, §2106; RL 1955, §46-45; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §19; HRS §321-54]

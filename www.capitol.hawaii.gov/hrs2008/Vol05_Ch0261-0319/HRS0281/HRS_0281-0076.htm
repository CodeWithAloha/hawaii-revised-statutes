§281-76  Tampering with samples; penalty.  Any person who tampers with any sample of liquor taken for analysis under this chapter shall be fined not more than $2,000 or imprisoned not more than one year, or both. [L Sp 1933, c 40, §42; RL 1935, §2611; RL 1945, §7264; RL 1955, §159-75; HRS §281-76; am L 1990, c 171, §23]

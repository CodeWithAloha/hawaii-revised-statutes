§128-21  Civil defense personnel's oath.  To comply with the Federal Civil Defense Act of 1950 each person appointed to serve in an organization for civil defense who is so required by the Federal Civil Defense Act of 1950, before entering upon the person's duties, shall take such oath as may be required by federal law. [L 1951, c 268, pt of §2; RL 1955, §359-20; HRS §128-21; gen ch 1985; am L 2006, c 48, §4]

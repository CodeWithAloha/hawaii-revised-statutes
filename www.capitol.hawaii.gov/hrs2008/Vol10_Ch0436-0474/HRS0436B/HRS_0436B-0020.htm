§436B-20  Suspended license.  No license shall be suspended by the licensing authority for a period exceeding five years.  A person whose license has been suspended may apply for reinstatement of the license to the extent authorized by law and upon complete compliance with any term or condition imposed by the order of suspension.  The application for reinstatement shall be accompanied by all applicable fees, including but not limited to reinstatement fees, any compliance resolution fund fees, and any recovery fund assessments. [L 1991, c 111, pt of §2; am L 1992, c 202, §17; am L 1993, c 109, §2]

[§576B-319]  Receipt and disbursement of payments.  A support enforcement agency or tribunal of this State shall disburse promptly any amounts received pursuant to a support order, as directed by the order.  The agency or tribunal shall furnish to a requesting party or tribunal of another state a certified statement by the custodian of the record of the amounts and dates of all payments received. [L 1997, c 295, pt of §1]

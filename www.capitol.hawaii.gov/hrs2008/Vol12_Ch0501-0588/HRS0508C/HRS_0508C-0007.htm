[§508C-7]  Notice.  (a)  A copy of an environmental covenant shall be provided by the person determined by the agency and in the manner required by the agency to:

     (1)  Each person that signed the covenant;

     (2)  Each person holding a recorded interest in the real property subject to the covenant;

     (3)  Each person in possession of the real property subject to the covenant;

     (4)  Each county or other subdivision of county government in which real property subject to the covenant is located; and

     (5)  Any other person the agency determines should receive notice.

     (b)  The validity of a covenant shall not be affected by failure to provide a copy of the covenant as required under this section. [L 2006, c 279, pt of §2]

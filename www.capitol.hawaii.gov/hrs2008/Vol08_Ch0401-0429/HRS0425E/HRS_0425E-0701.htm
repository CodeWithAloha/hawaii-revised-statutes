ARTICLE 7.  TRANSFERABLE INTERESTS AND RIGHTS

OF TRANSFEREES AND CREDITORS

 

     [§425E-701]  Partner's transferable interest.  The only interest of a partner which shall be transferable is the partner's transferable interest.  A transferable interest shall be personal property. [L 2003, c 210, pt of §1]

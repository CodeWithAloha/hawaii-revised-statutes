[§425-20]  Resignation of registered agent.  (a)  A registered agent may resign from the registered agent's appointment by signing and delivering to the director of commerce and consumer affairs for filing, a signed statement of resignation.  The statement may include a statement that the registered office is also discontinued.

     (b)  The registered agent shall mail one copy to the registered office (if not discontinued) and the other copy to the partnership at its principal office.

     (c)  The appointment of the agent shall be terminated, and the registered office discontinued if so provided, on the thirty-first day after the date on which the statement was filed. [L 2002, c 130, pt of §5]

[§88-325]  Employee contributions.  Each class H member shall contribute six per cent of the member's compensation to the annuity savings fund; provided that each sewer worker, water safety officer, and emergency medical technician who is a class H member shall contribute nine and seventy-five one-hundredths per cent of the member's compensation to the annuity savings fund for service in that capacity. [L 2004, c 179, pt of §1]

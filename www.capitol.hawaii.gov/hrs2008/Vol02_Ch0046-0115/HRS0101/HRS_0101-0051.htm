PART III.  SPECIAL PROCEEDINGS RELATING TO

PUBLIC PROPERTY

 

     §101-51  Definitions.  As used herein:  "Public property" means real property (1) owned by a political subdivision of the State or (2) owned by the State in its own right and not owned by the United States.  "Public property" shall not be deemed to include public lands, defined to be such by section 171-2. [L 1949, c 377, pt of §1; RL 1955, §8-50; HRS §101-51]

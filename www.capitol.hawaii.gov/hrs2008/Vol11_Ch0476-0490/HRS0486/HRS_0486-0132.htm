[§486-132]  Enforcement; rules.  The board is authorized to enforce the provisions of this part and shall adopt reasonable rules for the implementation and enforcement of this part.  These rules shall have the force and effect of law.  The board may adopt rules that include, but are not limited to, the licensing of measuremasters; determining the qualifications of the applicant for a license as a public measuremaster; annual license fees; the granting, renewal, or refusal of a license; period of license validity; measurement practices that must be followed, including the measurement or recording of tare; the required information to be submitted with or as part of a certificate; and the period of record keeping. [L 1991, c 153, pt of §7]

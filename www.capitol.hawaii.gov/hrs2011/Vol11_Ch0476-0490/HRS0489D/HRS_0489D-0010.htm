§489D-10  Application and license fees.  (a)  Each application shall be accompanied by:

     (1)  A nonrefundable application fee in the amount of $2,000 plus $300 for each additional location in the State, not to exceed an aggregate fee of $15,000; and

     (2)  An annual license fee of $2,000 plus $300 for each additional location in the State, not to exceed an aggregate fee of $15,000.

     (b)  The license fee shall be refunded if the application is denied. [L 2006, c 153, pt of §1; am L 2008, c 195, §6]

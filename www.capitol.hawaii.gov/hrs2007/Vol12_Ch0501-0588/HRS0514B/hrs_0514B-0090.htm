[§514B-90]  Refunds upon cancellation or termination.  Upon any cancellation under section 514B‑86 or 514B‑89, the purchaser shall be entitled to a prompt and full refund of all moneys paid, less any escrow cancellation fee and other costs associated with the purchase, up to a maximum of $250. [L 2005, c 93, pt of §4]

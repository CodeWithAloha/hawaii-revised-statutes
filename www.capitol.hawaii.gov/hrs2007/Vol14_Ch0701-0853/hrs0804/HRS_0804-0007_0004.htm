[§804-7.4]  General conditions of release on bail.  Any person released on bail, recognizance, supervised release or conditional release shall be released subject to the following conditions:

     (1)  The person shall not commit a federal, state or local offense during the period of release;

     (2)  The person shall appear for all court hearings unless notified by the person's attorney that the person's appearance is not required; and

     (3)  The person shall remain in the State of Hawaii unless approval is obtained from a court of competent jurisdiction to leave the jurisdiction of the court. [L 1987, c 139, §9(1); gen ch 1992]

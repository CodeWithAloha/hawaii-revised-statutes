§662-6  Pleadings, trial and appeal.  The Hawaii Rules of Civil Procedure and the District Court Rules of Civil Procedure as applicable shall be followed in any action under this chapter.  A certified copy of all pleadings shall be duly served on the attorney general.

     Sections 661-2 and 661-9 shall apply to actions under this chapter. [L 1957, c 312, pt of §1; Supp, §245A-6; HRS §662-6; am L 1972, c 164, §2(b); am L 1978, c 156, §3]

[§11-195.5]  Reporting deadline.  When any reporting deadline falls on a Saturday, Sunday, or holiday designated in section 8‑1, the reporting deadline shall be the next succeeding day that is not a Saturday, Sunday, or holiday. [L 2007, c 200, §1]

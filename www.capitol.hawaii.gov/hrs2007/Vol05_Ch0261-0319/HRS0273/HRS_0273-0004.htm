§273-4  Compensation to be made.  In the exercise of the powers mentioned in section 273-3, the corporation shall do as little damage as can be; and shall make full compensation in the manner provided in chapter 101, to all parties interested for all damage sustained by reason of the exercise of such power. [L 1878, c 29, §4; RL 1925, §880; RL 1935, §1786; RL 1945, §5044; am L 1951, c 12, §3; RL 1955, §106-4; HRS §273-4]

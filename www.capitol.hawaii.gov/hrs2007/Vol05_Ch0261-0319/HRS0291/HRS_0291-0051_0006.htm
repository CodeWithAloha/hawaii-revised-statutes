§291‑51.6  Issuance of temporary removable windshield placards.  Each county shall issue one temporary removable windshield placard and a second temporary removable windshield placard to each applicant who requests and presents a certificate of disability that verifies the duration of the applicant's disability in monthly increments, which shall not exceed six months, and upon payment of a fee to the issuing agency.  All fees collected by the counties for issuing temporary removable windshield placards shall be deposited into the state general fund.  The temporary removable windshield placard shall be designed, fabricated, and sold to the counties at a rate negotiated by the disability and communication access board.

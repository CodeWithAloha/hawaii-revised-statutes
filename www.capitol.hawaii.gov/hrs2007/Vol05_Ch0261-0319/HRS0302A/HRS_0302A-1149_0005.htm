[§302A-1149.5]  After-school plus program revolving fund.  (a)  There is established the after-school plus program revolving fund to be administered by the department.

     (b)  The after-school plus program revolving fund shall consist of:

     (1)  Fees collected by the department for administering and operating the after-school plus program, and the provision of program services;

     (2)  Legislative appropriations;

     (3)  All interest earned on the deposit or investment of moneys in the after-school plus program revolving fund; and

     (4)  Any other moneys made available to the after-school plus program revolving fund from other sources.

     (c)  The department may establish appropriate fees and other charges to be assessed to each participant for the cost of administering and operating the after-school plus program.  The revenues from those fees and charges shall be deposited into the revolving fund to be used to pay the costs of administering and operating the program. [L 2004, c 221, §2]

 

Note

 

  Non-school hour programs for children and youth, annual report by office of youth services.  L 2006, c 281.

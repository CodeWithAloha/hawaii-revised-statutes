§439-22  Penalty.  Any person who practices cosmetology, maintains a school or a beauty shop, or acts in any capacity wherein a license is required, without a license as provided in this chapter, shall be fined not more than $100, or imprisoned not more than ninety days, or both.  Each and every day of violation shall be a separate offense. [L 1949, c 397, pt of §1; RL 1955, §59-23; HRS §439-22; am L 1987, c 154, §22; am L 1989, c 153, §14]

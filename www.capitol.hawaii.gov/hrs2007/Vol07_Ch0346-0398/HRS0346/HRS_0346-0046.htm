[§346-46]  Certified nurse aides; training programs and recertification.  The director shall:

     (1)  Approve training programs for nurse aides and ensure that the training programs comply with all applicable federal and state requirements;

     (2)  Approve and arrange for the recertification process for nurse aides who work in medicare or medicaid certified nursing facilities; and

     (3)  Approve and arrange for the recertification process for nurse aides who work in state-licensed or state-certified health care settings. [L 2007, c 226, pt of §3]

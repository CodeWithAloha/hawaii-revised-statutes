[§305-4] Special fund. The provisions of section 304-8 notwithstanding there is hereby created a special fund to receive, disburse, and account for funds of special programs and activities of the community colleges, including but not limited to off-campus programs, summer session programs, overseas programs, evening sessions, study abroad, exchange programs, cultural enrichment programs, and consultative services which help make available the resources of the community colleges to the communities they serve. [L 1975, c 131, §2]

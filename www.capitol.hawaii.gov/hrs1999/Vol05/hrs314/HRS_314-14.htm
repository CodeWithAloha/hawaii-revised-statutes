[§314-14] Transfer of personnel. All officers and employees of the University of Hawaii, division of educational television, are hereby transferred with their respective functions and duties to the Hawaii public broadcasting authority and shall (except for clerical employees employed under the provisions of chapters 76 and 77) upon their transfer continue to perform their regular duties.

No officer or employee employed prior to July 1, 1973 and still so employed upon transfer of functions by this chapter, shall suffer any loss of salary, vacation, or sick leave or other employee benefits as a consequence of this action, provided that subsequent changes in status may be made pursuant to applicable personnel laws. [L 1973, c 70, pt of §4]

Rules of Court

Appeal to circuit court, see HRCP rule 72; appeal to supreme court, see Hawaii Rules of Appellate Procedure.

Case Notes

Award of concession under §261-7(a)(2) is not within purview of subsection (c) and no appeal lies. 47 H. 495, 393 P.2d 87.

License refers to license for an airport pursuant to §261-16(b). It does not refer to a license conferring the privilege of supplying goods, services, etc., under §261-7(a)(2). Id.

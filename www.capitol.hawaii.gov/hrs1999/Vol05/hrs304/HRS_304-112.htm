[§304-112] Western Governors University special fund. There is established in the treasury of the State a Western Governors University special fund into which shall be deposited all revenues derived from the State's participation in the Western Governors University, except University of Hawaii income from regular credit tuition and tuition-related course and fee charges to students. The fund shall be administered by the board of regents of the University of Hawaii and shall be used for the State's membership and participation in the Western Governors University. The special fund shall not be subject to sections 36-27 and 36-30 relating to special fund reimbursements to the state general fund. [L 1997, c 228, pt of §2]

Attorney General Opinions

Pursuant to this section and §89-9, board of regents may enter into a collective bargaining agreement providing for tuition exemption for faculty and staff members. Att. Gen. Op. 74-12.

Regents may exempt or waive tuition for the faculty and staff members as a "term of service." Att. Gen. Op. 74-12.

Case Notes

University policy of retirement at age 65, unless superior competence and services needed, violates equal protection. 56 H. 601, 546 P.2d 1005.

Denial of tenure upheld. 56 H. 680, 548 P.2d 253.

While the board may prescribe the terms of service, it may not prescribe a mandatory retirement age inconsistent with §78-3. 63 H. 366, 628 P.2d 1026.

Does not preclude the university from agreeing to submit tenure or promotion disputes to arbitration or preclude an arbitrator from granting promotion or tenure. 66 H. 214, 659 P.2d 720.

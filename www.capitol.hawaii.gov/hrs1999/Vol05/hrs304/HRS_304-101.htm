[PART V. BOOKSTORES]

[§304-101 Community college and Hilo campus bookstore revolving fund.] There is established a revolving fund for the community college and Hilo campus bookstores from which is paid the cost of goods or services rendered or furnished to the bookstores and which is replenished through charges made for goods and services or through transfers from other accounts or funds. [L 1971, c 206, §1]

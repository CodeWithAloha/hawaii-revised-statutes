§279E-4 Chairperson: function and term of office. The members of the MPO policy committee shall elect annually a chairperson on a rotating basis between the members of the state legislature and the legislative body of the appropriate county.

The chairperson shall place on the agenda for full hearing any issue, project, or subject matter relating to transportation which is requested by at least three members of the MPO policy committee. [L 1975, c 180, §4; am L 1985, c 194, §2]

PART VII. CONTROL OF VENDING

[§264-101] Vending from state highways prohibited. Any vehicle or structure parked or placed wholly or partly within the right-of-way of any state highway for the purpose of selling the vehicle or structure or of selling therefrom or therein any article, service, or thing, creates a hazardous condition or is a public nuisance and the department of transportation may remove or require the immediate removal of the vehicle or structure from the highway. [L 1980, c 44, pt of §1]

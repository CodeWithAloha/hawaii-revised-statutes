§266-22 Maintenance of fire boat. The cost of operating and maintaining the fire boat transferred to the city and county of Honolulu under Act 175 of the Session Laws of 1951 by and through the city and county of Honolulu shall be borne by the department of transportation. For this purpose, the department shall pay annually from its special fund to the city and county of Honolulu the annual cost. [L 1951, c 175, §3; RL 1955, §112-22; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §26; am L 1963, c 171, §1; HRS §266-22]

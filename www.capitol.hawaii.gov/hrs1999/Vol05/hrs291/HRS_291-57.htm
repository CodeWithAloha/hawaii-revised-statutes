[§291-57] Disabled parking; penalties. (a) A person using a parking space reserved for disabled persons without properly displaying a removable windshield placard, temporary removable windshield placard, or special license plate, in accordance with this part or any rule adopted thereunder, shall be guilty of a traffic infraction under chapter 291D and shall be fined not less than $150 nor more than $300.

(b) Any disabled person who refuses or fails to present an identification card issued under this chapter or the rules adopted thereunder to an enforcement officer upon request shall be guilty of a traffic infraction under chapter 291D and shall be fined not less than $150 nor more than $300.

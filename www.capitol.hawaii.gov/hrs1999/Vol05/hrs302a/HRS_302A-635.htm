[§302A-635] Educational assistants. All educational assistants employed in the department shall be employed under the provisions of chapter 76 and shall have their compensation fixed in accordance with chapter 77; provided that:

(1) The monthly rate of compensation so determined shall be multiplied by ten and then divided by twelve and the resulting amount shall be the employee's monthly salary payable over a twelve-month period;

(2) Weekly working hours for educational assistants shall be established in the same manner as working hours for teachers; and

(3) Educational assistants shall have the same vacation and sick leave allowances as teachers. [L 1996, c 89, pt of §2]

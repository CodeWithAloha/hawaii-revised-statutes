§302A-1154 Immunization upon entering school; tuberculosis clearance. (a) No child shall attend any school in the State unless the child presents to the appropriate school official certification from a licensed physician or advanced practice registered nurse stating that the child has received immunizations against communicable diseases as required by the department of health.

(b) No child shall be admitted to attend any school for the first time in the State unless the child presents to the appropriate school official certification from a licensed physician, advanced practice registered nurse, or other authorized personnel stating the child has received a tuberculin test or x-ray and is free from tuberculosis in a communicable form. [L 1996, c 89, pt of §2; am L 1998, c 60, §1]

[§302A-808] Penalty. Any person who engages in the profession of teaching in a public school without first being issued a license or credential shall be fined not more than $500. Any person who knowingly or intentionally violates this subpart by employing an individual as a public school teacher who does not possess a valid license or credential may be fined not more than $500. All fines shall be deposited into the Hawaii teacher standards board revolving fund. [L 1996, c 89, pt of §2]

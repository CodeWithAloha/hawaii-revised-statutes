[§302A-625] Educational officers' salary schedules. (a) The salary schedule for all educational officers of the department shall be negotiated pursuant to section 89-9.

(b) Any principal, vice-principal, or other educational officer on a ten-month work year must earn at least six credits within a three-year cycle in order to receive an increment or longevity step increase in the third year of the three-year cycle. [L 1996, c 89, pt of §2]

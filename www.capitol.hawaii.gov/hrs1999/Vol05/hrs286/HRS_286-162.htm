§286-162 Test results to be collected. The results of any test for alcohol content made upon any person, including any person who has been fatally injured in a traffic accident or upon the driver of a motor vehicle or moped involved in an accident which resulted in another person's death, shall be sent to the state director of transportation who shall compile the data without revealing the identity of any individual tested. This data shall be available only to the state and county highway safety councils and to other agencies the director of transportation deems necessary and advisable. [L 1967, c 214, pt of §2; HRS §286-162; am L Sp 1977 1st, c 20, §12; gen ch 1985; am L 1987, c 33, §5]

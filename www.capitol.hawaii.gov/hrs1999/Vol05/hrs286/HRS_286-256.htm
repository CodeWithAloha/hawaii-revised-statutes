§286-256 Immediate restoration of license. If a test conducted in accordance with part VII and section 321-161 and the rules adopted thereunder shows that the arrestee's alcohol concentration was less than .08, the director or the arresting agency shall immediately return the arrestee's license along with a certified statement that administrative revocation proceedings have been terminated with prejudice. [L 1990, c 188, pt of §3; am L Sp 1991, c 1, §8; am L 1995, c 226, §3]

[§286-262] Notice to other states. When a nonresident's driving privilege is administratively revoked under this part, the director shall notify, in writing, the officials in charge of traffic control or public safety in the nonresident's home state and in any other state in which the nonresident has driving privileges of the action taken in this State and shall return to the appropriate issuing authority in the other states any license seized under section 286-255. [L 1990, c 188, pt of §3; am L Sp 1991, c 1, §18]

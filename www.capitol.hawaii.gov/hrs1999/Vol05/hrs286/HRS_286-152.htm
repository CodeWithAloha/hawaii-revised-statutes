§286-152 Persons qualified to take blood specimen. No person, other than a physician, registered nurse, phlebotomist deemed qualified by the director of a clinical laboratory that is licensed by the State, or person licensed in a clinical laboratory occupation under section 321-13, may withdraw blood for the purpose of determining the alcohol concentration or drug content therein. This limitation shall not apply to the taking of a breath or urine specimen. [L 1967, c 214, pt of §2; HRS §286-152; am L 1986, c 196, §2; am L 1996, c 105, §1; am L 1997, c 103, §4]

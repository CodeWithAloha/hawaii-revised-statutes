§286-266 Computation of time. The time in which any act provided in this part is to be done is computed by excluding the first day and including the last, unless the last day is a Saturday, Sunday, or holiday, and then it is also excluded. [L 1990, c 188, pt of §3; am L Sp 1991, c 1, §18; am L 1996, c 230, §6]

PART III. OFFICERS AND DIRECTORS

§415B-61 Board of directors. The affairs of a corporation shall be managed by a board of directors. The directors need not be residents of this State or members of the corporation unless required by the articles of incorporation or the bylaws. The articles of incorporation or the bylaws may prescribe other qualifications for directors. [L 1985, c 270, pt of §1; am L 1999, c 249, §19]

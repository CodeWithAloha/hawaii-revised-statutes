§425D-1107 Fees for filing documents and issuing certificates. [Section effective until June 30, 2000. For section effective July 1, 2000, see below.] The following fees shall be paid to the director upon the filing of limited partnership documents:

(1) Certificate of limited partnership, $50;

(2) Any certificate of amendment, restatement, or correction, $20;

(3) Certificate of cancellation, $20;

(4) Annual statement for domestic or foreign limited partnership, $10;

(5) Any other certificate or document of domestic or foreign limited partnership, $20;

(6) Application for registration as a foreign limited partnership, $100;

(7) Any certificate of amendment or agent change for foreign limited partnership, $20;

(8) Application for certificate of withdrawal of foreign limited partnership, $20;

(9) Reservation of name, $20;

    (10) Transfer of reservation of name, $20;

    (11) Good standing certificate, $20;

    (12) Special handling fee for review of any limited partnership document, $50;

    (13) Special handling fee for certificates issued by the director, $20 per certificate; and

    (14) Special handling fee for certification of documents, $1 per page.

All fees collected under this section shall be managed in accordance with section 26-9(l). [L 1989, c 288, pt of §1; am L 1995, c 198, §11; am L 1996, c 181, §5; am L 1999, c 129, §15]

 

§425D-1107 Fees for filing documents and issuing certificates. [Section effective July 1, 2000. For section effective until June 30, 2000, see above.] (a) The following fees shall be paid to the director upon the filing of limited partnership documents:

(1) Certificate of limited partnership, $50;

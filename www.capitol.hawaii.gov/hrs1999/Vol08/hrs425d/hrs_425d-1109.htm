[§425D-1109] Merger or conversion of domestic limited partnerships. [Section effective until June 30, 2000. For section effective July 1, 2000, see below.] One or more domestic limited partnerships may be converted into or merged with a domestic limited liability company pursuant to section 428-902 or sections 428-904 to 428-906, as the case may be. [L 1996, c 92, §4]

 

§425D-1109 Merger of domestic limited partnerships. [Section effective July 1, 2000. For section effective until June 30, 2000, see above.] One or more domestic limited partnerships may be merged with a domestic limited liability company pursuant to sections 428-904 to 428-906. [L 1996, c 92, §4; am L 1999, c 280, §20]

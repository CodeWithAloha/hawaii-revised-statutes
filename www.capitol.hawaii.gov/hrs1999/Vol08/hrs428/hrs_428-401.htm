PART IV. RELATIONS OF MEMBERS TO ONE ANOTHER AND TO THE

LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANY

[§428-401] Form of contribution. The contribution of a member of a limited liability company may consist of tangible or intangible property or other benefit to the company, including money, promissory notes, services performed, or other obligations to contribute cash or property, or contracts for services to be performed. [L 1996, c 92, pt of §1]

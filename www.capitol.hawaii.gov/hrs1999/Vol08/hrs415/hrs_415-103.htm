§415-103 Filing of decree of dissolution. In case the court shall enter a decree dissolving a corporation, it shall be the duty of the clerk of the court to cause a certified copy of the decree to be delivered to the director for filing. No fee shall be charged by the director for the filing thereof. [L 1983, c 167, pt of §1; am L 1985, c 270, §4; am L 1987, c 135, §72]

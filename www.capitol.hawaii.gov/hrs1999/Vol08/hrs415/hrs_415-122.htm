§415-122 Issuance of certificate of revocation. Upon revoking any certificate of authority, the director shall issue a certificate of revocation that shall be filed in the director's office, and the authority of the foreign corporation to transact business in this State shall cease. [L 1983, c 167, pt of §1; am L 1985, c 270, §4; gen ch 1985; am L 1987, c 135, §88]

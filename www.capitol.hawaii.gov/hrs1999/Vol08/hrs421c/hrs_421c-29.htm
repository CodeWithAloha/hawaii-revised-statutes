[§421C-29] Annual report. Every association, within one hundred twenty days of the close of its fiscal year, shall file with the director of commerce and consumer affairs an annual report as prescribed by the director. A copy of the report shall be submitted to the members at their annual meeting, mailed to each member of the association, or printed in an official publication of the association. [L 1982, c 97, pt of §2 and c 204, §8; am L 1983, c 124, §17]

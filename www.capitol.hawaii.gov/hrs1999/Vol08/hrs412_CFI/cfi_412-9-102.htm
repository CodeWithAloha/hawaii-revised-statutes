§412:9-102 Annual license fee. On or before December 31 of each year, each financial services loan company shall pay to the commissioner an annual license fee of $50 for each license that it holds for the ensuing year. A financial services loan company whose application for a license was approved in December may pay to the commissioner the first annual license fee of $50 for the ensuing year on or before the expiration of thirty days after receiving notice of the approval of the financial services loan company's application. [L 1993, c 350, pt of §1]

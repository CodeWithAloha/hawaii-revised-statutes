§425-12 Fee for filing documents and issuing certificates. [Section effective until June 30, 2000. For section effective July 1, 2000, see below.] The following fees shall be paid to the director upon the filing of general partnership documents:

(1) Partnership registration statement, $25;

(2) Partnership change of name statement, $25;

(3) Partnership dissolution statement, $25;

(4) Foreign general partnership registration statement, $25;

(5) Statement of change, $25;

(6) Application for certificate of withdrawal, $10;

(7) Statement of correction, $25;

(8) Reservation of name, $20;

(9) Transfer of reservation of name, $20;

    (10) Annual statement for domestic or foreign general partnership, $10;

    (11) Good standing certificate, $25;

    (12) Any other statement, certificate, or other document for a domestic or foreign general partnership, $25;

    (13) Special handling fee for review of any general partnership document, $20;

    (14) Special handling fee for certificates issued by the director, $20 per certificate; and

    (15) Special handling fee for certification of documents, $1 per page.

All fees collected under this section shall be managed in accordance with section 26-9(l). [L 1969, c 247, pt of §1; am L 1979, c 162, §1; am L 1982, c 204, §8; am L 1983, c 124, §17; am L 1985, c 189, §7; am L 1988, c 141, §48; am L 1992, c 6, §7; am L 1996, c 181, §3; am L 1999, c 129, §13]

 

§425-12 Fee for filing documents and issuing certificates. [Section effective July 1, 2000. For section effective until June 30, 2000, see above.] (a) The following fees shall be paid to the director upon the filing of general partnership documents:

(1) Partnership registration statement, $25;

(2) Partnership change of name statement, $25;

(3) Partnership dissolution statement, $25;

(4) Foreign general partnership registration statement, $25;

(5) Statement of change, $25;

(6) Application [for] certificate of withdrawal, $10;

(7) Statement of correction, $25;

(8) Reservation of name, $20;

(9) Transfer of reservation of name, $20;

    (10) Annual statement for domestic or foreign general partnership, $10;

    (11) Good standing certificate, $25;

    (12) Articles of conversion, $200;

    (13) Any other statement, certificate, or other document for a domestic or foreign general partnership, $25;

    (14) Special handling fee for review of any general partnership document, $20;

    (15) Special handling fee for certificates issued by the director, $20 per certificate;

    (16) Special handling fee for certification of documents, $1 per page; and

    (17) Special handling fee for review of articles of conversion, $150.

(b) All fees collected under this section shall be managed in accordance with section 26-9(l).

§415A-22 Annual report of professional corporations. The annual report of each professional corporation shall be delivered to the director for filing pursuant to the Hawaii Business Corporation Act, chapter 415, and shall include a statement that all of the shareholders, not less than one-half of the directors, and all of the officers other than the secretary and treasurer of the corporation are qualified persons with respect to the corporation. [L 1985, c 259, pt of §1; am L 1987, c 135, §126]

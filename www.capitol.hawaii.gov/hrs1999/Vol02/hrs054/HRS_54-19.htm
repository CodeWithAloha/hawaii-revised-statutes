§54-19 Construction, additions, extensions, increases, betterments, and improvements. The board of water supply shall locate and determine the character and type of all construction and additions, extensions, increases, betterments, and improvements to the waterworks, and shall determine the policy for construction or the making of additions, extensions, increases, betterments, and improvements out of any public funds under its jurisdiction. [L 1961, c 155, pt of §1; Supp, §145A-9; HRS §54-19]

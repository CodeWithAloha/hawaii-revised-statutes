[§103D-318] Finality of determinations. The determinations required by sections 103D-302(g), 103D-303(a), 103D-303(g), 103D-306, 103D-307, 103D-310, 103D-312(c), 103D-313, and 103D-314 shall be final and conclusive unless they are clearly erroneous, arbitrary, capricious, or contrary to law.

§103D-321 Record of procurement actions. (a) The chief procurement officer shall maintain a record of all procurements for goods, services, or construction of $25,000 or more made under sections 103D-102(b)(4), 103D-304, 103D-306, and 103D-307 for a minimum of five years. The record shall contain:

(1) Each contractor's name;

(2) The amount and type of each contract; and

(3) A listing of the goods, services, or construction procured under each contract.

(b) A copy of the record shall be submitted to the legislature on an annual basis. The record shall be available for public inspection. [L Sp 1993, c 8, pt of §2; am L 1995, c 178, §14; am L 1997, c 352, §11; am L 1999, c 149, §16]

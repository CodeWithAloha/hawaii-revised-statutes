BOARD OF TRUSTEES

§87-11 Composition of board. The board of trustees shall consist of nine trustees, three of whom shall be representatives from different organizations representing public employees, three from different private business organizations, a member of the clergy, a teacher, and the director of finance or a designated representative. [L 1961, c 146, pt of §1; am L 1963, c 114, §1; Supp, §5A-6; HRS §87-11; am L 1988, c 287, §1]

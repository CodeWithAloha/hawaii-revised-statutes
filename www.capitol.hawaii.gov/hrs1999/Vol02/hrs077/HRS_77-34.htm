§77-34 Provisions of previous sections applicable. All of the provisions of this chapter not by their terms inapplicable apply with equal force to each of the counties of Hawaii, Maui, and Kauai as the same apply to the State. The term "State" wherever it appears means each of the counties; the term "governor" means the mayor of each of such counties; the term "legislature" means the county council of each county; and the term "fiscal officer" means the director of finance. [L 1955, c 274, pt of §2; RL 1955, §4-31; HRS §77-34]

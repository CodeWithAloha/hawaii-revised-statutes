§76-26 Reemployment of retirees for service-connected disability. Any former employee who has been retired for a service-connected disability but who is not totally and permanently incapacitated from performing service shall be reemployed in those classes for which the employee requests reemployment and for which the employee meets minimum qualification requirements. The appointment shall not be subject to competitive examination. The order of certification when more than one person is eligible for certification for a particular class shall be fixed by rule. [L 1963, c 183, §1; Supp, §3-21(w); HRS §76-26; gen ch 1985; am L 1998, c 151, §2]

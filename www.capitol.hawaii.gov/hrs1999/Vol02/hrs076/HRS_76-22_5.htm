§76-22.5 Recruitment flexibility. [Repeal of section on June 30, 1994, by L 1992, c 32, §5, deleted by L 1994, c 223, §7.] Notwithstanding section 76-23, the director of human resources development shall determine, establish, and maintain the manner in which positions shall be filled in accordance with section 78-1 and the following standards:

(1) Equal opportunity for all regardless of race, sex, age, religion, color, ancestry, physical handicap, or politics;

(2) First consideration for competent employees already within public service; and

(3) Impartial selection of the ablest person through competitive means which are fair, objective, and practical. [L 1992, c 32, §2; am L 1994, c 223, §7; am L 1998, c 11, §4]

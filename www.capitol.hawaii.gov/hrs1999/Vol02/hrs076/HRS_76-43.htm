§76-43 Layoff. Rules and regulations shall be promulgated by the director of human resources development to govern the conditions under which an employee is to be released from the employee's position due to lack of work or lack of funds. [L 1965, c 146, §1; Supp, §3-21(x); HRS §76-43; gen ch 1985; am L 1994, c 56, §21]

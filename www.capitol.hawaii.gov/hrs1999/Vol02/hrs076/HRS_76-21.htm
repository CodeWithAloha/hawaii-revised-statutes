§76-21 Noncompetitive examinations. Noncompetitive examinations may be given when, in the opinion of the director of human resources development, the class for which an examination is to be given calls for special qualifications and training which do not admit of competition. [L 1955, c 274, pt of §1; RL 1955, pt of §3-21(d); HRS §76-21; am L 1994, c 56, §21]

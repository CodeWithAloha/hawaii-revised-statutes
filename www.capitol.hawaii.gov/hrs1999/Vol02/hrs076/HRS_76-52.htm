§76-52 Reports and statements. Every officer and employee, including any appointing authority, shall promptly make such reports and file such statements as may be required by this part or the rules and regulations prescribed thereunder. In case any officer or employee refuses or fails within the prescribed or within a reasonable time to make a report or file a statement, the director of human resources development or in case of the counties the civil service commission may notify the comptroller of the refusal or failure, and upon notification, the comptroller shall withhold any compensation payable to the officer or employee until such time as the commission or the director notifies the comptroller in writing that the officer or employee has made the report or filed the statement. [L 1955, c 274, pt of §1; RL 1955, §3-28; am L 1957, c 152, §1; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §11; HRS §76-52; am L 1994, c 56, §21]

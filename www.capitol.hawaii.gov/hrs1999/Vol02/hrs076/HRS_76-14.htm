§76-14 General duties of commission. The civil service commission shall hear and decide appeals from any action of the director of human resources development under this chapter, as well as from dismissals, demotions, and suspensions as hereinafter provided. [L 1955, c 274, pt of §1; RL 1955, pt of §3-17; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §11; HRS §76-14; am L 1994, c 56, §21]

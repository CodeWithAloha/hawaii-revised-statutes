§79-12 Rules. The head of a department shall be required to provide all officers and employees under the department head's supervision with an annual statement showing their accumulated annual leave and sick leave. The director of human resources development of the State and the respective directors of personnel services for each of the political subdivisions of the State shall adopt rules relating to leaves of absences provided by this chapter, subject to the approval of the governor, in the case of the State, and the respective mayors and legislative bodies, in the case of the counties.

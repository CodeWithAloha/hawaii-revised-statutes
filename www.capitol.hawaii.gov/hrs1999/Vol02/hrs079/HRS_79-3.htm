§79-3 Certain leave not considered vacation. Except in the case of members of the fire departments of the State and the several counties, officers or employees working without a regular weekly holiday shall been entitled to and granted four days' leave with pay for every twenty-eight days of service, which leave shall not be accumulated or considered as vacation. [L 1941, c 329, pt of §1; RL 1945, pt of §550; am L 1953, c 278, pt of §7; am L 1955, c 274, pt of §3; RL 1955, §5-34; HRS §79-3]

§88-26 Expenses of trustees. The trustees shall serve without compensation but they shall be reimbursed from the expense fund for all necessary expenses and for any loss of salary or wages they may suffer through serving the board of trustees. [L 1925, c 55, §5(4); RL 1935, pt of §7924; RL 1945, pt of §707; RL 1955, §6-63; HRS §88-26; am L 1969, c 110, pt of §1]

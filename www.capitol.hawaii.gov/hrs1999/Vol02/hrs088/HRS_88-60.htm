§88-60 Members whose services are on loan to other governments. Any member whose services are on loan to another government, as authorized by section 79-17, shall retain the member's membership and shall receive credit in the system for such service, provided the member returns to the member's former employment within ninety days after the termination of such service and, provided further that the government receiving the loan of the member's services fulfills all of the requirements of section 79-18. [L 1969, c 110, pt of §1; gen ch 1985]

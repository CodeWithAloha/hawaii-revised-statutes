§88-193 Compliance with law required. No pension shall be granted by any county (except under present provisions of law relating to police, firefighters, and bandsmen and under part V) unless the same shall comply with the requirements of this part. [L 1937, c 237, §7; RL 1945, §6190; am imp L 1945, c 264; RL 1955, §6-172; HRS §88-193; am L 1983, c 124, §15]

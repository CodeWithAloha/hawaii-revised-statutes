Attorney General Opinions

Employees are entitled to overtime compensation for all hours worked on a legal holiday. Att. Gen. Op. 62-42.

Employee scheduled to work on legal holiday, but taken sick should not be charged sick leave. Att. Gen. Op. 64-30.

Change in work shift does not entitle employee to overtime. Att. Gen. Op. 64-30.

Subs. (g) does not apply to part-time employee. Att. Gen. Op. 64-30.

For employees on Monday to Thursday work schedule, state holiday falling on Friday is to be observed on Monday. Att. Gen. Op. 64-43.

Type of employees contemplated under subs. (c) discussed. Att. Gen. Op. 64-43.

Application of overtime provisions for part-time employees discussed. Att. Gen. Op. 69-21.

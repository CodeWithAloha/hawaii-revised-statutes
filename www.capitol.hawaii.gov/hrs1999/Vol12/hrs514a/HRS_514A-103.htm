§514A-103 Designation of residential units. The developer of any project containing residential units shall designate at least fifty per cent of such units for sale to prospective owner-occupants pursuant to section 514A-105. Such units shall constitute a proportionate representation of all the residential units in the project with regard to factors of square footage, number of bedrooms and bathrooms, floor level, and whether or not such unit has a lanai. [L 1980, c 189, pt of §2; am L 1985, c 164, §5; am L 1992, c 50, pt of §2]

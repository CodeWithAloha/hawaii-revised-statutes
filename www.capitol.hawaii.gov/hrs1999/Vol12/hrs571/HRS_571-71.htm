PART VII. JUVENILE CRIME PREVENTION BUREAU

§571-71 Juvenile crime prevention bureau; establishment of. Any chief of police may establish as a subdivision of the police department under the chief's jurisdiction a juvenile crime prevention bureau, to be maintained and conducted as hereinafter provided. [L 1965, c 232, pt of §1; Supp, §333-32; HRS §571-71; gen ch 1985]

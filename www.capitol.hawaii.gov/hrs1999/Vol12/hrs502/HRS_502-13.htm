§502-13 Names of parties indexed. The registrar shall, within a reasonable time, cause the name of each and every grantor, grantee, or other party thereto to be entered at length and alphabetically in its appropriate index. [L 1905, c 4, §4; RL 1925, §3130; RL 1935, §5116; RL 1945, §12716; RL 1955, §343-7; HRS §502-13; gen ch 1993]

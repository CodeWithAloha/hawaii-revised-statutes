§431:10D-110 Incontestability after reinstatement. The reinstatement of any policy of life insurance or contract of annuity delivered or issued for delivery in this State may be contestable on account of fraud or misrepresentation of facts material to the reinstatement only for the same period following reinstatement as the policy provides with respect to contestability after original issuance. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2]

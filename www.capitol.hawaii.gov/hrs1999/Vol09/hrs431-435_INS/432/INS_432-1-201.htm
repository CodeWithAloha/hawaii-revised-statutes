PART II. ORGANIZATION

§432:1-201 Incorporation by charter. Any mutual benefit society may be or become incorporated by charter as provided in sections 416-19 and 416-20, notwithstanding any limitations under the corporation law as to the purposes for which charters may be granted. Any society so chartered as a corporation shall be subject in all respects to this article. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2]

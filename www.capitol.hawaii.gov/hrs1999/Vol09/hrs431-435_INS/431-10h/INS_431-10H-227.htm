[§431:10H-227] Filing requirements. Prior to an insurer or similar organization offering group long-term care insurance to a resident of this State pursuant to section 431:10H-105, it shall file with the commissioner evidence that the group policy or certificate thereunder has been approved by a state having statutory or regulatory long-term care insurance requirements substantially similar to those adopted in this State. [L 1999, c 93, pt of §2]

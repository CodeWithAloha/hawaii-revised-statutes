[§431:10H-303] Conflict with Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act. If a conflict occurs between a provision of this article and the federal Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996, P.L. 104-191, as amended, the provision shall be deemed amended to comply with that federal Act and any related regulations, to the extent that a particular policy is intended to qualify for federal income tax benefits. [L 1999, c 93, pt of §2]

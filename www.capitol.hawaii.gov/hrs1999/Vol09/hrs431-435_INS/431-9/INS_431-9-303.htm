[§431:9-303] Continuing education recordkeeping. [Section effective January 1, 2001.] (a) Licensees shall maintain their own continuing education records and shall keep the records for four years after completion of an approved continuing education course.

(b) Approved course providers shall maintain attendance records for five years for the commissioner to verify the attendance and course completion of all licensees enrolled in an approved course. These course providers shall make the records available at all times to the commissioner. [L 1999, c 163, pt of §2]

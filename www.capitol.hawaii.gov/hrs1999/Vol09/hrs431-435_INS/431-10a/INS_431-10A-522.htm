§431:10A-522 Prohibition. [Section repealed on July 1, 2000. L 1999, c 93, §§8,18.] (a) No insurance policy may be advertised, marketed, or offered as long-term care or nursing home insurance unless it complies with this part.

(b) No group long-term care insurance may be offered to a resident of this State under a group policy issued in another state to a group described in paragraph (4) of the definition of "group long-term care insurance" unless this State, or another state having statutory and regulatory long-term care insurance requirements substantially similar to those adopted in this State, has made a determination that the requirements have been met. [L 1989, c 335, pt of §2; am L 1990, c 34, §28; am L 1991, c 48, §6]

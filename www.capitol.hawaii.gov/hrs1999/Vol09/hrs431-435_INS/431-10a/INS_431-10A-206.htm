§431:10A-206 Coverage of newborn children. All group or blanket policies providing family coverage, as defined in section 431:10A-103 and reciprocal beneficiary family coverage, as defined in section 431:10A-601, on an expense incurred basis shall provide coverage for newborn children in compliance with section 431:10A-115. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2; am L 1997, c 383, §7]

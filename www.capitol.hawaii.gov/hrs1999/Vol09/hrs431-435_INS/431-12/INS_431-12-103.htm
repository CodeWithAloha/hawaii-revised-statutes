§431:12-103 Mass merchandising authorized. An insurer may mass merchandise motor vehicle, property and casualty insurance to the employees of any employer under a mass merchandising plan audited by the commissioner; provided that such mass merchandising is agreed to by the employer. An employer may contract with one or more insurers for mass merchandising of motor vehicle, property and casualty insurance to its employees. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2]

[§432D-7] Investments. All investments permitted under this section or section 432D-3(a)(1) can be considered as admitted assets in determination of net worth; provided that these investments are in compliance with rules adopted by the commissioner. With the exception of investments made in accordance with section 432D-3(a)(1), the funds of a health maintenance organization shall be invested only as permitted by rules adopted by the commissioner pursuant to chapter 91.

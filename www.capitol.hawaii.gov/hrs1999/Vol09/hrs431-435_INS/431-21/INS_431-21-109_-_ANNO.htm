Case Notes

Decisions under prior law.

Chapter 294

Although negligent infliction of emotional distress claims are entitled to independent protection under general Hawai`i tort law, emotional distress claims under chapter 294 are derivative, when; derivative claims are subject to "each person" liability coverage limit. 77 H. 2, 881 P.2d 489.

Chapter 294, Pt. I

Did not preempt financial responsibility law. 76 H. 209, 873 P.2d 88.

§294-2

"Accidental harm", under chapter 294, includes emotional distress. 77 H. 2, 881 P.2d 489.

§431-448

Automobile insurance policies may bar dual recovery under both liability and underinsured coverages without violating the underinsured motorist statute. 77 H. 490 (App.), 889 P.2d 67.

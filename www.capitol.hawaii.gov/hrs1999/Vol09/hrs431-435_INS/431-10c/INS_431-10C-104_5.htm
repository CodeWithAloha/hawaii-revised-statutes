§431:10C-104.5 Amnesty period for uninsured motorists. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, any penalties and any provision for surcharge based on prior failure, since January 1, 1996, to maintain no-fault or motor vehicle insurance or suspension or revocation of license due solely to failure to maintain no-fault or motor vehicle insurance shall not apply to any uninsured motorist who obtains the required coverages prior to December 31, 1999. [L 1992, c 124, pt of §1; am L 1997, c 251, §15; am L 1998, c 275, §7; am L 1999, c 109, §2]

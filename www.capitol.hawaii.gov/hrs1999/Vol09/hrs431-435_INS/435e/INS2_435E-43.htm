§435E-43 Investigation, publication. The commissioner may, in the commissioner's discretion, (1) make such public or private investigations within or outside of this State as the commissioner deems necessary to determine whether any person has violated or is about to violate this chapter, or to aid in the enforcement of this chapter, and (2) publish information concerning the violation of this chapter. [L 1977, c 182, pt of §1; am L 1982, c 203, §16; gen ch 1985]

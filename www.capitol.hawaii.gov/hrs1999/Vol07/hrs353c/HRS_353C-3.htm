[§353C-3] Deputy directors; appointment. The director shall appoint, without regard to chapters 76 and 77, three deputy directors to serve at the director's pleasure. Unless otherwise assigned by the director, one deputy director shall oversee the correctional programs and facilities of the department, one deputy director shall oversee the law enforcement programs of the department, and one deputy director shall oversee administration of the department.

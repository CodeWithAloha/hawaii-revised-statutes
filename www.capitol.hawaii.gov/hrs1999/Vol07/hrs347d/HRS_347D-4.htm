§347D-4 Responsibility of council for payment of interpreter services. The council, subject to legislative appropriations, shall coordinate the payment of interpreter services for deaf, hard-of-hearing, or deaf-blind persons participating in the programs and activities of tax exempt organizations qualified under section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, which do not receive federal financial assistance. For the purposes of this section, payment of interpreter services may include transportation and per diem. [L 1987, c 289, pt of §2; am L 1989, c 397, §2; am L 1991, c 270, §4]

[§397-5.5] Boiler and elevator safety revolving fund. [Section repealed July 31, 2003.] There is established a revolving fund for use by the department in providing inspection services for boilers, pressure systems, or elevators and kindred equipment. Moneys in the fund may be expended for materials, salaries, equipment, training, and other costs related to providing inspection services. Moneys derived from the inspection services provided under section 397-5 and from other sources shall be deposited in the fund. The balance of moneys in the fund shall not exceed $1,200,000; provided that moneys in excess of $1,200,000 shall be deposited into the state general fund. [L 1998, c 142, §1]

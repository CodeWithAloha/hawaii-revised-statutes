§364-5 Commitments to purchase veterans mortgages. The director of finance may enter into commitment contracts with mortgagees for the purchase of veterans mortgages not in existence, or for which full disbursement has not been made, at the time the contract is entered into. [L 1953, c 211, §5; RL 1955, §350-5; am L 1961, c 193, §2; HRS §364-5]

§364-11 Deposit of bonds. Any other provision of law to the contrary notwithstanding, any bonds issued under this chapter may, with the approval of the governor, be deposited with and pledged to, or be otherwise disposed of to, the United States or any board, agency, or instrumentality of the United States government, to secure the repayment, or in actual payment, of any loans or advances made or to be made, under any act or acts of the Congress of the United States authorizing such loans or advances, by the United States, or any such board, agency, or instrumentality to the State for the purchase of veterans mortgages. [L 1953, c 211, §11; RL 1955, §350-11; am L 1961, c 193, §2; HRS §364-11]

§375-13 Penalty. No employer shall employ any industrial homeworker under a special certificate in violation of the terms thereof. Any employer who wilfully violates this chapter shall be subject to a fine of not more than $500 or imprisonment for not more than ninety days, or both such fine or imprisonment, for each such offense. Each day a violation shall continue shall constitute a separate offense. [L 1959, c 53, §13; am L 1963, c 193, §31; Supp, §96A-13; HRS §375-13]

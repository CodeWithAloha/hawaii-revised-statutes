§375-8 Termination of certificates. Certificates shall be valid under the terms set forth in the certificate for a period of not more than twelve months from the date of issuance or such shorter period as may be fixed in the certificate. Application for renewal of any certificate shall be filed in the same manner as an original application. A "properly executed" application is one which contains the complete information required on the form. [L 1959, c 53, §8; Supp, §96A-8; HRS §375-8]

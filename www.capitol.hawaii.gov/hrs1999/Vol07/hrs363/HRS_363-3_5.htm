[§363-3.5] Advisory board on veterans' services; appointment; duties. (a) There shall be a policy advisory board on veterans' services. The board shall consist of seven members appointed by the governor as provided in section 26-34. Five members shall be veterans, and there shall be at least one member residing in each of the counties except Kalawao county. The director of health, the director of human services, the director of labor and industrial relations, and the adjutant general shall serve as ex-officio members. The chairperson of the board shall be elected by the majority of the board. The members shall serve without compensation but shall be allowed their actual and necessary expenses incurred in the performance of their duties.

(b) The advisory board shall advise the director in, but not limited to, the following areas:

(1) The identification of issues and alternative approaches to solutions;

(2) The development of position statements and papers;

(3) Advocacy and legislative actions; and

(4) Program development and operations. [L 1988, c 115, §7]

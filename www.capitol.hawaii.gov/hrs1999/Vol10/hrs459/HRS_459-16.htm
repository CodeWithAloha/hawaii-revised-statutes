[§459-16] Adverse effects, temporary reporting requirements. The board of examiners in optometry shall promulgate rules and regulations as will require a licensed optometrist to report findings of adverse effects experienced by any of the optometrist's patients from the optometrist's use of pharmaceutical agents during an examination. This requirement shall expire on June 30, 1988. [L 1985, c 294, §6; gen ch 1985]

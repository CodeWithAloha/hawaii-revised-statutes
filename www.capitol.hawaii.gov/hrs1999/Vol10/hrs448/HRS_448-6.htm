§448-6 Powers and duties, meetings. (a) The board shall meet for the purpose of examining applicants and for other purposes at times that it designates. Adequate statewide public notice of the times and places of examinations shall be given. The board may prescribe which members shall participate in the examination and licensing procedures.

(b) In addition to any other powers and duties authorized by law, the board shall adopt such rules as it deems proper and necessary for the performance of its work.

[§443B-6] Place of business; principal collector. (a) A collection agency shall have and maintain a regular active business office in the State for the purpose of conducting collection agency business. The business office shall be open to the public during stated reasonable business hours.

(b) Every collection agency shall designate a principal collector to assume responsibility for the direct management and control of the daily operation of the office. [L 1987, c 191, pt of §1]

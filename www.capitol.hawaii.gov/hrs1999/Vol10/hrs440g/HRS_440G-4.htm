§440G-4 Issuance of cable franchises and regulation of cable operators by director of commerce and consumer affairs. The director shall be empowered to issue cable franchises and otherwise administer and enforce this chapter. [L 1970, c 112, pt of §1; am L 1982, c 204, §8; am L 1983, c 124, §17; am L 1987, c 301, §4]

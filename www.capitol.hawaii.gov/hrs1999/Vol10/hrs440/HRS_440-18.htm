§440-18 Inspectors; duties. The director of commerce and consumer affairs shall employ official representatives designated as inspectors, each of whom shall receive from the boxing commission a card or badge authorizing the person to act as inspector whenever the commission may designate the person to so act. An inspector or the secretary shall be present at all boxing contests and see that this chapter and the rules are strictly observed and shall also be present at the counting of the gross receipts or proceeds of all boxing contests, and shall, as soon as practicable thereafter, mail or deliver to the commission the official box office statement received by the inspector or secretary.

§440-24 Number of rounds. The boxing commission may, in respect to any bout or in respect to any class of contestants, limit the number of rounds of a bout within the maximum of fifteen rounds. [L 1929, c 216, §27; RL 1935, §7014; RL 1945, §7575; am L 1949, c 264, §13; RL 1955, §165-26; HRS §440-24]

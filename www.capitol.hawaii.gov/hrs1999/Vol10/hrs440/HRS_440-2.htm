§440-2 Commission established. There shall be a board which shall be known as the state boxing commission of Hawaii. The commission shall consist of five members. One of the members shall be a member of the Hawaiian association of an amateur athletic federation of the United States of America. One member shall be designated by the governor as chairperson of the commission.

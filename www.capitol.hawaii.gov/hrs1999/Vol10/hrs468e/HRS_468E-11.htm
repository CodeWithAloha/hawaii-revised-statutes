§468E-11 Waiver of examination or parts thereof. The board shall waive the requirements of section 468E-5 for applicants for licensure who, on the effective date of this chapter, are actually engaged in this State in the practice of speech pathology or audiology, upon proof of bona fide practice presented to the board in a manner prescribed by rules adopted by the board. [L 1974, c 48, pt of §1; am L 1988, c 320, §8]

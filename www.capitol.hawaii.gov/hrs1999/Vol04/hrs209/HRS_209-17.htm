§209-17 Use of funds in relation to federal projects. The funds allocated to this part shall be expended by the housing and community development corporation of Hawaii only upon the finding that the housing project found necessary does not qualify for federal aid or participation. [L 1961, c 189, §11; Supp, §98P-11; HRS §209-17; am L 1986, c 339, §14; am L 1987, c 337, §9(2); am L 1997, c 350, §14]

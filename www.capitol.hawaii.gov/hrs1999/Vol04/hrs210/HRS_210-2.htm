§210-2 Hawaii capital loan program. There is created a capital loan program which shall be administered by the director of business, economic development, and tourism in accordance with the spirit and intent of this chapter. [L 1963, c 76, §3; Supp, §98R-3; HRS §210-2; am L 1987, c 336, §7; am L 1990, c 293, §8]

§226-56 Functional plans; form and submittal. (a) Functional plans shall be prepared to further define and implement statewide goals, objectives, policies, and priority guidelines contained in this chapter.

(b) Functional plans shall be prepared and revised in accordance with guidelines developed by the department of budget and finance.

(c) The governor shall transmit approved state functional plans to the legislature for its information. [L 1978, c 100, pt of §2; am L 1980, c 225, §3; am L 1984, c 236, §9; am L 1987, c 336, §4(8); am and ren L 1991, c 76, pt of §1]

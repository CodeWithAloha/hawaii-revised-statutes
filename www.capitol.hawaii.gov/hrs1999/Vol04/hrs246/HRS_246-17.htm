§246-17 Development and maintenance of wasteland development property. Within one year following the approval of the application, the owner shall develop that portion of the owner's land as specified in the owner's application and as approved by the director of taxation. Additional areas shall be developed each year as prescribed by the director. [L 1965, c 255, §6; Supp, §128-9.35; HRS §246-17; gen ch 1985]

D. Public Housing; Liens

Note

Subpart heading amended by L 1998, c 212, §12.

§201G-71 Definitions. The following terms wherever used or referred to in this subpart shall have the following respective meanings, unless a different meaning clearly appears from the context:

"Housing project" means and includes any housing project or projects owned, managed, administered, or operated by the corporation under or pursuant to subparts A and B or under or pursuant to any other law.

"Tenant" includes any person occupying a room, dwelling accommodation, living quarters, or space in any housing project, under or by virtue of any tenancy lease, license, or permit under or from the corporation. [L 1997, c 350, pt of §2; am L 1998, c 212, §16]

PART III. APPROVAL OF LOCATION AND

SANITARY CONDITION OF BUSINESSES

§322-41 Location of slaughterhouses. No slaughterhouse shall be maintained in any part of the State, in any place where the department of health forbids the maintenance of the same. [L 1868, c 27, §1; RL 1925, §931; RL 1935, §1318; RL 1945, §2709; RL 1955, §47-40; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §19; HRS §322-41]

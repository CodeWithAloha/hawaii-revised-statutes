PART IV. DWELLINGS; LODGING HOUSES

§322-51 Grounds; closets of dwellings. The yard and grounds about all dwellings shall be well drained and kept free from rubbish of every description, with a closet, or privy, also to be kept in repair by the lodging house keeper or employer of laborers, for every six adults. [L 1880, c 3, §2; RL 1925, §971; RL 1935, §1321; RL 1945, §2712; RL 1955, §47-51; HRS §322-51]

§322-52 Entry by health officers. Every owner or keeper and every other person having the care or management of a lodging house or of a dwelling for laborers, shall at all times when required by the department of health or its agents give free access to the house or any part thereof. [L 1880, c 3, §3; RL 1925, §972; RL 1935, §1322; RL 1945, §2713; RL 1955, §47-52; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §19; HRS §322-52]

PART III. REGULATION OF MANUFACTURE, DISTRIBUTION,

PRESCRIPTION, AND DISPENSING OF CONTROLLED SUBSTANCES

§329-31 Rules. The department of public safety may promulgate rules and charge reasonable fees relating to the registration and control of the manufacture, distribution, prescription, and dispensing of controlled substances within this State. [L 1972, c 10, pt of §1; am L 1990, c 281, §10]

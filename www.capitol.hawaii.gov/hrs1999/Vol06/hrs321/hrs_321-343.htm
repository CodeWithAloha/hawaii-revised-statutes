[§321-343] Access to information. (a) Upon written request of the director, all providers of medical care and state and county agencies shall disclose to the department, and those individuals appointed by the director to participate in the review of child deaths, child death review information regarding the circumstances of a child's death so that the department may conduct a multidisciplinary and multiagency review of child deaths pursuant to section 321-31 and this part.

(b) To the extent that this section conflicts with other state confidentiality laws, this section shall prevail. [L 1997, c 369, pt of §1]

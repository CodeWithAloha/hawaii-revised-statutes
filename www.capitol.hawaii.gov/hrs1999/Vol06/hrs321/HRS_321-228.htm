§321-228 Emergency medical services; counties. The department of health shall determine, in consultation with the advisory committee under section 321-225, the levels of emergency medical services which shall be implemented in each county. The department of health may contract to provide emergency medical services or any necessary component of a county emergency services system in conformance with the state system. In the event any county shall apply to the department to operate emergency medical ambulance services within the respective county, the department of health may contract with the county for the provision of such services. The department shall operate emergency medical ambulance services or contract with a private agency in those counties which do not apply to it under this section. Any county or private agency contracting to provide emergency medical ambulance services under this section shall be required by the department to implement such services in a manner and at a level consistent with the levels determined under this section.

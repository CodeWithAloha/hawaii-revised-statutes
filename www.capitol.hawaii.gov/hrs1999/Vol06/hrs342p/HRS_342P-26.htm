§342P-26 Injunctive and other relief. The director may institute a civil action for injunctive and other relief in any court of competent jurisdiction to prevent the violation of this chapter, any rule adopted pursuant to this chapter, or any condition of a permit or variance issued pursuant to this chapter, without the necessity of a prior revocation of the permit or variance, to impose and collect civil penalties, to collect administrative penalties, or to obtain other relief. The court may grant relief in accordance with the Hawaii rules of civil procedure. [L 1991, c 219, pt of §1; am L 1995, c 180, §41]

[PART VII.] MARKETING ORDER REVOLVING FUND

[§147-101] Marketing order revolving fund. There is established a revolving fund for use by the department of agriculture in providing inspection services for federal marketing order programs. Moneys in the fund may be expended for materials, salaries, equipment, training, and other costs related to providing inspection services. Moneys derived from the inspection services provided shall be deposited in the fund. Marketing order inspectors hired under this chapter shall be exempt from chapters 76 and 77. [L 1994, c 264, §3]

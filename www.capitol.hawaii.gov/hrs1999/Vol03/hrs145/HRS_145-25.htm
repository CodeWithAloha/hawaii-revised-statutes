[§145-25] Enforcement; citation and summons. Violations of this part or any rule adopted pursuant thereto may be enforced by citation and summons issued by:

(1) Any state or county law enforcement officer; or

(2) Any officer or employee of the department of agriculture, authorized and designated by the board of agriculture to investigate and enforce this chapter and all rules adopted by the department pursuant thereto. [L 1999, c 186, pt of §2]

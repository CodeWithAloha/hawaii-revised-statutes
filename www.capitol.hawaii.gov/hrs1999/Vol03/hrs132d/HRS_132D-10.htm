[§132D-10] Permits. A permit shall be required for the purchase, setting off, ignition, or discharge of:

(1) Any non-aerial common fireworks when the proposed date of the use of the fireworks is not within the periods prescribed in section 132D-3(1); and

(2) Any aerial common and any special fireworks under all circumstances and at any time. [L 1994, c 180, pt of §1]

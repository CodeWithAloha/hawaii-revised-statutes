[§132D-11] Fee. The fee for the license required under section 132D-7 shall not exceed $110 for each year or fraction of a year in which the licensee plans to conduct business and shall be payable to the department. The fee for the permit required under section 132D-7 shall be no greater than $25 for each year or fraction of a year in which the permittee plans to conduct business and shall be payable to the county in which the permitted activity is to occur. [L 1994, c 180, pt of §1]

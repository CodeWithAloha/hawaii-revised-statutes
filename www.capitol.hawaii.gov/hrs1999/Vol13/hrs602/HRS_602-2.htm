§602-2 Salary, supreme court justices. Effective July 1, 1999, the salary of the chief justice of the supreme court shall be $98,571 a year and the salary of each associate justice of the supreme court shall be $97,531 a year. Effective July 1, 2000, the salary of the chief justice of the supreme court shall be $102,514 a year and the salary of each associate justice of the supreme court shall be $101,432 a year.

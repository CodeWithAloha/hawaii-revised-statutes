Case Notes

Necessary to allege relation of landlord and tenant exists or has existed, how such tenancy was created, whether by lease or parol, when and how terminated and that required notice to quit was given. 4 H. 154, 157; 13 H. 385; 22 H. 739. Complaint held sufficient. 15 H. 632.

Right to possession, not title in issue. 20 H. 712; 22 H. 129.

The question of whether the right of trial by jury guaranteed by Article I, section 10, State Constitution, applies to a summary possession proceeding was raised but not decided. 58 H. 276, 567 P.2d 1239.

Actions to dispossess lessees involving short-term rental agreements or other leases that grant lessees solely the right of possession may only be adjudicated in district court pursuant to this section. 74 H. 294, 845 P.2d 1186.

Cited: 6 H. 407, 408.

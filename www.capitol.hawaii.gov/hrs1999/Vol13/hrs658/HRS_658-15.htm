§658-15 Appeal when. Unless the agreement for award provides that no appeal may be taken an appeal may be taken from an order vacating an award, or from a judgment entered upon an award, as from an order or judgment in an action, otherwise no appeal may be had. [L 1925, c 276, pt of §1; RL 1935, §4184; RL 1945, §8715; RL 1955, §188-15; HRS §658-15]

§603-4 Other circuits, judges. The circuit court of the fifth circuit shall consist of one judge, who shall be styled judge of the circuit court of the fifth circuit. The circuit court of the second circuit shall consist of three judges, who shall be styled as first, second, and third judge, respectively, and each as a judge of the circuit court of the second circuit. The circuit court of the third circuit shall consist of three judges, who shall be styled as first, second, and third judge, respectively, and each as a judge of the circuit court of the third circuit.

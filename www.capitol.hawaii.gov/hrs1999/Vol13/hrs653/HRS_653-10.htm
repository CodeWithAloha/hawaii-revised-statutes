§653-10 Superiors and successors of garnishee. The obligations and inhibitions imposed upon the garnishee shall be equally binding upon the garnishee's official superiors and successors, to whose notice the fact shall come, of the service upon the garnishee. [L 1890, c 50, §9; RL 1925, §2854; RL 1935, §4299; RL 1945, §10330; RL 1955, §238-10; HRS §653-10; gen ch 1985]

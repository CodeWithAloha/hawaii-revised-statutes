[§612-21] Jurors disqualified, exempted, or excused. Whenever a juror has been disqualified, exempted, or excused, that fact shall be noted on the juror's juror qualification form and the juror shall not be subject to service for the period of time commensurate with the nature and circumstances of the juror's disqualification, exemption, or excuse. [L 1973, c 191, pt of §1; gen ch 1993]

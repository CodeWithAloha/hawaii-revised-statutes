Attorney General Opinions

Sales involving undivided interest in land followed by an agreement to manage and resell by the seller is within the definition of "security". Att. Gen. Op. 66-8.

Agreements between the condominium owner and the developer involving the rental and management of the apartment to the public is a "security". Att. Gen. Op. 66-12.

Sale of undivided interests in land based on possibility of profit to be derived from future sale of land when it has increased in value involves an investment contract and thus a security within this section. Att. Gen. Op. 70-25.

[§481H-8] Penalty and remedies. (a) Any violation of the provisions of this chapter shall constitute unfair methods of competition and unfair or deceptive acts or practices in the conduct of any trade or commerce under section 480-2.

(b) The penalty provided in subsection (a) is in addition to remedies otherwise available against the same conduct under the common law or other statutes of this State. [L 1991, c 260, pt of §1]

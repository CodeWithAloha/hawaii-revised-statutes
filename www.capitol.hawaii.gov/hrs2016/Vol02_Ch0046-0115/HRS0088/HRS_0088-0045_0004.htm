[§88-45.4]  Contributions for unpaid leaves of absence.  Contributions required as a condition to inclusion in membership service of unpaid leaves of absence shall be made by the member within one year after return from the leave of absence. [L 2015, c 86, §2]

 

Revision Note

 

  Section was enacted as an addition to subpart A but was codified to this subpart pursuant to §23G-15.

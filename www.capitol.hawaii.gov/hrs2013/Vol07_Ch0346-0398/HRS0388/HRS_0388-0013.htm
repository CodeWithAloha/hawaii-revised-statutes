§388-13  Rules and regulations.  The director of labor and industrial relations is authorized to issue such rules and regulations as the director determines necessary for the purpose of carrying out this chapter. [L 1963, c 158, pt of §3; Supp, §95-12; HRS §388-13; gen ch 1985]

§432D-17  Fees.  (a)  The commissioner shall collect in advance the following fees:

     (1)  For filing an application for a certificate of authority or amendment thereto, $600; and

     (2)  For renewal of the certificate of authority, $400 per year.

     (b)  The commissioner shall provide each holder of a certificate of authority at least thirty days' advance written notice of the applicable extension date.  If the fee for the extension of the certificate of authority is not paid before or on the extension date, a penalty shall be imposed in the amount of fifty per cent of the fee.  If the fee and the penalty are not paid within thirty days immediately following the extension date, the commissioner may revoke the certificate of authority and shall not reinstate the certificate of authority until the fee and penalty have been paid.

     (c)  All fees and penalties collected pursuant to this section and penalties collected pursuant to section 432D-14 shall be deposited to the credit of the compliance resolution fund. [L 1995, c 179, pt of §1; am L 1999, c 247, §2; am L 2012, c 251, §13]

Case Notes

 

  Where appellant was already bound by an arbitration agreement executed on February 24, 2000, and therefore the post-July 1, 2002 arbitration agreement was not a "new" arbitration agreement that would dictate the application of this chapter to the arbitration proceedings, §658A-3(a) was inapplicable to the case.  113 H. 127, 149 P.3d 495.

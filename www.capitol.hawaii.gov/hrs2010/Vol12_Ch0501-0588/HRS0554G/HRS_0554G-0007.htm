[§554G-7]  Retained interests of transferor.  (a)  A permitted transfer shall be subject to this chapter notwithstanding a transferor's retention of any or all of the powers and rights described in section 554G-5(c) and notwithstanding the transferor's service as investment advisor pursuant to section 554G-5(e)(3).

     (b)  The transferor shall have only the powers and rights specifically conferred by the trust instrument.  Except as permitted by sections 554G-5(c) and 554G-5(e), a transferor shall have no rights or authority with respect to the property that is the subject of a permitted transfer or to the income from property that is the subject of a permitted transfer.  Any agreement or understanding purporting to grant or permit the retention of any greater rights or authority shall be void. [L 2010, c 182, pt of §2]

§155-3  Restriction.  Loans provided for by this chapter shall be authorized only if these loans cannot be made by two lenders, which may include any of the following:

     (1)  Private lenders;

     (2)  Members of the farm credit system; or

     (3)  The United States Department of Agriculture; provided that the board of agriculture may waive this requirement for emergency loans. [L 1961, c 104, §2; Supp, §102-3.5; HRS §155-3; am L 1979, c 222, §3; am L 1990, c 259, §1; am L 1997, c 258, §1; am L 1999, c 32, §2]

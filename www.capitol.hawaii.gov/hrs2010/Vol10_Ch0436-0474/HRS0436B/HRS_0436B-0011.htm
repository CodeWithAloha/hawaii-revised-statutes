§436B-11  Additional licensure requirements.  In addition to any other requirements provided by law, the licensing authority may also require by rule that the applicant provide the following:

     (1)  In the case of a partnership applicant, each general partner shall attest that each partner is beyond the age of majority;

     (2)  Proof that the applicant is competent, trustworthy, fair, and has financial integrity; and

     (3)  Proof that the applicant has satisfied all applicable business registration requirements prior to applying for licensure. [L 1991, c 111, pt of §2; am L 1992, c 202, §13; am L 1995, c 35, §3]

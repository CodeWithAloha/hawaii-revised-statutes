HRS 0657-0001_0005 ANNOTATIONS

 

Case Notes

 

  Plaintiffs water commission and department of water supply of county of Hawai`i could not benefit from this section; plaintiffs were obligated to bring their claims in a timely manner within applicable limitations period.  930 F. Supp. 1411.

  Where plaintiff office of Hawaiian affairs brought suit in its own corporate name under §10-16, rather than as an agency of the State on behalf of the people of the State, this section did not exempt plaintiff from the statute of limitations for bringing a suit under §673-10.  110 H. 338, 133 P.3d 767.

§489-6  Complaint against unfair discrimination; reporting requirements.  The civil rights commission shall receive complaints of unfair discriminatory treatment in public accommodations in accordance with the procedures established under chapter 368; provided that this section shall not apply to complaints under part II of this chapter. [L 1986, c 292, pt of §1; am L 1989, c 386, §11; am L 2000, c 227, §4]

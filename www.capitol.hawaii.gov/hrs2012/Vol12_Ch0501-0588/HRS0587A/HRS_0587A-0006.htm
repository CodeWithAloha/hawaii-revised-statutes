[§587A-6]  Venue.  A child protective proceeding under this chapter may be filed in the county in which a child is found or resides when the petition is filed, or in the county in which a parent having legal custody of the child resides or is domiciled when the petition is filed. [L 2010, c 135, pt of §1]

§501-110  Statement of encumbrances.  If at the time of any transfer there appears upon the registration book encumbrances or claims adverse to the title of the registered owner, they shall be stated in the new certificate or certificates, except as far as they may be simultaneously released or discharged and except as provided in sections 501-241 to 501-248. [L 1903, c 56, §59; RL 1925, §3248; RL 1935, §5058; RL 1945, §12658; RL 1955, §342-59; HRS §501-110; am L 1998, c 219, §9]

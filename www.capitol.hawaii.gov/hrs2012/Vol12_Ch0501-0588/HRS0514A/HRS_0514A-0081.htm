PART V.  CONDOMINIUM MANAGEMENT

 

Note

 

  This part, referenced as repealed in the main volume, was reenacted by L 2007, c 244, pt of §2.  Source notes for each section in this part begin with L 2007, c 244.

 

     §514A-81  Bylaws.  The operation of the property shall be governed by bylaws, a true copy of which shall be recorded in the same manner as the declaration.  No amendment to the bylaws is valid unless the amendment is duly recorded. [L 2007, c 244, pt of §2]

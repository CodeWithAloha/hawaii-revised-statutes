§145-3 Records. Every produce dealer shall keep a record of each lot, shipment, or consignment of farm produce received or taken by the produce dealer, which shall be in such form and in such detail as may be prescribed by the department of agriculture. [L 1951, c 74, pt of §1; RL 1955, §23-3; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §22; am L 1961, c 132, §2; HRS §145-3; gen ch 1985]

[§246A-3] Pending proceedings. No suit or prosecution pending at the time this chapter takes effect shall be affected by this chapter. The right of the director of taxation to institute proceedings for prosecution for an offense or an action to recover a penalty or forfeiture shall henceforth be vested in such person or persons as may be directed by ordinance to be adopted by each of the respective counties. [L 1980, c 279, §5]

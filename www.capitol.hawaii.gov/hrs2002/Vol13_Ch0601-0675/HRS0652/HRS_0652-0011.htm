§652-11 Section 652-9 to appear on summons. Section 652-9 shall be printed or written conspicuously on every summons issuing out of any court of the State which is intended to be served on any alleged attorney, factor, trustee, or debtor of a defendant in any action. [L 1876, c 35, §14; RL 1925, §2841; RL 1935, §4280; RL 1945, §10311; RL 1955, §237-11; HRS §652-11]

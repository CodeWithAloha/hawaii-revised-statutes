§667-7 Notice, construction, contents; affidavit. The true intent and meaning of section 667-5 is that the notice of intention of foreclosure may also contain a description of the mortgaged property and a statement of the time and place proposed for the sale thereof at any time after the expiration of four weeks from the date when first advertised; and also that the affidavit contemplated by section 667-5 may lawfully be made by any person duly authorized to act for the mortgagee, and in such capacity conducting the foreclosure. [L 1890, c 9, §1; am L 1915, c 121, §2; RL 1925, §2880; RL 1935, §4725; RL 1945, §12425; RL 1955, §336-6; HRS §667-7]

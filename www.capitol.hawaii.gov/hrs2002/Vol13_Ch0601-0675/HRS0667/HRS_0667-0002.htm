§667-2 Other mortgagees joined. All prior and subsequent mortgage creditors, whose names are or can be discovered by the party foreclosing a mortgage, shall be made parties to the action. [CC 1859, §1232; RL 1925, §2888; RL 1935, §4721; RL 1945, §12421; RL 1955, §336-2; HRS §667-2; am L 1972, c 90, §9(b)]

Cross References

Case Notes

Section 657-18 did not apply to toll the no-fault statute of limitations where a nonresident motorist defendant was at all times subject to the jurisdiction of Hawaii's courts and amenable to service of process under its long-arm statutes as set forth in §634-36 and this section. 89 H. 1, 967 P.2d 1059.

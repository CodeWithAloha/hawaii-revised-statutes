HRS 0603-0037 ANNOTATIONS

Rules of Court

Hawaii Rules of Civil Procedure do not extend or limit jurisdiction or venue, see HRCP rule 82.

Case Notes

Change of venue and discretion therein. 9 H. 360; 11 H. 314, 317; 20 H. 7, 11; in ejectment, 19 H. 336; 31 H. 65.

Where action is brought in wrong circuit, this section cannot be used as a remedy to transfer case to proper circuit. 47 H. 271, 386 P.2d 880.

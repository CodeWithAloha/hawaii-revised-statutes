HRS 0635-0032 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

In case not capital, discretion of court to segregate jurors during trial. 9 H. 522. Failure to segregate in capital case (now life imprisonment) not error. 30 H. 697. Separation of jury and use of liquor, not prejudicial as matter of law. 45 H. 457, 370 P.2d 468. Review of nonevidentiary table posted in courtroom by juror coming out of jury room during deliberations. 49 H. 116, 121-126, 412 P.2d 662.

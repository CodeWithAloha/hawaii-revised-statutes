HRS 0657-0009 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Enforcement of valid and subsisting foreign judgment. 128 F. Supp. 697.

Where, pursuant to Philippine case law, relevant Philippine statutes of limitations were tolled as a matter of law, action was brought in this State within the time required under the relevant Philippine statutes of limitations as required by this section. 89 H. 91, 969 P.2d 1209.

§53-55 Urban redevelopment coordinator, office created. In any county which shall, by resolution adopted in the manner required by law for an ordinance, announce its intention to devise and adopt a "workable program" for urban renewal, pursuant to this chapter, the county may by ordinance create the office of urban renewal coordinator. [L 1955, c 271, pt of §1; RL 1955, §143-55; HRS §53-55]

§103-4 Expenditure from general appropriation unlawful, when. Whenever a specific appropriation is made for a particular object, it shall be unlawful to make any expenditure from a general appropriation which would otherwise cover such particular object. [L 1909, c 62, §5; RL 1925, §1481; RL 1935, §111; RL 1945, §368; RL 1955, §9-3; HRS §103-4]

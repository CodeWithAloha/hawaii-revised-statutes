§88-30 Actuary. The actuary shall be the technical adviser of the board of trustees on the matters regarding the operation of the funds of the system and shall perform such other duties as are required in connection therewith. [L 1925, c 55, §5(13); RL 1935, pt of §7924; RL 1945, pt of §707; RL 1955, §6-70; HRS §88-30; am L 1969, c 110, pt of §1]

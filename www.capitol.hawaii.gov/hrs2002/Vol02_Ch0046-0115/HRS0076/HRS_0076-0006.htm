§76-6 Chapter inoperative, when. If any provision of this chapter jeopardizes the receipt by the State or any county of any federal grant-in-aid or other federal allotment of money, the provision shall, insofar as the fund is jeopardized, be deemed to be inoperative. [L 1955, c 274, §12; RL 1955, §3-7; HRS §76-6; am L 2000, c 253, §7]

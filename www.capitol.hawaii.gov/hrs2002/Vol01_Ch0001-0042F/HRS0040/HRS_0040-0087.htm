[§40-87] Lapse of department of education appropriations. [Repeal on June 30, 1998 extended to June 30, 2005. L 1998, c 115, §24.] Upon the lapse of an appropriation to the department of education, moneys which remain unencumbered shall be returned to the state treasury within ninety days. [L 1986, c 321, §3]

HRS 0806-0031 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Allegation that accused gave bribe to officer with intent to influence officer in discharge of duty, alleges by inference that accused had knowledge of official character of officer and is sufficient. 23 H. 616, 618; 25 H. 747.

Leading, conducting, driving a person to place where prostitution is being carried on. 37 H. 586.

Nature and cause of accusation could not be understood by person of common understanding from a reading of the complaint itself, where generic term "felony" did not, indirectly or by inference, inform defendant that underlying felony was terroristic threatening in first degree. 78 H. 66, 890 P.2d 303.

Cited: 23 H. 476, 484, explained 49 H. 160, 185, 413 P.2d 221; 23 H. 546, 549.

Referred to: 43 H. 54, 58.

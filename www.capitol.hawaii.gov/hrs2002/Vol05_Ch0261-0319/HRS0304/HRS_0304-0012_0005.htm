[§304-12.5] Graduate assistants; scholarship program. The University of Hawaii shall grant scholarships, to graduate students who teach, conduct laboratory sessions, or perform any other type of instructional or research work for the University of Hawaii. The scholarships may be used to provide full or partial coverage of tuition and other fees. Scholarships awarded under this section shall be in accordance with rules adopted by the board of regents.

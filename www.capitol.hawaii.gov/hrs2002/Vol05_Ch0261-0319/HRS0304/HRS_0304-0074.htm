§304-74 Board to act as agency. The board of regents, University of Hawaii, is designated and authorized to serve as the agency of this State to formulate, submit to the Secretary of Agriculture of the United States and administer plans, hereinafter termed "agricultural plans", pursuant to section 7 of the Soil Conservation and Domestic Allotment Act. [L 1937, c 226, §2; RL 1945, pt of §1971; RL 1955, §44-53; HRS §304-74]

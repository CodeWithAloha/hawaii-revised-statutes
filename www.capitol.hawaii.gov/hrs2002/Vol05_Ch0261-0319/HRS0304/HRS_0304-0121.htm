[§304-121] Medical education special fund. There is established within the state treasury a special fund to be known as the medical education special fund, into which shall be deposited all funds received by the medical education council, including:

(1) Moneys from the federal Centers for Medicaid and Medicare Services or other federal agencies;

(2) State appropriations; and

(3) Grants, contracts, donations, or private contributions.

The fund shall be administered by the council. Moneys in the fund shall be expended by the council in accordance with this part. [L 2003, c 181, pt of §1]

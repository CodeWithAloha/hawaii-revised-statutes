[§304-28] Tuberculosis clearance certification; exemption. Notwithstanding any law or rule to the contrary, a person wishing to enroll at a campus within the University of Hawaii system solely to participate in a non-credit, short-term course lasting less than fifteen days, shall not be required to present, as a condition to enrollment or otherwise, a tuberculosis clearance certification. [L 1995, c 62, §1]

PART II. SPECIAL DIVISIONS, PROGRAMS, AND DEPARTMENTS

A. Aquarium and Marine Laboratory

[§304-30] State aquarium. (a) The Waikiki aquarium is established and designated as the official state aquarium.

(b) The University of Hawaii may contract with a private nonprofit entity for the operation and management of the state aquarium; provided that the entity is a private nonprofit corporation established solely to manage and improve the exhibits and facilities; conduct education, research, volunteer, fund-raising, and membership programs; and operate concessions of the state aquarium.

(c) The status conferred by subsections (a) and (b) shall not impinge on the ownership of land and improvements which shall remain with the University of Hawaii. All board of regents' policies regarding the use of University of Hawaii facilities shall apply to the state aquarium. [L 1995, c 184, §2]

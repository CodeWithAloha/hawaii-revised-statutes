[§302A-465] Compliance report. By December 31, 2000, the superintendent of education shall submit to the legislature and the advisory commission on gender equity in sports a report of compliance with Public Law 92-318, Title IX, of the federal Education Amendments of 1972, including a compliance plan with timelines for every public high school, an analysis and assessment of current activities with respect to Title IX compliance, and itemized expenditures for athletics. [L 2000, c 229, pt of §1]

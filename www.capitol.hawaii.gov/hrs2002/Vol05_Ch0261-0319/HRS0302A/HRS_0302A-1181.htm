[D.] New Century Charter Schools

§302A-1181 New century charter schools; definitions. (a) All schools previously designated as student-centered schools shall be designated as new century charter schools.

[(b)] For the purposes of this subpart:

"Executive director" means the chief executive officer of the charter school administrative office established under section 302A-1187. [L 1999, c 62, pt of §2; am L 2003, c 203, §1]

[§302A-1150] Use of school buildings. The fullest freedom shall be given to citizens of the State to use for lawful purposes all public school buildings throughout the State during the hours the structures are not in use for strictly educational purposes; provided that the person vested with the proper authority over the building shall issue a permit to the applicant, when the proposed use is shown to be lawful by the applicant. [L 1996, c 89, pt of §2]

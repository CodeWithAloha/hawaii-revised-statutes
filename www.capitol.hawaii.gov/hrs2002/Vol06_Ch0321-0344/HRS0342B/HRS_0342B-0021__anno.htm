HRS 0342B-0021 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Prior law.

Administrative rules must be adopted pursuant to chapter 91 regarding hydrogen sulfide emissions prior to issuance of air pollution permit authorizing construction and operation of geothermal wells. 73 H. 56, 828 P.2d 802.

Where health department did not have rules adopted under chapter 91 governing the standards of emissions of hydrogen sulfide into the air as required by §342B-32 (1991) at the time geothermal well developer was issued permit, department was required to refuse the issuance of the permit. 73 H. 56, 828 P.2d 802.

PART III. FROZEN FOOD PRODUCTS

§328-61 Notifying consumer. No person shall sell or offer to sell any uncooked thawed food which substantially resembles the physical appearances of the fresh food without a label, as hereinafter described, notifying the purchaser that such food had been frozen and then thawed out; provided that this section shall not apply if the thawed foods are processed by grinding or have been dehydrated before being offered for retail sale. [L 1967, c 206, pt of §1; HRS §328-61; am L 1980, c 40, §2]

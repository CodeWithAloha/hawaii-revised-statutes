Dumping of garbage at sea. 283 U.S. 473.

Does not authorize board to destroy what is not a nuisance or to declare that to be which is not in fact a nuisance. 14 H. 533, 537. Courts will review board's actions when taken without notice to owner. 22 H. 327, 345.

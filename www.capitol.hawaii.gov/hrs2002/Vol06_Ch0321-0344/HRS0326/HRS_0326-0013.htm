§326-13 Expenses; rules. The department of health shall bear all expenses of travel and other necessary expenses incurred under sections 326-1 to 326-14; and may prescribe all rules, regulations, and forms and perform all acts necessary and proper for carrying out their provisions. [L 1909, c 81, §8; RL 1925, §1190; RL 1935, §1152; RL 1945, §2414; RL 1955, §50-15; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §19; HRS §326-13]

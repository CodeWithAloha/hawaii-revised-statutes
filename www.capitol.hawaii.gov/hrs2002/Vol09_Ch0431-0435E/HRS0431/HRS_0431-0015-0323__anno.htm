HRS 0431-0015-0323 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

As proceedings under this section are not in the nature of assumpsit, trial court did not err in denying insurance commissioner attorneys' fees under §607-14 in suit against customer of liquidated mutual benefit society. 99 H. 53, 52 P.3d 823.

Nothing in the language of §478-3 or this section precluded an award of post-judgment interest to insurance commissioner upon final judgment awarding commissioner the unpaid premiums from customer of liquidated mutual benefit society. 99 H. 53, 52 P.3d 823.

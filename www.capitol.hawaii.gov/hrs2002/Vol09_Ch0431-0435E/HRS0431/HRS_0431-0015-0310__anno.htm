HRS 0431-0015-0310 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Under §431:15-313 and this section, the commissioner has exclusive standing under this article to assert claims arising out of the liquidation or rehabilitation of an insurance company on behalf of not only the insolvent insurer, but also its policyholders, creditors, and all other interested parties. 89 H. 427, 974 P.2d 1017.

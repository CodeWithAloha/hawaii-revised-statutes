§431:8-313 Surplus lines broker's annual statement. (a) Each surplus lines broker shall file with the commissioner on or before March 15 of each year a verified statement of all surplus lines insurance transacted during the preceding calendar year.

(b) The statement shall be on forms as prescribed and furnished by the commissioner and shall show:

(1) Gross amount of premiums for each kind of insurance transacted;

(2) Aggregate gross premiums charged;

(3) Aggregate of returned premiums paid to insureds;

(4) Aggregate of net premiums;

(5) Amount of aggregate tax remitted; and

(6) Additional information as required by the commissioner. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2; am L 2003, c 212, §47]

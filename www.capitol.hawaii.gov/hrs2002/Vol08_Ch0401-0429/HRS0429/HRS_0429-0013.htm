[§429-13] Summons and complaint; service on whom. In an action or proceeding against a nonprofit association, a summons and complaint shall be served on the agent authorized by appointment to receive service of process, the officer, managing or general agent, or the person authorized to participate in the management of its affairs. If none of them can be served, service may be made on a member. [L 1999, c 161, pt of §1]

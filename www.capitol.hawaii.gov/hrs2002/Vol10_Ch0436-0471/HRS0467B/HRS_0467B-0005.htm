§467B-5 Records to be kept. Every charitable organization, professional fund-raising counsel, and professional solicitor subject to this chapter shall keep true and accurate records as to its activities in a form that will accurately provide support for the information required by this chapter. Upon demand, the records shall be made available to the director for inspection. The records shall be retained for a period of not less than five years. [L 1969, c 167, pt of §1; am L 1971, c 162, §3; am L 1990, c 129, §4; am L 1993, c 206, §5]

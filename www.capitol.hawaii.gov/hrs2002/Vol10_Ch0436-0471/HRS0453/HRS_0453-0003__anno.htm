HRS 0453-0003 ANNOTATIONS

Attorney General Opinions

"Direction" of physician under prior law construed. Att. Gen. Op. 63-55.

Case Notes

Where a physician without a Hawaii license is authorized under paragraph (3) to practice under the direction of a licensed physician, the absence of a license is immaterial on the question of negligence in the treatment of a patient. 53 H. 526, 497 P.2d 1354.

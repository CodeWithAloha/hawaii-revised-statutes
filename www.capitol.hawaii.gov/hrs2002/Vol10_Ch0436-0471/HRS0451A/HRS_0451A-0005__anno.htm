HRS 0451A-0005 ANNOTATIONS

Note

L 1997, c 88, §16 provides:

"SECTION 16. All rules, guidelines, and other material adopted or developed by the board of hearing aid dealers and fitters shall remain in full force and effect until amended or repealed by the department of commerce and consumer affairs pursuant to chapter 91, Hawaii Revised Statutes. In the interim, every reference to the board of hearing aid dealers and fitters in those rules, policies, procedures, guidelines, and other material is amended to refer to the director of commerce and consumer affairs as appropriate."

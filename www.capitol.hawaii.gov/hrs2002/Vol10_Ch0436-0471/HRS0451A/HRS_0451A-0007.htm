§451A-7 Issuance of license. The director shall license each applicant who satisfactorily meets the experience requirements and who passes an examination as provided in section 451A-2. Upon the payment of application, examination, and license fees, the director shall issue to the applicant a license. [L 1969, c 205, pt of §1; am L 1984, c 7, §30; am L 1997, c 88, §6]

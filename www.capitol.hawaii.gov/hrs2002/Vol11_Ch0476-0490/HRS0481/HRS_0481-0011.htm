§481-11 Remedies cumulative. The remedies prescribed in this part are cumulative and in addition to the remedies prescribed in chapter 269 for discriminations by public utilities. If any conflict arises between this part and chapter 269, the latter prevails. [L 1937, c 223, pt of §12; RL 1945, §9332; am L 1955, c 241, §1(g); RL 1955, §205-13; HRS §481-11]

§485-24 Delivery of prospectus to purchaser. No sale or contract of sale of any security required to be registered by qualification shall be concluded unless prior to the conclusion thereof a copy of a prospectus meeting the requirements of paragraph (3) or (4) of subsection (b) of section 485-10 is delivered to the purchaser thereof. [L 1957, c 314, pt of §1; Supp, §199-19.5; HRS §485-24; am L 1984, c 96, §7]

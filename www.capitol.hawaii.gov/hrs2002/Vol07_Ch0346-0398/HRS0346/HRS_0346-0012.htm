§346-12 Fair hearing. An applicant or recipient, deeming oneself aggrieved, shall be entitled to appeal to the director of human services in the manner prescribed by department of human services regulations and shall be afforded reasonable notice and opportunity for a fair hearing. [L 1941, c 296, §1(8); RL 1945, §4808; RL 1955, §107-8; HRS §346-12; am L 1970, c 105, §5; gen ch 1985; am L 1987, c 339, §4]

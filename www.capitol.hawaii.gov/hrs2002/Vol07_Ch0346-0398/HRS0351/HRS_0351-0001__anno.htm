HRS 0351-0001 ANNOTATIONS

Attorney General Opinions

No award to someone who suffers psychological or physical trauma in reaction to the death of a crime victim. Att. Gen. Op. 88-2.

Case Notes

In light of its purpose, chapter should be construed liberally in determining eligibility of applicants. 55 H. 514, 523 P.2d 311; 63 H. 254, 625 P.2d 372.

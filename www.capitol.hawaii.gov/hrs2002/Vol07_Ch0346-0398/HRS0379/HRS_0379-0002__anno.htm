HRS 0379-0002 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Plaintiff had standing to seek declaratory judgment that all [paragraphs] of this [section] were unconstitutional or preempted, where, inter alia, plaintiff's interests were adversely impacted by the application of paragraphs (1) and (3), since plaintiff could be prosecuted under Hawaii law for criminal conspiracy under these paragraphs. 82 F. Supp. 2d 1151, amended, 103 F. Supp. 2d 1233.

Section unenforceable as it applies to private employers covered by National Labor Relations Act (NLRA) and private third party employment or recruitment agencies in their dealings with employers covered by NLRA. 82 F. Supp. 2d 1151, amended, 103 F. Supp. 2d 1233.

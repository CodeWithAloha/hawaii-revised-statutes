HRS 0502-0042 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Policy of law is to uphold certificates of acknowledgment when substance is found. 15 H. 570, 573; 16 H. 294, 299; 18 H. 179, 184.

Deed valid as between parties, even if not properly acknowledged. 49 H. 62, 63, 412 P.2d 326. See 2 H. 161, 163; 17 H. 56, 58.

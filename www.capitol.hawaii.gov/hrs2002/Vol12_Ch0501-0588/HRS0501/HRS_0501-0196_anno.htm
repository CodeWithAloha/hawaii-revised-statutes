HRS 0501-0196 ANNOTATIONS

Rules of Court

Alteration, see RLC rule 65.

Case Notes

Power to cancel memorandum. 32 H. 680.

Notice of marital status may be given under this section. 35 H. 816, 825.

Land court's jurisdiction under section is in personam as to all interested parties properly served. 5 H. App. 304, 689 P.2d 204.

Respondent's filing came within the purview of this statute, respondent having filed its petition for one of the purposes listed under rule 65 of the rules of the land court, and pursuant to this statute, notice to all parties in interest was required to be given; proposed amendment of their certificates would make registered owners "parties in interest" entitled to notice under this statute. 79 H. 56 (App.), 897 P.2d 983.

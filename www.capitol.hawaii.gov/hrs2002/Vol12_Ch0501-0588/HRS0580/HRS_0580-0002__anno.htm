HRS 0580-0002 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Insane person may file for divorce through guardian. 9 H. 475.

Great particularity not required in prayer for alimony. 14 H. 152. Except under certain circumstances amended libel should be served within reasonable time. 9 H. 405. Service of amended pleading if amendment made in open court before opposing counsel may be waived. Id. When decree vacated because thirty days has not elapsed, court should re-try the case after such lapse, upon evidence then adduced. 23 H. 451, 456. Setting decree aside. 34 H. 675.

Discontinuance, right. 41 H. 481.

Cited: 3 H. 300, 301; 17 H. 463, 464; 20 H. 633, 637; 24 H. 29, 34.

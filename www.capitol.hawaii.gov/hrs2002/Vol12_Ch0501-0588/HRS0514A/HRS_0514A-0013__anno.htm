HRS 0514A-0013 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Condominium owner has duty to warn lessee of a known hazardous condition in the condominium's common area. 69 H. 530, 750 P.2d 929.

Conversion of a common element to a limited common element required the consent of all the apartment owners. 70 H. 469, 776 P.2d 393.

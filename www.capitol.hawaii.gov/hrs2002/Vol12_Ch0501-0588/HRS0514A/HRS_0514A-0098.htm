§514A-98 False statement. It shall be unlawful for any person or person's agents to testify before or file with the commission any notice, statement, application, or other document required under this chapter that is false or untrue or contains any material misstatement of fact, or contains forgery. In addition to any sanctions or remedies as provided in this chapter, any violation of this section shall constitute a misdemeanor. [L 1989, c 297, pt of §3; am L 1996, c 106, §8]

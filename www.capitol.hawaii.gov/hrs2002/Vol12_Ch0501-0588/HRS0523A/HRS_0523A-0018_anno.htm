HRS 0523A-0018 ANNOTATIONS

Attorney General Opinions

Notices for persons in all counties may be published in The Honolulu Advertiser. Att. Gen. Op. 85-20.

Act 214, L 1996, which amended Hawaii's unclaimed property law to permit escheat of abandoned property, was constitutional; notice of custodial taking under this section to include notice that, if property remained unclaimed for statutory period, it may escheat to State under §523A-3.5, recommended. Att. Gen. Op. 98-4.

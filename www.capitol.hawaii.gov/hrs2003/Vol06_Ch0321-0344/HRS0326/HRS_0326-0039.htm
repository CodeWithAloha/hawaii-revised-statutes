§326-39 Penalty. Any person violating this chapter, or any rule or regulation of the department of health relating thereto, shall be deemed guilty of a misdemeanor. Except as herein otherwise provided, the punishment therefor shall be the same as provided by section 321-18. [L 1951, c 157, §19; RL 1955, §59-40; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §19; HRS §326-39]

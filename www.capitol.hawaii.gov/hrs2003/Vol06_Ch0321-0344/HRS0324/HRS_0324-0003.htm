§324-3 Legal proceedings; information excluded from. Any findings, conclusions, or summaries resulting from medical studies within the scope of this part shall not be used or made available in any legal proceeding. Any information set forth in section 324-1 provided to any research or study committee shall not be used or made available in any legal proceeding unless it is unobtainable from the original source. In such event, the judicial officer shall in camera inspect the committee's findings, conclusions, or summaries and make available factual information contained therein. [L 1963, c 109, §3; Supp, §48B-3; HRS §324-3]

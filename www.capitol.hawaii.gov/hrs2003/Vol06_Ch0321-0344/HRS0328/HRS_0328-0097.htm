[§328-97] Posting requirements. Every pharmacy shall prominently display, in clear and unobstructed public view, a sign in block letters which shall read: "HAWAII LAW REQUIRES THAT LESS EXPENSIVE GENERICALLY EQUIVALENT DRUG PRODUCTS BE OFFERED TO THE CONSUMER. CONSULT YOUR PHYSICIAN AND PHARMACIST CONCERNING THE AVAILABILITY OF THE LEAST EXPENSIVE DRUG PRODUCT FOR YOUR USE." The letters must be at least one inch in height. [L 1980, c 187, pt of §1]

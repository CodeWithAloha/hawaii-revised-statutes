§76-103 Veteran's preference. The extent to which veteran's preference shall be given to veterans, to disabled veterans, to spouses of disabled veterans, and to surviving spouses of deceased servicemen who have not remarried shall be provided by rules and regulations. [L 1955, c 274, pt of §1; RL 1955, §3-81; am L 1967, c 279, §1; HRS §76-103; am L 1973, c 177, §1(3)]

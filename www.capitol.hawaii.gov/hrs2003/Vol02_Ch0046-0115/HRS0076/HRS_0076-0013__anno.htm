Attorney General Opinions

Only the director of personnel services and the administrative director of the courts are empowered to act on reallocation requests. Att. Gen. Op. 81-10.

Effective date of reallocation determines whether retroactive pay adjustment made. Att. Gen. Op. 85-4.

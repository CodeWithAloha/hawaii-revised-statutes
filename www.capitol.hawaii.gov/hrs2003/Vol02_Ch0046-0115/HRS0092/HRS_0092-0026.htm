§92-26 Fees; exemption. One department of the state government shall not be required to pay any fee to any other department of the state government for the preparation and certification by the latter of any government record, nor shall section 92-21 be held to amend or repeal section 94-4. [L 1921, c 96, §2; RL 1925, §167; RL 1935, §148; RL 1945, §459; RL 1955, §7-3; HRS §92-26; am L 1991, c 145, §3]

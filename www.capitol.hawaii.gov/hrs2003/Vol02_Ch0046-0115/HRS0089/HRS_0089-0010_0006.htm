§89-10.6 School/community-based management waiver. A school or a learning support center participating in the school/community-based management program may initiate a waiver from policies, rules, or procedures, including collective bargaining agreements, as provided for in section 302A-1126. [L 1993, c 364, pt of §11; am L 1994, c 272, §23; am L 1996, c 89, §6]

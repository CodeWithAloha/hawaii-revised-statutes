§46-44 Destruction of paid or cancelled warrants, bonds and interest coupons. The director of finance of each county with the approval of the legislative body and the legal advisor of such county, may authorize the destruction by burning, machine shredding, chemical disintegration, or any acceptable method of disposal of (1) all warrants of the county which have been paid and which bear any date ten years prior to the date of destruction, and (2) all bonds and interest coupons of the county which have been cancelled or paid and which bear any date two years prior to the date of destruction. The director of finance shall submit such reports on the destruction as may be required by the legislative body. [L 1947, c 146, pt of §1; RL 1955, §138-9; am L 1963, c 35, §1; HRS §46-44; am L 1971, c 156, §1]

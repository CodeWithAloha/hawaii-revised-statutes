§88-285 Service connected disability retirement. A member who would be eligible to receive a service connected disability retirement allowance pursuant to section 88-79 shall receive a maximum retirement allowance of one and one-fourth per cent of the average final compensation multiplied by the number of years of credited service unreduced for age, but not less than fifteen per cent of average final compensation. [L 1984, c 108, pt of §8; am L 2001, c 101, §5]

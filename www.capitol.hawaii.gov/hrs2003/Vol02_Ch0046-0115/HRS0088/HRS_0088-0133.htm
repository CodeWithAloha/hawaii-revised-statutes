§88-133 Benefits and conditions applicable to service member. All service members shall by reason of their retention of membership in the system under the Servicemen's Act, have the status, be entitled to the benefits, and be subject to the conditions and limitations set forth in sections 88-131 to 88-142. [L 1951, c 262, pt of §3; RL 1955, §6-112; HRS §88-123; am L 1969, c 110, pt of §1]

§466J-9 Violations of chapter; penalties. It shall be a misdemeanor for any person, including any corporation, association, or individual to:

(1) Sell or fraudulently obtain or furnish any radiographer's, radiation therapist's, or nuclear medicine technologist's diploma, license, renewal, or record or aid or abet therein;

(2) Use in connection with the person's name any designation tending to imply that the person is a certified radiographer, a certified radiation therapist, or certified nuclear medicine technologist unless licensed to practice under this chapter;

(3) Practice radiologic technology during the time the person's license issued under this chapter is suspended or revoked; or

(4) Violate this chapter. [L 1974, c 253, pt of §2; am L 1980, c 128, pt of §1; am L 1990, c 273, §11; am L 1995, c 143, §8]

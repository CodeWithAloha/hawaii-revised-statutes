HRS 0467-0014 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Indicates no exception for transactions in which broker acts as middleman. 68 H. 371, 715 P.2d 805.

Provision authorizing commission to revoke or suspend license for fee-splitting does not authorize a private right of action for recovery of sales commission paid or to bar a claim for services rendered. 1 H. App. 448, 620 P.2d 750.

Cited: 6 H. App. 188, 716 P.2d 163.

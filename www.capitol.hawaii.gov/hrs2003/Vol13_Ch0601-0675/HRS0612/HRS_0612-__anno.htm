The Prosecutor's Duty to Disclose Exculpatory Evidence to the Grand Jury: Did the Hawaii Supreme Court Retreat from Fundamental Fairness? 2 UH L. Rev. 145.

Case Notes

Trial court's reassigning from the jury venire to other trials occurring that day potential jurors who admitted having knowledge about the case did not result in a "substantial failure to comply" with the provisions of this chapter and did not prejudice defendant. 98 H. 1, 41 P.3d 157.

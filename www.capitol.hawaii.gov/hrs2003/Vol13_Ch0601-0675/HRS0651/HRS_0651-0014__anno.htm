Rules of Court

Case Notes

Compensation for services of receivers, rests in discretion of court. 8 H. 740.

Under court authorization, receiver can complete a construction project begun by a corporation before insolvency and the costs will be chargeable to the property specifically benefited. 57 H. 321, 555 P.2d 864.

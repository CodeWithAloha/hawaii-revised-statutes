HRS 0603-0023 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

City may bring action for mandatory injunction to remove structure that is a nuisance. 45 H. 232, 364 P.2d 646.

Continuing operation of a private school without license required by law is per se an irreparable injury to public policy. 65 H. 289, 651 P.2d 473.

Allows courts to enjoin public nuisance; does not extend equity jurisdiction to enforcement of criminal laws. 5 H. App. 463, 701 P.2d 175.

Mentioned: 52 H. 427, 478 P.2d 320.

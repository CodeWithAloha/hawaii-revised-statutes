HRS 0657-0004 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Claim for defamation accrues when defamee discovers or reasonably should have discovered publication. 65 H. 478, 653 P.2d 1155.

Distinguished from "general" personal injury statute of limitations of §657-7. 73 H. 578, 837 P.2d 1247.

Cited: 42 H. 177, 179.

Mentioned: 817 F. Supp. 850.

HRS 0668-0002 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Petition to partition equitable title allowed without joining trustees who held legal title under trust for execution of mortgage, since discharged. 10 H. 487. State not "person" having interest; no waiver of sovereign immunity. 48 H. 92, 96, 395 P.2d 620.

Intervening minor. 30 H. 323.

Unborn heirs. 31 H. 517.

Cited: 35 H. 262, 292.

§353-22.6 Victim restitution. The director of public safety shall enforce victim restitution orders against moneys earned by the prisoner while incarcerated. The amount deducted and paid once annually to the victim shall be ten per cent of the prisoner's annual earnings. This section shall not apply to moneys earned on work furlough pursuant to section 353-17. [L 1987, c 127, §1 and c 338, §10; am L 1988, c 141, §32; am L 1989, c 211, §8]

§386-122 Notice of insurance. If the insurance so effected is under section 386-121(a)(1), the employer shall file with the director in a form prescribed by the director a notice of the employer's insurance together with a statement of benefits provided by the policy of insurance. [L 1963, c 116, pt of §1; Supp, §97-121; HRS §386-122; gen ch 1985; am L 1986, c 304, §4; am L 1988, c 35, §1]

[§346-315] Nonparticipating manufacturers. If the department and a drug manufacturer fail to reach agreement on the terms of a rebate, the department shall conduct a review of whether to place that manufacturer's products on the prior authorization list or formularies for the state medicaid program in accordance with this chapter. The department may release the names of manufacturers that do not enter into rebate agreements. This information shall be deemed public information. The department may also provide to doctors, pharmacists, and other health professionals information about the relative cost of drugs produced by manufacturers that enter into rebate agreements compared to the cost of drugs produced by those that do not enter into rebate agreements. The department shall adopt rules under chapter 91 creating procedures for the implementation of this section. [L 2002, c 76, pt of §1]

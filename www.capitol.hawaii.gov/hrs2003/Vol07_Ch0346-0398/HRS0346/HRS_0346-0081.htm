PART IV. SERVICES TO ADULTS

[A. Day Care Center]

§346-81 Purpose. The purpose of this subpart is to establish the nature and type of services to elders, disabled, and aged who are qualified to receive social services according to standards and conditions prescribed by the department of human services. [L 1972, c 198, pt of §1; am L 1987, c 339, §4; am L 1990, c 67, §8]

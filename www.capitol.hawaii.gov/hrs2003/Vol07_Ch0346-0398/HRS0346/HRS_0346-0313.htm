[§346-313] Program eligibility. (a) All residents of the State shall be eligible to participate in the Rx program.

(b) The department:

(1) Shall establish procedures for determining eligibility and shall issue program enrollment cards to eligible residents;

(2) Shall undertake outreach efforts to build public awareness of the program and maximize the enrollment of eligible residents; and

(3) May adjust the requirements and terms of the program by rule to accommodate any federally funded prescription drug program. [L 2002, c 76, pt of §1]

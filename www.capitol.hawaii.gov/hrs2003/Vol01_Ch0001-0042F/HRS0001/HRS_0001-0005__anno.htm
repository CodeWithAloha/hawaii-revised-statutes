Contract for services by a public official within the scope of his official duties, void. 24 H. 540. Ultra vires agreement by public officer to be distinguished from one contrary to public policy, former being enforceable against one who has received benefits. 19 H. 41.

No recovery on sales contract made without taking out license. 8 H. 746 (Prior to annexation). No recovery on note given for liquor sold without license. 24 H. 263.

Illegal consideration for indivisible note voids note. 24 H. 263. Accord: 24 H. 540.

Illegal transaction, action for money had and received, whether parties in pari delicto. 21 H. 513; 22 H. 759.

Provision as to waiver applied. 5 H. 300, 304; 11 H. 428, 429; 12 H. 262, 264. But see as to appeals, jurisdictional requisites, note appended to chapter 641.

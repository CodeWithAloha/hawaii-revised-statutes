§15-6 Return envelope, ballot envelope; instructions. The clerk shall provide the absentee voter with the ballots, ballot envelopes, and a return envelope which shall contain a statement to be subscribed to by the voter which affirms the fact that the voter is the person voting, the instruction that the voter's ballot will be valid only if the affirmation statement is signed, and any other information prescribed by the rules promulgated by the chief election officer. [L 1975, c 36, pt of §3; am L 1980, c 248, §1(d); gen ch 1985]

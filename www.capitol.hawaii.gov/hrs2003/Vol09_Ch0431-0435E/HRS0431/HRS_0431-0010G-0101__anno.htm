HRS 0431-0010G-0101 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

See also notes at end of this chapter.

Decisions under prior law.

Coverage denied to uninsured motorcyclist claiming underinsured motorist benefits from a family member's policy where motor vehicle insurance did not expressly provide coverage for motorcycles; distinction between motorcycles and motor vehicles under no-fault laws discussed. 72 H. 205, 811 P.2d 473.

Case Notes

The plain language of this chapter as well as its legislative history disqualified plaintiff, who was injured by someone operating a motorcycle, from no-fault benefits. 91 H. 299 (App.), 983 P.2d 200.

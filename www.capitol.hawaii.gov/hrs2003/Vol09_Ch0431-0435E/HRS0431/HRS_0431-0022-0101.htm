[ARTICLE 22]

LOSS MITIGATION GRANT PROGRAM

[§431:22-101] Definitions. As used in this article:

"Commissioner" means the insurance commissioner.

"Loss mitigation" means actions undertaken to reduce losses that may result from a hazard.

"Wind resistive devices" means devices and techniques, as identified and determined in accordance with section 431:22-104(b), that increase a building's or structure's resistance to damage from wind forces. [L 2002, c 179, pt of §3]

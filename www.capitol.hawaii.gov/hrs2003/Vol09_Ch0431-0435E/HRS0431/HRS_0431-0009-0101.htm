ARTICLE 9

LICENSING OF ADJUSTERS AND BILL REVIEWERS

PART I. GENERAL PROVISIONS

§431:9-101 Scope. This article shall govern the qualifications and procedures for granting licenses to all insurance adjusters, independent bill reviewers, and limited service representatives. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2; am L 2000, c 288, §6; am L 2001, c 216, §7]

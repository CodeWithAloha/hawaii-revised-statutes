ARTICLE 10D

LIFE INSURANCE AND ANNUITIES

PART I. INDIVIDUAL LIFE INSURANCE, ANNUITIES

AND PURE ENDOWMENT CONTRACTS

§431:10D-101 Scope. This part applies to contracts of life insurance and annuities other than group life insurance, group annuities and except as provided in section 431:10D-302, other than industrial life insurance. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2]

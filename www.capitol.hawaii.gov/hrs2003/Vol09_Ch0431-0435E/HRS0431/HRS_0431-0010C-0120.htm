§431:10C-120 Prohibitions, penalty. (a) No insurer shall issue or offer to issue any policy which the insurer represents is a motor vehicle insurance policy unless such insurer meets the requirements of this article.

(b) Any insurer, any producer, or any representative of an insurer who violates subsection (a) shall be subject to section 431:10C-117. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2; am L 1989, c 195, §34; am L 1997, c 251, §30; am L 1998, c 275, §11; am L 2001, c 216, §19]

[§431:14F-110] Information to be furnished enrollees; hearings and appeals of enrollees. Every managed care plan which makes its own rates, within a reasonable time after receiving written request therefor and upon payment of such reasonable charges as it may make, shall furnish to any enrollee affected by a rate made by it or to the authorized representative of the enrollee, all pertinent information as to the rate. [L 2002, c 74 pt of §2]

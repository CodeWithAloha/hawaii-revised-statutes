[§488-7] Failure to comply; penalty. Any plan which neglects or refuses to comply with this chapter shall be notified in writing by the director of commerce and consumer affairs of the neglect or refusal and to take corrective action; if the neglect or refusal continues for seven days after notification, the plan, group, or plan administrator may be fined not more than $1,000. Every day's neglect or refusal after the expiration of seven days shall be a separate offense. [L 1976, c 156, pt of §1; am L 1982, c 204, §8; am L 1983, c 124, §17]

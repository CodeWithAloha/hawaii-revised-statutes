HRS 0486K-0008 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Permits hotel to remove hotel guest's personal belongings from hotel room and to change the room's doorlock so as to prevent hotel guest from re-entering the room without hotel's permission, upon the expiration of the license to occupy mutually agreed to by hotel guest and hotel. 10 H. App. 123, 861 P.2d 736.

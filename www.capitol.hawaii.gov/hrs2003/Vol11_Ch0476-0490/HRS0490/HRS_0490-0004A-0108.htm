§490:4A-108 Exclusion of consumer transactions governed by federal law. This Article does not apply to a funds transfer any part of which is governed by the Electronic Fund Transfer Act of 1978 (Title XX, Public Law 95-630, 92 Stat. 3728, 15 U.S.C. §1693 et seq.) as amended from time to time. [L 1991, c 41, pt of §1]

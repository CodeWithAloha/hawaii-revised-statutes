§833-4 Costs and attorney fees. All costs shall be paid by the designated agent and reasonable attorneys fees shall be awarded to the person whose removal is sought, if an order authorizing the person's return to the custody of the demanding court, judge or magistrate is not issued. [L 1971, c 115, §4; HRS §713C-4; ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1; gen ch 1985]

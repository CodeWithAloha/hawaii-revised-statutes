HRS 0710-1016_0009 ANNOTATIONS

COMMENTARY ON §§710-1016.6 TO 1016.9

Act 130, Session Laws 1987, was enacted by the legislature because it was concerned about the increase in the incidence of police impersonators. The legislature believed it was important to be able to prosecute people who intend to deceive others by impersonating law enforcement officers. Senate Conference Committee Report No. 85, House Conference Committee Report No. 60.

HRS 0801-0004 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Costs as part of sentence. 6 H. 732, 737.

On appeal accrued costs must be paid. 18 H. 341.

Costs can only be taxed as part of punishment, not separately. 23 H. 766, 769.

Where costs are ordered paid on conviction they must be taken into account in imposing maximum penalty. 38 H. 258.

Case Notes

Signed by foreperson. 16 H. 743.

Not sufficient ground to quash indictment that defendant was subpoenaed before grand jury, when it appears defendant was informed of defendant's right to refuse to answer questions the answers to which might incriminate defendant. 24 H. 621.

Examination of wife of accused by grand jury, effect. 45 H. 221, 365 P.2d 202.

Indictment may be based on hearsay under certain circumstances. 53 H. 513, 497 P.2d 559.

There is presumption that indictment was based on sufficient evidence, and burden is on one who asserts that it was not. 53 H. 513, 497 P.2d 559.

Effect of incompetent evidence on validity of indictment where there are other legal and competent evidence. 58 H. 474, 572 P.2d 497.

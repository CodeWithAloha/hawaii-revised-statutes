Case Notes

Since this section provides chief of police or designee with independent authority and discretion to admit persons charged with misdemeanor offenses to bail in accordance with §804-9, senior judge of family court lacked authority to issue a bail schedule divesting police of that authority and discretion. 75 H. 357, 861 P.2d 1205.

See 18 H. 500.

Prior law.

Where accused, lawfully arrested, procures accused discharge by voluntarily making deposit of cash in lieu of bail without statutory authority, accused release is unlawful; accused cannot recover same after it has been paid into treasury. 22 H. 759.

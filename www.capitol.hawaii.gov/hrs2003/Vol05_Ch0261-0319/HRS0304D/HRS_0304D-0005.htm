[§304D-5] Center for nursing special fund. There is established in the treasury of the State a center for nursing special fund into which shall be deposited any legislative appropriations, federal or private grants, and any other funds collected for the purposes of this chapter. The fund shall be administered by the board of regents of the University of Hawaii, and moneys in the fund shall be expended to support the center's activities. [L 2003, c 198, pt of §2]

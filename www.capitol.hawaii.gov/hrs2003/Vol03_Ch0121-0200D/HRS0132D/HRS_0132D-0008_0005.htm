[§132D-8.5] Importation of aerial common fireworks, special fireworks, or both, for public display. Aerial common fireworks, special fireworks, or both, shall only be imported and stored, if necessary, in an amount sufficient for an anticipated three-month inventory; provided that if a licensee under section 132D-7 provides aerial common fireworks, special fireworks, or both, for public displays as allowed under section 132D-16 more than once a month, the licensee may import or store, if necessary, sufficient aerial common fireworks, special fireworks, or both, for a six-month inventory. [L 2000, c 233, §2]

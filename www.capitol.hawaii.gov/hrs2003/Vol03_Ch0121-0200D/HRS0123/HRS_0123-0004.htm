§123-4 Officers; appointment; term. The officers of the naval militia shall be appointed by the governor and shall consist of a commander, a lieutenant commander, a surgeon and a paymaster, each with rank of lieutenant, and such other officers as may be designated by the secretary of the navy. The term of commission shall be for four years. [L 1915, c 151, §4; RL 1925, §274; RL 1935, §7863; RL 1945, §13083; RL 1955, §354-4; HRS §123-4]

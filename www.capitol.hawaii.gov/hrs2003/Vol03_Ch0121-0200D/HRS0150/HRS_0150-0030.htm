§150-30 Disposition of fees and charges. All fees and charges received pursuant to this chapter shall be deposited with the state director of finance to the credit of the general fund. [L 1945, c 90, pt of §2; RL 1955, §26-14; am L 1963, c 114, §1 and c 193, §16; HRS §150-30; am L 1987, c 164, §12]

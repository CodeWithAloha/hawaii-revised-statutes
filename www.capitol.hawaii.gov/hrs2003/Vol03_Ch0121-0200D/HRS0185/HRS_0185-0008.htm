§185-8 Government agencies to assist. All agencies of the State or its political subdivisions shall assist in extinguishing any fire when duly summoned by the administrator or any manager. [L 1953, c 100, §11; RL 1955, §19-59; HRS §185-9; ren and am L 1974, c 64, pt of §1; am L 1994, c 140, §8]

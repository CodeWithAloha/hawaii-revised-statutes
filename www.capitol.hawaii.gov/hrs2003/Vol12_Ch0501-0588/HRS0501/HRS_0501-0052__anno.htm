HRS 0501-0052 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Jurisdiction limited, power to register title of applicant only. 24 H. 298; 29 H. 815, 816; 35 H. 254; 37 H. 74, 95, aff'd 158 F.2d 122.

Issuance of new certificate to reflect acquisition by condemnation may be compelled. 31 H. 781, aff'd 61 F.2d 896.

Court may determine validity of deed though it cannot reform it. 20 H. 355, 357.

Construction of will in land court, see 29 H. 815.

§533-8 Widow's right to remain in husband's house. A widow may remain in the house of her husband sixty days after his death, without being chargeable with rent therefor. In the meantime she shall have her reasonable sustenance out of his estate. [CC 1859, §1306; RL 1925, §3024; RL 1935, §4837; RL 1945, §12107; RL 1955, §319-8; HRS §533-8]

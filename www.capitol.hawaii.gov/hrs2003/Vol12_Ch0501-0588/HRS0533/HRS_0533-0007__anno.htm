HRS 0533-0007 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Right of occupancy as including right to plant crops, see 5 H. 182.

Heir who objects to widow's occupancy under this section may bring ejectment. 17 H. 525, 527.

Before enactment of this section no right of occupancy before dower assigned, and where widow occupied her possession might be adverse. 4 H. 536, 538; 11 H. 755.

Effect of this section on doctrine that dowress not a tenant in common, see 21 H. 431, 434; compare 15 H. 284, 286.

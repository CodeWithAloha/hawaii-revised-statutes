§580-25 Inheritance by children. The children of such illegal marriage shall be entitled to succeed in the same manner as legitimate children, to all the real and personal estate of both parents in the State. [CC 1859, §1317; RL 1925, §2959; RL 1935, §4454; RL 1945, §12205; RL 1955, §324-5; HRS §580-25]

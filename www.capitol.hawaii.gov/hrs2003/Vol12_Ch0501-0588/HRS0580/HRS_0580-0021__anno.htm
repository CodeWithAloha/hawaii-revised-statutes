HRS 0580-0021 ANNOTATIONS

Rules of Court

Foreign law, see HFCR rule 99.

Attorney General Opinions

Uncle and niece by adoption not disqualified to intermarry. Att. Gen. Op. 62-49.

Case Notes

Marriage voidable, not void, of girl under fourteen. 6 H. 289. Nonassent of a party to marriage is not a statutory ground for annulment. 8 H. 77. Held that failure to procure license, ground for annulment. 25 H. 397, reversing 16 H. 377. Presumption that marriage was valid; burden on libellant to prove incompetency. 29 H. 716. Insufficiency of evidence on ground of nonage. 8 H. 360.

Requires proper allegation. 7 H. 219. Proper parties. 7 H. 278.

Enumeration of grounds does not deprive court of jurisdiction to annul a marriage on some other ground. 26 H. 89; 29 H. 770, 795.

§571-84.5 Support order, decree, judgment, or acknowledgment; social security number. The social security number of any individual who is a party to a divorce decree, or subject to a support order or paternity determination, or has made an acknowledgment of paternity issued under this chapter or chapter 576B, 580, or 584 shall be placed in the records relating to the matter. [L 1997, c 293, §5; am L 1998, c 11, §28]

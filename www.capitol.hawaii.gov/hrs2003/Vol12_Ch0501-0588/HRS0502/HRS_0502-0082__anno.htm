HRS 0502-0082 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Presumption of regularity raised by acknowledgment. 25 H. 470, 473; 49 H. 62, 73, 412 P.2d 326. Certificate not conclusive, 16 H. 294, 300. See 18 H. 412, 413.

Record or certified copy admissible though original produced. 14 H. 276, 277. Record may be offered to impeach original by showing alteration in description after recording. 14 H. 276, 278.

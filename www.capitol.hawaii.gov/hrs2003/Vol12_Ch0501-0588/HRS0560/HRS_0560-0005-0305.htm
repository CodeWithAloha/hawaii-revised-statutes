§560:5-305 Acceptance of appointment; consent to jurisdiction. By accepting appointment, a guardian of the person submits personally to the jurisdiction of the family court in any proceeding relating to the guardianship that may be instituted by any interested person. Notice of any proceeding shall be delivered to the guardian or mailed to the guardian by ordinary mail at the guardian's address as listed in the court records and to the guardian's address as then known to the petitioner. [L 1976, c 200, pt of §1; gen ch 1985]

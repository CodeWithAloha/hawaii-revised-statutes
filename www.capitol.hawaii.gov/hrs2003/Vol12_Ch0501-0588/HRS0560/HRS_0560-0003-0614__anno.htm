HRS 0560-0003-0614 ANNOTATIONS

Rules of Court

Special administration, see HPR rule 56.

Case Notes

Circuit court properly denied appellant's petition for appointment as special administrator where appellant was not an "interested person", as defined in §560:1-201, who could petition the court under this section; appellant had no familial relationship to decedent's family, did not have a property right or claim against decedent's estate, and did not have priority under §560:3-203 to be appointed as personal representative of decedent's estate. 88 H. 148, 963 P.2d 1124.

ARTICLE 9. FOREIGN LIMITED PARTNERSHIPS

[§425D-901] Law governing. Subject to the Constitution of this State:

(1) The laws of the state under which a foreign limited partnership is organized govern its organization and internal affairs and the liability of its limited partners; and

(2) A foreign limited partnership may not be denied registration by reason of any difference between those laws and the laws of this State. [L 1989, c 288, pt of §1]

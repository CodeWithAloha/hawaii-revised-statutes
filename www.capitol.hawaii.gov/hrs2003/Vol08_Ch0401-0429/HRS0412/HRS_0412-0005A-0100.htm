ARTICLE 5A. INTERNATIONAL BANKING CORPORATIONS

PART I. GENERAL PROVISION

§412:5A-100 Applicability of other provisions of this chapter. Unless otherwise expressly provided in this article, the entities covered by this article shall have none of the powers generally given to Hawaii financial institutions under this chapter. Neither shall such entities be required to file any reports or give any notices except as may be expressly provided in this article. [L 1993, c 350, pt of §1]

§412:3-213 Denial of charter or license. If the commissioner is not satisfied that the applicant meets all the criteria set forth for approval of the charter or license, the commissioner shall issue a written decision denying the applicant's application. An applicant who is denied approval may request a hearing before the commissioner in accordance with chapter 91. Any final decision of the commissioner denying an applicant a charter or license may be appealed to the circuit court as provided in chapter 91. [L 1993, c 350, pt of §1]

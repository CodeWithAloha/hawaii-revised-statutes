§425-11 Record of statements. The director shall keep books or files in which the information required by this chapter to be filed with the director shall be recorded. The books or files shall be open to public inspection. [L 1969, c 247, pt of §1; am L 1982, c 204, §8; am L 1983, c 124, §17; gen ch 1985; am L 2002, c 130, §83]

§15-11 Voting by absentee voter at polls prohibited. Any person having voted an absentee ballot pursuant to this section shall not be entitled to cast a ballot at the polls on election day. An absentee voter who does cast a ballot at the polls shall be guilty of an election offense under section 19-3(5). [L 1975, c 36, pt of §3]

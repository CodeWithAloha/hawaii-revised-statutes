§26-54 Administrative director of the State. Effective January 1, 1989, and January 1, 1990, the salary of the administrative director of the State shall be $86,164 and $90,041 a year, respectively, and, effective July 1, 2004, and every eight years thereafter, shall be as last recommended by the executive salary commission pursuant to section 26-55, unless rejected by the legislature.

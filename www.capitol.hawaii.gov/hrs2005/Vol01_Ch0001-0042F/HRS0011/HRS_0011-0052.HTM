§11-52 Hearing; decision final. [Section effective until June 30, 2006. For section effective July 1, 2006, see below.] When the appeal is perfected, the supreme court shall hear the cause either in term time or in vacation as soon thereafter as may be reasonable. The determination by the court of the question shall be final. [L 1970, c 26, pt of §2]

§11-52 Hearing; decision final. [Section effective July 1, 2006. For section effective until June 30, 2006, see above.] When the appeal is perfected, the court shall hear the appeal as soon thereafter as may be reasonable. [L 1970, c 26, pt of §2; am L 2004, c 202, §2]

Note

L 2004, c 202, §82 provides:

"SECTION 82. Appeals pending in the supreme court as of the effective date of this Act [July 1, 2006] may be transferred to the intermediate appellate court or retained at the supreme court as the chief justice, in the chief justice's sole discretion, directs."

ARTICLE 2

FRATERNAL BENEFIT SOCIETIES

PART I. STRUCTURE AND PURPOSE

§432:2-101 Scope of article. This article relates only to fraternal benefit societies, as defined herein, which desire to be authorized to pay benefits in accordance with this article after July 10, 1961. This article shall not apply to prepaid legal service plans subject to chapter 488 even though the plan may be offered by a fraternal benefit society. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2]

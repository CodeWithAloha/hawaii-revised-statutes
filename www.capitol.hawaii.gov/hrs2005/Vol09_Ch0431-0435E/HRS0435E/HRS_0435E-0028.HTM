§435E-28 Meeting of members. There shall be a meeting of all members participating in the interindemnity arrangement, at least annually, after not less than ten days' written notice has been given, at a location reasonably convenient to the participating members and on a date which is within a reasonable period of time following the distribution of the annual financial statements. [L 1977, c 182, pt of §1]

PART I. COOPERATIVE INDEMNITY

§435E-1 Definitions. Unless the context clearly requires otherwise:

"Commissioner" means the insurance commissioner of this State.

"Physician" or "surgeon" means any person licensed under chapter 453 or 460; or any professional corporation, partnership, or other entity whose stockholders or partners are comprised solely of persons licensed under chapter 453 or 460. [L 1977, c 182, pt of §1; am L 1982, c 203, §15; am L 1996, c 202, §10]

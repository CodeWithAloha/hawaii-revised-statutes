[§431:10C-117.5] Additional civil liability. An insurer whose insured causes death or injury to another person, and that is not entitled to the reduction provided in section 431:10C-301.5, shall be entitled to recover the amount of the covered loss deductible that would have applied from the insured whose conduct resulted in inapplicability of the covered loss deductible. [L 2000, c 70, §1]

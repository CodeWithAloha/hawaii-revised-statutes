§431:14-105 Policy revisions that alter coverage. Any policy revisions that alter coverage in any manner shall be filed with the commissioner, consist of two printed copies and one copy by electronic, telephonic, or optical means, and include an analysis of the impact of each revision on rates.

After review by the commissioner, the commissioner shall determine whether a rate filing for the policy revision must be submitted in accordance with section 431:14-104. [L 1987, c 349, §9; am L 2005, c 132, §4]

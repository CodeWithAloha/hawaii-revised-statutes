§431:10-235 Forms for proof of loss furnished. Upon written request of any person claiming to have a loss under any insurance contract, an insurer shall furnish forms of proof of loss for completion by the person. The insurer shall not, by reason of the requirement to furnish forms, have any responsibility for or with reference to the completion of the proof of loss form or the manner of completion or attempted completion. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2]

§431:15-405 Ancillary summary proceedings. The commissioner, in the commissioner's sole discretion, may institute proceedings under section 431:15-201 through section 431:15-203 at the request of the commissioner or other appropriate insurance official of the domiciliary state of any foreign or alien insurer having property located in this State. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2]

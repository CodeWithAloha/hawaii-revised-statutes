§431:10D-115 Dealing in dividends. No life insurer nor any of its representatives, producers, or affiliates, shall buy, take by assignment other than in connection with policy loans, or otherwise deal or traffic in any rights to dividends existing under participating life insurance policies issued by the insurer. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2; am L 2002, c 155, §71]

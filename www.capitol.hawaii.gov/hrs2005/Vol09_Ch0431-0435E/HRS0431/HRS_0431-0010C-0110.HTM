§431:10C-110 Rejection of application, joint underwriting plan placement. A producer, including a branch office of a foreign or alien insurer, upon rejection of an application for a motor vehicle insurance policy or optional additional insurance, shall immediately offer, subject to the guidelines established by rules of the commissioner, to place the requested insurance coverages with the joint underwriting plan. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2; am L Sp 1993, c 4, §2; am L 1997, c 251, §21; am L 2001, c 216, §18]

§431:2-202.5 Approval; when deemed effective. Except as provided otherwise, any approval required by law shall be deemed granted on the thirtieth calendar day following the filing of the request for approval if the commissioner does not take any affirmative action to grant or deny the approval within thirty calendar days of the request. [L 2000, c 264, §1; am L 2001, c 121, §1]

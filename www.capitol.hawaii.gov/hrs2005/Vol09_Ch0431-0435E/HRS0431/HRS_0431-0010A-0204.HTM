§431:10A-204 Optional provision, examination and autopsy. There may be a provision that the insurer shall have the right and opportunity to examine the person of any individual covered under the policy when and so often as it may reasonably require during the pendency of claim under the policy and also the right and opportunity to make an autopsy in case of death where it is not prohibited by law. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2]

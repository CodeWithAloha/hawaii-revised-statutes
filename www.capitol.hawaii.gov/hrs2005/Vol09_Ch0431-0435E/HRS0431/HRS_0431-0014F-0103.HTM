[§431:14F-103] Making of rates. (a) Rates shall not be excessive, inadequate, or unfairly discriminatory and shall be reasonable in relation to benefits provided.

(b) Except to the extent necessary to meet the provisions of subsection (a), uniformity among managed care plans in any matters within the scope of this section shall be neither required nor prohibited. [L 2002, c 74, pt of §2]

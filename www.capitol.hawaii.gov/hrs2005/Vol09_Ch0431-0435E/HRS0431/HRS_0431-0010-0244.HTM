§431:10-244 Filing procedure for contracts approved by commissioner. Each insurance contract requiring approval by the commissioner pursuant to section 392-48 and section 386-124 shall be accompanied by a $20 fee payable to the commissioner, which fee shall be deposited in the commissioner's education and training fund. [L 1988, c 363, §1]

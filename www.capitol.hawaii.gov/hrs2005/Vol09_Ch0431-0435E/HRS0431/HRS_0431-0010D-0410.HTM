§431:10D-410 Penalties. In addition to any other penalties provided by the laws of this State, an insurer or producer that violates a requirement of this part shall be guilty of an unfair or deceptive act or practice in violation of article 13. [L 2000, c 252, pt of §2; am L 2004, c 122, §56]

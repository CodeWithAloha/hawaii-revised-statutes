§712-1270.5 Injunctions against persons. Nothing in this part shall be construed to prohibit injunctions against persons causing, maintaining, aiding, abetting, or permitting a nuisance from entering or residing in any public or private building, premises, or place, in or upon which the nuisance exists. [L 1998, c 286, §1; am L 2005, c 123, §2]

§806-63 Defense. All persons tried for any offense shall be admitted after the close of the case for the prosecution to make full answer and defense thereto by counsel or attorney. [L 1876, c 40, §46; RL 1925, §4026; RL 1935, §5527; RL 1945, §10832; RL 1955, §258-42; HRS §711-63; ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1]

§834-5 Release of inmate. The superintendent or other official in charge of a penal or correctional institution in this State shall give over the person of any inmate thereof whenever so required by the operation of the Agreement on Detainers. [L 1965, c 160, §5; Supp, §250A-5; HRS §714-5; ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1]

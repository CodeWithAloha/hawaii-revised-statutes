§846-22 Oaths and investigations. The attorney general and each of the attorney general's authorized subordinates may administer oaths and require and take the oral or written statements under oath of any person in connection with any information validly required by the attorney general or the attorney general's subordinates under this part or any rule issued under this part. [L 1983, c 78, pt of §3(6)]

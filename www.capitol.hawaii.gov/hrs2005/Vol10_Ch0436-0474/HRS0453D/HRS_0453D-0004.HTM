[§453D-4] Fees; disposition. (a) Application, examination, reexamination, license, renewal, restoration, penalty, and any other fees relating to the administration of this chapter, shall not be refundable and shall be provided in rules adopted by the director pursuant to chapter 91.

(b) Fees assessed shall defray costs incurred by the department to support the operation of the mental health counselor licensing program. Fees collected shall be managed in accordance with section 26-9(l). [L 2004, c 209, pt of §2]

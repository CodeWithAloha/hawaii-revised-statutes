[§444-9.5] Licensing of craftsmen. At least half of all craftsmen requiring licenses employed on a construction project by a specialty contractor in the trade in which the craftsman is licensed shall be licensed in accordance with and to the extent required by chapter 448E. The board may waive this requirement in any county when there are insufficient licensed craftsmen in that county to comply herewith. [L 1971, c 183, §2; gen ch 1985]

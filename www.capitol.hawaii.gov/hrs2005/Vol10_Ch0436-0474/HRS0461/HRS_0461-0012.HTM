§461-12 Adequate equipment. A pharmacy that compounds drugs shall be equipped with adequate pharmaceutical equipment to ensure the proper compounding of prescriptions. The board shall by rules prescribe the minimum of professional and technical equipment and reference materials that a pharmacy shall at all times possess. [L 1949, c 175, pt of §1; RL 1955, §71-12; HRS §461-12; am L 1986, c 143, §12; am L 1998, c 70, §1]

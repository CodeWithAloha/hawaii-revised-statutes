[§436E-14] Foreign school curricula and standards. The board of acupuncture shall not recognize and approve an earned doctoral degree from a foreign university or college whose curricula and standards are not equivalent to or higher than institutions in the United States which have been recognized and approved by the board in the study or practice of acupuncture. [L 1985, c 214, pt of §1]

Note

Actions taken by the board prior to December 31, 1984, remain in full force and effect. L 1985, c 214, pt of §1.

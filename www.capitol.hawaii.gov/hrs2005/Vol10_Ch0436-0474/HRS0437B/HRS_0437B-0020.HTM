§437B-20 License condition precedent to lien. No person required to be licensed under this chapter shall have the benefit of any lien for labor or materials or the right to sue on a contract for motor vehicle repairs done by the person unless the person was licensed at the time the person performed the contract. [L 1975, c 143, pt of §2; gen ch 1985; am L 1998, c 199, §7]

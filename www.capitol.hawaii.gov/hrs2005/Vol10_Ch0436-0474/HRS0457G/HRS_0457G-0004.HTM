[§457G-4] Civil penalty. Any person violating any provisions of this chapter shall be fined a sum of not less than $500 nor more than $10,000 for each violation, which sum shall be collected in a civil action brought by the attorney general or the director of the office of consumer protection on behalf of the State. [L 1978, c 146, pt of §1]

§471-7 Power to investigate. The board of veterinary examiners or any member thereof, or any person designated by the board for the purpose, may investigate any violation or suspected violation of this chapter. Each member of the board may administer oaths in connection with any such investigation. [L 1949, c 280, pt of §1; RL 1955, §73-7; HRS §471-7]

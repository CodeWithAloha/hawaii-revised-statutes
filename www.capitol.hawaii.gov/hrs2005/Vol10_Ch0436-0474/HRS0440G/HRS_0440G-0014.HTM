§440G-14 Reports. Each cable operator shall file with the director reports of its financial, technical, and operational condition and its ownership. The reports shall be made in a form and on the time schedule prescribed by the director and shall be kept on file open to the public. [L 1970, c 112, pt of §1; am L 1987, c 301, §16]

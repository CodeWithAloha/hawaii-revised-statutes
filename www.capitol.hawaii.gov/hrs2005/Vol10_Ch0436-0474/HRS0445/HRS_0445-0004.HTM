§445-4 Fees in advance. No license shall be issued under this chapter until the full fee required therein has been paid in advance for the term of one year.

Each treasurer shall account for all fees collected by the treasurer as county realizations. [L 1896, c 64, §6; am L 1909, c 151, pt of §2; RL 1925, pt of §1959; RL 1935, §2404; RL 1945, §7005; RL 1955, §155-5; HRS §445-4; gen ch 1985]

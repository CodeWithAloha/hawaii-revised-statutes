PART X. SCRAP DEALERS

Note

Sections 445-231 to 235 designated as Part X by L 1990, c 164, §28.

Assessment under §26H-4 removed. L 1992, c 232, §1.

§445-231 Definitions. As used in this chapter, unless the context otherwise requires:

"Scrap" means any secondhand or used metal except those used motor vehicle parts provided in chapter 289.

"Scrap dealer" means any person engaged in the business of buying, selling, or dealing in scrap, or any person operating, carrying on, conducting, or maintaining a scrap yard.

"Scrap yard" means any yard, plot, space, enclosure, building, or any other place where scrap is collected, stored, gathered together, and kept. [L 1976, c 230, pt of §2]

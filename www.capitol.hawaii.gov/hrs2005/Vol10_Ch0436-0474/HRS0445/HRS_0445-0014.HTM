§445-14 Limits of license. Except as otherwise in this chapter provided, a license granted thereunder authorizes the carrying on of the business licensed only at the place indicated in the license. A license granted to carry on a business from place to place shall be limited to the county in which the business is to be conducted. [L 1923, c 266, §3; RL 1925, §2113; RL 1935, §2415; RL 1945, §7016; RL 1955, §155-15; am L 1967, c 248, §3; HRS §445-14]

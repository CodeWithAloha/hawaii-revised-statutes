§467-10 Form of licenses, registrations, and certificates. The form of every license, registration, and certificate shall be prescribed by the commission, and shall be issued in the name of the commission. [L 1933, c 140, §9; RL 1935, §7318; am L 1935, c 126, §1; RL 1945, §7739; RL 1955, §170-9; HRS §467-10; gen ch 1993; am L 1994, c 100, §8]

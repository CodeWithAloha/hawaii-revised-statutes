§460-17 Records. The board shall keep a record which shall be open to public inspection at all reasonable times, of proceedings relating to the issuance, refusal, renewal, suspension, and revocation of licenses to practice osteopathy. This record shall also contain the name, known place of business and residence, and the date and number of the license of every registered osteopathic physician.

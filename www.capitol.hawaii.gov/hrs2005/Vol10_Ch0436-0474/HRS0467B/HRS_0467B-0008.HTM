§467B-8 Information filed to become public records. Statements, reports, professional fundraising counsel contracts or professional solicitor contracts, and all other documents and information required to be filed under this chapter or by the attorney general shall become government records in the department and be open to the general public for inspection at such times and under such conditions as the attorney general may prescribe. [L 1969, c 167, pt of §1; am L 1993, c 206, §7; am L 2004, c 93, §7]

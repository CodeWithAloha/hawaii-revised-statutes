§441-42 Trustee's compensation. The trustee shall be entitled to the same compensation as provided by law for trust companies in the management of fiduciary accounts, but in no event shall any sum in excess of five per cent of the income derived from the fund or pre-need trust in any year be paid as compensation to the trustee for its services in the administration of the fund or trust. [L 1967, c 199, pt of §1; HRS §441-42; am L 1985, c 101, §22]

§441-44 Trustee's account; enforcement by attorney general. The trustee shall annually file with the director an account which shall be made in such form as shall be prescribed by the director. The director shall notify the attorney general of any failure on the part of the trustee to comply with sections 441-37 to 441-44, or of the instrument creating the fund, or trust, and the attorney general may take such action as deemed appropriate. [L 1967, c 199, pt of §1; HRS §441-44; am L 1975, c 21, §17; am L 1981, c 221, §21; am L 1985, c 101, §23]

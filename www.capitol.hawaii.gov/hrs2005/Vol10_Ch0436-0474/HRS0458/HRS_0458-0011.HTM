§458-11 Reinstatement. The director may reinstate without examination the license of any dispensing optician issued under this chapter which has been revoked or may modify the suspension of any such license which has been suspended. [L 1949, c 183, pt of §1; RL 1955, §69-11; HRS §458-11; am L 1987, c 300, §12; am L 1992, c 202, §120; am L 1997, c 301, §9]

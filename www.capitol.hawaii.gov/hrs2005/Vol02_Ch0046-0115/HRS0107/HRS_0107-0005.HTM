§107-5 Costs, fees and charges. The department of accounting and general services shall be reimbursed for the reasonable costs incurred in furnishing supplies and performing services for any other state department or agency in accordance with a schedule of fees and charges approved by the director of finance after taking into consideration the amount of funds available to such department or agency for such purpose, provided that this section shall not apply to the department of land and natural resources. [L 1951, c 283, §3; RL 1955, §103-3; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §§12, 14, 21; am L 1961, c 132, §1; am L 1963, c 114, §1; HRS §107-5]

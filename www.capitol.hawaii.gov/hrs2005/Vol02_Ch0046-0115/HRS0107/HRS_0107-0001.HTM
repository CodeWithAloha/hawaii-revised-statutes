§107-1 Management of internal improvements. The comptroller is charged with the superintendence and management of the internal improvements of the State. [CC 1859, §167; RL 1925, §798; RL 1935, §1700; RL 1945, §4901; am L 1947, c 180, §1; RL 1955, §110-1; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §12; HRS §107-1]

Within duties to accept promise to pay for use of sewer. 19 H. 41.

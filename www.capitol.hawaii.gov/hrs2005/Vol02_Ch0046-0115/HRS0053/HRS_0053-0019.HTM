§53-19 Report. Each redevelopment agency shall make an annual report of its receipts, expenditures, and activities, and of its proposed program and estimated cost thereof for the ensuing year to the council of its county, before August 1 as of the end of the preceding fiscal year. [L 1949, c 379, pt of §4; am L 1955, c 271, §1(i); RL 1955, §143-19; am imp L 1965, c 166, §§1, 2; HRS §53-19]

§76-23.5 Recruitment incentives. Within limits set forth in rules of the director, appointing authorities may pay for all or a portion of the travel and transportation expenses or to provide a monetary incentive to enhance the recruitment of persons employed or appointed to critical-to-fill and labor shortage positions. [L 1991, c 205, §2; am L 2000, c 253, §18]

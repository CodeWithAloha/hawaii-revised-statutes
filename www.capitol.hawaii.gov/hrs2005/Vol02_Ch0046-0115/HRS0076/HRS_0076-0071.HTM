PART II. SPECIAL CIVIL SERVICE PROVISIONS FOR THE

COUNTIES OF HAWAII, MAUI, AND KAUAI

Note

Part II heading renumbered from part III and amended by L 2000, c 253, §32.

§76-71 Department of civil service. There shall be a department of civil service for each of the counties of Hawaii, Maui, and Kauai, which shall include a personnel director and a merit appeals board established under section 76-47. [L 1955, c 274, pt of §1; RL 1955, §3-60; HRS §76-71; am L 1977, c 61, pt of §1; am L 2000, c 253, §33]

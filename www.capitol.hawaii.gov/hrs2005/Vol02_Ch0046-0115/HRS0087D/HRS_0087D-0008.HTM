[§87D-8] State and county contributions to the trust; active employees. Upon the establishment of a voluntary employees' beneficiary association trust, the State, through the department of budget and finance, [and] the counties through their respective departments of finance, shall pay to the trust a monthly contribution equal to the amount specified in the applicable public sector collective bargaining agreement from July 1, 2005, and thereafter. [L 2005, c 245, pt of §2]

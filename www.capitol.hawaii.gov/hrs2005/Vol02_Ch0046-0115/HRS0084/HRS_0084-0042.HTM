[§84-42] Mandatory ethics training course. All state officers and employees enumerated in section 84-41 shall complete an ethics training course administered by the state ethics commission as provided in this part. For the purposes of this part, "ethics training" includes education and training in:

(1) The ethics laws set forth in this chapter; and

(2) The lobbying laws set forth in chapter 97. [L 2004, c 198, pt of §1]

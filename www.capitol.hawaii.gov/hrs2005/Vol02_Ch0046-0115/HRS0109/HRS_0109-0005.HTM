§109-5 Security personnel, powers. The person employed as the chief security officer by the authority shall have all of the powers of police officers, including the power of arrest; provided that such powers shall remain in force and in effect only while the person is in the actual performance of the person's duties at the stadium or the Kapolei recreational sports complex. [L 1976, c 118, §1; gen ch 1985; am L 1999, c 160, §10]

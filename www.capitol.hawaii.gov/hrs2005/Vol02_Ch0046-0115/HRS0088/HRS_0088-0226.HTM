§88-226 Payments to federal government. From the contribution fund the custodian of the fund shall pay to the Secretary of the Treasury such amounts and at such time or times as may be directed by the state agency in accordance with any agreement entered into under section 88-212 and the Social Security Act. [L 1953, c 217, §6(c); RL 1955, §6-202; HRS §88-226]

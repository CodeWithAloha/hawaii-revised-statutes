§88-204 County appropriations directed. The respective councils of the several counties shall appropriate annually for each fiscal period a sum sufficient to cover the payment of the pensions granted under this part; provided that the board of water supply of the city and county of Honolulu shall appropriate sufficient funds to cover pensions granted under this part as a result of employment in the service of the board of water supply. [L 1945, c 262, §4; RL 1955, §6-183; HRS §88-204]

Revision Note

The words "boards of supervisors or", preceding "councils", have been deleted.

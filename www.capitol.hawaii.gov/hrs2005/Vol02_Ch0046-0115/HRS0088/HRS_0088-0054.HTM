§88-54 Service while legislative employee. Any member who takes a leave of absence to be employed by the legislature during any legislative session shall be entitled to all benefits and required to make all employee contributions under the system for the period during which such employee worked for the legislature; provided that the contributions shall be made on the same basis as would have been required had the employee not been on leave of absence. [L 1947, c 58, §1; am L 1951, c 193, §1; RL 1955, §6-37; am L 1967, c 176, §2; HRS §88-54; am L 1969, c 110, pt of §1; am L 1982, c 165, §2(7)]

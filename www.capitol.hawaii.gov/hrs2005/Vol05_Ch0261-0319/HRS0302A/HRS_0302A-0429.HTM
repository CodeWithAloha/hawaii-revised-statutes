§302A-429 Work-based learning program authorized. To provide students with opportunities to apply knowledge and skills acquired in the classroom to real life work experiences, the department may establish and regulate a work-based learning program under conditions determined by the department and the University of Hawaii. [L 1996, c 89, pt of §2; am L 1997, c 344, §2; am L 2001, c 242, §1]

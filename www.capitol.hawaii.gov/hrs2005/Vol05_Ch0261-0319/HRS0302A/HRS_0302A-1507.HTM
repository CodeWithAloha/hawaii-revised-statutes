§302A-1507 Classroom cleaning project; established. (a) There is established a classroom cleaning project in all public schools, excluding new century charter schools and new century conversion charter schools. Each school, through its school community council, may develop mechanisms to provide for classroom cleaning, including but not limited to having parent, student, or other community groups clean the classrooms on a regular, continuing basis.

(b) Schools may use any available resources to achieve the purposes of this section; provided that no full-time custodial staff employed at the school shall be displaced. [L 1996, c 89, pt of §2; am L 2004, c 51, §31]

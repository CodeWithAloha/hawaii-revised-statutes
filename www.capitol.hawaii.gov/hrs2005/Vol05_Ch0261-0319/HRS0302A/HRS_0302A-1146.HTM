§302A-1146 Schools; merchandise. It shall be unlawful for any public school, without the written permission of the department, to operate stores or to sell merchandise, with the following exceptions: school lunches, milk, ice cream, candy, and products made or grown at the school as part of the educational program. The department, with the advice of the comptroller, shall adopt rules in conformance with chapter 91 necessary for the purposes of this section. [L 1996, c 89, pt of §2; am L 2004, c 220, §6]

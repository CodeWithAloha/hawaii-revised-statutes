§287-45 Exceptions. This chapter, except for section 287-20 shall not apply with respect to any motor vehicle owned by the United States, this State, or any political subdivision thereof; nor, except for sections 287-4 and 287-37, with respect to any motor vehicle which is subject to the requirements of chapter 288. [L 1949, c 393, pt of §1; am L 1951, c 184, §1; RL 1955, §160-123; HRS §287-45]

§281-94 Forfeiture of fee paid. If any license is revoked and canceled by the liquor commission or liquor control adjudication board, the fee paid for the license shall be forfeited to the county as respects the unexpired portion of the fee paid for the license. [L Sp 1933, c 40, §57; RL 1935, §2626; RL 1945, §7278; RL 1955, §159-93; HRS §281-94; am L 1998, c 249, §15]

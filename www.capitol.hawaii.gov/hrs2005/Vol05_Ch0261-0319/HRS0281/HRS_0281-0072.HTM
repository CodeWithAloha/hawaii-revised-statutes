§281-72 Condition of premises. All premises licensed or proposed to be licensed shall be constructed, arranged, furnished, equipped, maintained, and operated in such manner as may be prescribed by the liquor commission. [L Sp 1933, c 40, §38; RL 1935, §2607; RL 1945, §7260; RL 1955, §159-71; HRS §281-72]

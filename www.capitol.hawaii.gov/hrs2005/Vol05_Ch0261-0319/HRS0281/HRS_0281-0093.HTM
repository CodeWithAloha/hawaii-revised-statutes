§281-93 Reports to prosecuting officers. When the revocation or suspension of any license is by reason of any violation of law the liquor commission or the administrator shall report the facts to the prosecuting officer for prosecution. [L Sp 1933, c 40, §56; RL 1935, §2625; RL 1945, §7277; RL 1955, §159-92; HRS §281-93; am L 1998, c 249, §14]

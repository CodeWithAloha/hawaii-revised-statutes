§291-51.6 Issuance of temporary removable windshield parking placards. Each county may issue one temporary removable windshield placard and a second temporary removable windshield placard to each applicant who requests and presents a certificate of disability that verifies the duration of the applicant's disability in monthly increments, which shall not exceed six months, and upon payment of a fee to the issuing agency. The temporary removable windshield placard shall be designed, fabricated, and sold to the counties at a rate negotiated by the disability and communication access board. [L 1992, c 46, pt of §2; am L 1997, c 308, §7; am L 2001, c 297, §4; am L 2003, c 30, §3]

§271-3 Application of chapter, interstate, or foreign commerce. Neither this chapter nor any provision hereof shall apply to commerce with foreign nations or to interstate commerce, except insofar as the application is permitted under the Constitution and laws of the United States. [L 1961, c 121, pt of §2; Supp, §106C-3; HRS §271-3]

Attorney General Opinions

Commission jurisdiction extends over motor carriers handling property under through route and joint fare arrangements. Att. Gen. Op. 63-26.

Case Notes

Hawaii household goods movers no longer exempt from Interstate Commerce Commission regulation. 395 F. Supp. 261.

[§425E-808] Liability of general partner and person dissociated as general partner when claim against limited partnership barred. If a claim against a dissolved limited partnership is barred under section 425E-806 or 425E-807, any corresponding claim under section 425E-404 shall also be barred. [L 2003, c 210, pt of §1]

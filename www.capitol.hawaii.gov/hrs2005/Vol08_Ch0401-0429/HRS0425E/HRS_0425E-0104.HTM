[§425E-104] Nature, purpose, and duration of entity. (a) A limited partnership is an entity distinct from its partners. A limited partnership is the same entity regardless of whether its certificate states that the limited partnership is a limited liability limited partnership.

(b) A limited partnership may be organized under this chapter for any lawful purpose.

(c) A limited partnership has a perpetual duration. [L 2003, c 210, pt of §1]

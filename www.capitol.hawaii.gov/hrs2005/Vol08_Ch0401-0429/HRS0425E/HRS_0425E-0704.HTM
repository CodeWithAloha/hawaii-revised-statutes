[§425E-704] Power of estate of deceased partner. If a partner dies, the deceased partner's personal representative or other legal representative may exercise the rights of a transferee as provided in section 425E-702 and, for the purposes of settling the estate, may exercise the rights of a current limited partner under section 425E-304. [L 2003, c 210, pt of §1]

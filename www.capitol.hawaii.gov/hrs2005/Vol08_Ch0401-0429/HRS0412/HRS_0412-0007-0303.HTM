§412:7-303 Limitations on loans and extensions of credit to one borrower. A savings and loan association shall comply with the lending limitations with respect to one borrower contained in section 5u of the Home Owners' Loan Act of 1933, as amended, 12 U.S.C. §1464u. [L 1993, c 350, pt of §1]

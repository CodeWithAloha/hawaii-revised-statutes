PART III. LOANS AND INVESTMENTS

§412:6-300 Applicability of part. This part sets forth the requirements and restrictions for lending and investments by all savings banks. A savings bank may make loans and extensions of credit and may invest its assets as may be permitted by this part and as may be provided elsewhere in this article. [L 1993, c 350, pt of §1]

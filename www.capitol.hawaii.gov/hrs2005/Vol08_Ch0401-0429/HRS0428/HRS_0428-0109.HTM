§428-109 Resignation of registered agent. (a) A registered agent of a domestic or foreign limited liability company may resign from the registered agent's appointment by signing and delivering to the director for filing the signed statement of resignation. The statement may include a statement that the registered office is also discontinued.

(b) The registered agent shall mail one copy to the registered office (if not discontinued) and the other copy to the company at its principal office.

(c) The appointment of the agent is terminated, and the registered office discontinued if so provided, on the thirty-first day after the date on which the statement was filed. [L 1996, c 92, pt of §1; am L 2002, c 130, §103; am L 2003, c 124, §71]

§428-108 Change of registered office or registered agent. A limited liability company or a foreign limited liability company may change its registered office or its registered agent by delivering to the director for filing a statement of change which sets forth:

(1) The name of the company;

(2) The street address of its current registered office in this State, the name of its current registered agent at its registered office in this State, and any changes required to keep the information current; and

(3) That after the change or changes are made, the street addresses of its registered office and registered agent shall be identical. [L 1996, c 92, pt of §1; am L 2002, c 130, §102]

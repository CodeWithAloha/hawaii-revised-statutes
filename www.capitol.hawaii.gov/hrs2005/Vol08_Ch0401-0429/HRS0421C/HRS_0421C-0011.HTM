PART II. SPECIAL PROVISIONS

§421C-11 Forms. The department of commerce and consumer affairs may prescribe and furnish on request forms for documents required or permitted to be filed by this chapter. The director of commerce and consumer affairs may require that the use of these forms be mandatory. [L 1982, c 97, pt of §2 and c 204, §8; am L 2003, c 124, §45]

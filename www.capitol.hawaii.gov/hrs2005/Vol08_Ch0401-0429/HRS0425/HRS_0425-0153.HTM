§425-153 Statement of qualification. A statement of qualification shall contain:

(1) The name of the partnership;

(2) A statement that the partnership elects to be a limited liability partnership; and

(3) The mailing address of the partnership's initial principal office, the street address of the partnership's initial registered office in the State, and the name of its initial registered agent at its initial registered office in the State. [L 2000, c 218, pt of §1; am L 2001, c 129, §73; am L 2002, c 130, §86; am L 2004, c 121, §30]

Limited Liability Partnerships

§425-151 Name. The name of a limited liability partnership shall contain "Registered Limited Liability Partnership" or "Limited Liability Partnership", or the abbreviation "R.L.L.P.", "L.L.P.", "RLLP", or "LLP". [L 2000, c 218, pr of §1; am L 2001, c 241, §1]

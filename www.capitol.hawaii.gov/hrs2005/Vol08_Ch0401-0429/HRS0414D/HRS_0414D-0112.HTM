[§414D-112] Voting requirements. (a) Unless this chapter, the articles, or the bylaws require a greater vote or voting by class, if a quorum is present, the affirmative vote of the votes represented and voting (which affirmative votes also constitute a majority of the required quorum) is the act of the members.

(b) A bylaws amendment to increase or decrease the vote required for any member action shall be approved by the members. [L 2001, c 105, pt of §1]

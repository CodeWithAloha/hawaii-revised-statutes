§485-22 Statutory or common-law remedies. Nothing in this chapter shall limit any statutory or common-law right of any person to bring any action in any court for any act involved in the sale of securities, or the right of the State to punish any person for any violation of any law. [L 1957, c 314, pt of §1; Supp, §199-18; HRS §485-22]

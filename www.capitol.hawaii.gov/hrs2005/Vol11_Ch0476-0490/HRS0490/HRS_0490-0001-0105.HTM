§490:1-105 Severability. If any provision or clause of this chapter or its application to any person or circumstances is held invalid, the invalidity does not affect other provisions or applications of this chapter which can be given effect without the invalid provision or application, and to this end the provisions of this chapter are declared to be severable. [L 2004, c 162, pt of §1]

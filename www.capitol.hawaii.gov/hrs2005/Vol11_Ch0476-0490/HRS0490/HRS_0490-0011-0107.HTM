§490:11-107 Transition provisions as to priorities. Except as otherwise provided in Article 11, old U.C.C. shall apply to any question of priority if the positions of the parties were fixed prior to the effective date of July 1, 1979. In other cases questions of priority shall be determined by the new U.C.C. [L 1978, c 155, pt of §1(34)]

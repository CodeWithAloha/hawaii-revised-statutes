§486-78 Authority to inspect. The administrator may upon the administrator's own initiative or upon complaint, after properly identifying or making a legitimate effort to identify oneself, enter upon any public or private property, in accordance with law, where odometers may reasonably be stored, held, sold, repaired, altered, manipulated, or otherwise influenced so as to display any digital or analog representation, factual or otherwise, to determine if any actions relating to odometers are being conducted contrary to law. [L 1988, c 156, pt of §3]

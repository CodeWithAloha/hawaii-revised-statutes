[§486N-5] Contents of contracts. In addition to all other requirements of this chapter, every health club contract shall include the following:

(1) The name and address of the health club;

(2) The name and address of the buyer; and

(3) A list of the names and addresses of other clubs, if the contract is transferable for use at other facilities. [L 1984, c 161, pt of §1]

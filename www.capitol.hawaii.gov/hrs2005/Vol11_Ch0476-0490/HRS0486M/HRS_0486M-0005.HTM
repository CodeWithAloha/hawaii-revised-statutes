§486M-5 Inspections. The chief of police of each county or the chief of police's authorized representative may immediately inspect, during normal business hours or whenever the dealer or the dealer's agents or employees are otherwise present, any records required by this chapter and any articles described in such records that the police reasonably believe are stolen goods. [L 1982, c 282, pt of §2; gen ch 1985; am L 1991, c 120, pt of §1]

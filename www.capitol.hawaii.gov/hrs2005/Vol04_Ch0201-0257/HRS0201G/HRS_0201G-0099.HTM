§201G-99 Regulation of traffic within public housing projects in other counties. [Section effective until June 30, 2006. For section effective July 1, 2006, see below.] Any law to the contrary notwithstanding, the county councils of the counties of Hawaii, Maui, and Kauai may regulate traffic within the various public housing projects of the corporation within their respective counties by ordinance as provided by law. [L 1997, c 350, pt of §2; am L 2005, c 120, §23]

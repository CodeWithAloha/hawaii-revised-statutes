[§201G-4.6] Resident advisory board nomination of resident board member. [Section effective until June 30, 2006. For section effective July 1, 2006, see below.] In the event of a vacancy for the resident member seat on the corporation board, the resident advisory board shall compile a list of five individuals for the governor's consideration for appointment to the board; provided the nominees to the board shall be:

(1) Participants who are directly assisted by the corporation under the federal public housing or section 8 tenant-based programs and who need not be members of the resident advisory board;

(2) At least eighteen years of age; and

(3) Authorized members of the assisted household. [L 2001, c 289, pt of §2]

§201G-4.6 Resident advisory board nomination of resident board member. [Section effective July 1, 2006. For section effective until June 30, 2006, see above.] In the event of a vacancy for the resident member seat on the administration board, the resident advisory board shall compile a list of five individuals for the governor's consideration for appointment to the board; provided the nominees to the board shall be:

(1) Participants who are directly assisted by the administration under the federal public housing or section 8 tenant-based programs and who need not be members of the resident advisory board;

(2) At least eighteen years of age; and

(3) Authorized members of the assisted household. [L 2001, c 289, pt of §2; am L 2005, c 196, §26(a)]

E. Public Housing; Corporation-County Cooperation

Note

Subpart heading amended by L 1998, c 212, §12.

Effective July 1, 2006, "Corporation" in subpart heading changed to "Administration". L 2005, c 196, §26(a).

[§201G-91] Gifts, etc., to counties from corporation. [Section effective until June 30, 2006. For section effective July 1, 2006, see below.] The corporation may make and pay gifts, or donations, of money directly to any county. Any county may receive the gifts or donations as a general fund realization, and expend the same for any purpose authorized by law. [L 1997, c 350, pt of §2]

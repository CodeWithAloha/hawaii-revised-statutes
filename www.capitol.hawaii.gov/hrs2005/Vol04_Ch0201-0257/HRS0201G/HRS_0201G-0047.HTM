§201G-47 Housing, government aid, political subdivisions. [Section effective until June 30, 2006. For section effective July 1, 2006, see below.] Any political subdivision may appropriate money for the purposes of meeting any local participation in housing costs or expenses required in order to obtain assistance from the federal government in the development and administration of public housing projects and programs under this subpart, or of providing funds for use by the corporation in developing and administering public housing projects. [L 1998, c 212, pt of §4; am L 2005, c 120, §12]

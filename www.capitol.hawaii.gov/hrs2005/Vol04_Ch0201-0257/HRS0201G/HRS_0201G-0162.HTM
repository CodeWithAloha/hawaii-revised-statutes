[§201G-162] Issuance of bonds for the development of infrastructure. [Section effective until June 30, 2006. For section effective July 1, 2006, see below.] Without limiting section 201G-161, the corporation, pursuant to and in accordance with this subpart, is hereby authorized to issue bonds for the purpose of financing the development of infrastructure on land owned by the corporation.

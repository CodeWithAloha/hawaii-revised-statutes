§201G-435 Eligible applicants for funds. [Section effective until June 30, 2006. For section effective July 1, 2006, see below.] Eligible applicants for funds shall include nonprofit and for-profit corporations, limited liability companies, partnerships, and government agencies, who are qualified in accordance with rules adopted by the corporation pursuant to chapter 91. [L 1997, c 350, pt of §2; am L 2005, c 196, §6]

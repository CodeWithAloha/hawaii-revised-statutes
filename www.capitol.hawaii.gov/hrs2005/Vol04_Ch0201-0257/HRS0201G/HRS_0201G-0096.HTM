§201G-96 Furnishing of free water not required. [Section effective until June 30, 2006. For section effective July 1, 2006, see below.] Sections 201G-92 to 201G-95 shall not be construed to require the furnishing of any free water to the corporation or to the tenants or occupants of any public housing project or complex owned, operated, or administered by the corporation. [L 1997, c 350, pt of §2; am L 2005, c 120, §21]

§201G-96 Furnishing of free water not required. [Section effective July 1, 2006. For section effective until June 30, 2006, see above.] Sections 201G-92 to 201G-95 shall not be construed to require the furnishing of any free water to the administration or to the tenants or occupants of any public housing project or complex owned, operated, or administered by the administration. [L 1997, c 350, pt of §2; am L 2005, c 120, §21 and c 196, §26(a)]

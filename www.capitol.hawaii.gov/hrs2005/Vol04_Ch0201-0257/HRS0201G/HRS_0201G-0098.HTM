§201G-98 Regulation of traffic within public housing projects in city and county of Honolulu. [Section effective until June 30, 2006. For section effective July 1, 2006, see below.] Any law to the contrary notwithstanding, the city council of the city and county of Honolulu may regulate traffic within the various public housing projects of the corporation in the city and county of Honolulu by ordinance. [L 1997, c 350, pt of §2; am L 2005, c 120, §22]

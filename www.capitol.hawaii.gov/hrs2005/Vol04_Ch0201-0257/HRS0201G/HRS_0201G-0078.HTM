§201G-78 Lien attaches to personalty in possession. [Section effective until June 30, 2006. For section effective July 1, 2006, see below.] Sections 201G-76 and 201G-77 shall also apply to any personal property which, before May 2, 1949, was left in or about any public housing project, and was taken into the possession of the corporation, as herein set forth and provided. [L 1997, c 350, pt of §2; am L 2005, c 120, §17]

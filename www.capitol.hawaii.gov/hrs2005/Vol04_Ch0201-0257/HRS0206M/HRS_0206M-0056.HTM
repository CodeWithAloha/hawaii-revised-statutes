[§206M-56] Annual report. The high technology innovation corporation shall submit an annual report, not later than twenty days prior to the convening of each regular session, to the governor, president of the senate, and the speaker of the house of representatives. The report shall include but not be limited to the high technology innovation corporation's audited financial statement, total amount of payroll and other disbursements made, and progress and accomplishments made during the year.

[§201H-11] Agents, including corporations. [Section effective July 1, 2006.] The administration may exercise any or all of the powers conferred upon it, either generally or with respect to any specific housing project through an agent that it may designate, including any corporation that is formed under the laws of this State, and for such purposes the administration may cause one or more corporations to be formed under the laws of this State or may acquire the capital stock of any corporation. Any corporate agent, all of the stock of which shall be owned by the administration or its nominee, to the extent permitted by law, may exercise any of the powers conferred upon the administration in this chapter.

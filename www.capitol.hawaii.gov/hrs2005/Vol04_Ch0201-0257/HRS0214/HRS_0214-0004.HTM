§214-4 Rules. The department of budget and finance and the office of planning may adopt rules and regulations necessary and convenient for the administration and implementation of this chapter subject to approval by the governor. [L 1965, c 234, §5; Supp, §138F-5; HRS §214-4; am L 1987, c 336, §8; am L 1996, c 299, §3]

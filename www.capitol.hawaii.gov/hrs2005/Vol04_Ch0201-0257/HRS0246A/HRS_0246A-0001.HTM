[§246A-1] Purpose. The legislature finds that section 3 of article VIII of the constitution of the State has been amended to provide that all functions, powers, and duties relating to the taxation of real property heretofore reserved to the State shall be exercised exclusively by the counties with the exception of the County of Kalawao. It is the purpose of this chapter to provide for the orderly transfer of these functions, powers, and duties, including the transfer of personnel, records, and equipment to the counties. [L 1980, c 279, §1]

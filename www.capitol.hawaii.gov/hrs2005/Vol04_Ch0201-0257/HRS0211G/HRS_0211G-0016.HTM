[§211G-16] Capital formation revolving fund. There is established a revolving fund for the corporation to be designated as the capital formation revolving fund. The following shall be deposited into the capital formation revolving fund, all moneys:

(1) Appropriated by the legislature;

(2) Received as repayment of loans;

(3) Earned on investments;

(4) Received pursuant to a venture agreement;

(5) Received as royalties;

(6) Received as premiums or fees charged by the corporation; or

(7) Otherwise received by the corporation. [L 2004, c 215, pt of §1]

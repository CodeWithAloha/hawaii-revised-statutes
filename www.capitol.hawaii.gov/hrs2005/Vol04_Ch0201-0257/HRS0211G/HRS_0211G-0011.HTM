PART II. IMPLEMENTATION

[§211G-11] Business plan. To fulfill its mission as the mobilizer of equity and near-equity capital, the implementation of this chapter by the corporation shall be subject to the supervision of the board. The corporation shall develop an annual business plan for the implementation of this chapter. The business plan shall be submitted to the board for its approval and shall be included in its annual report, which shall be published as provided in section 211G-15. [L 2004, c 215, pt of §1]

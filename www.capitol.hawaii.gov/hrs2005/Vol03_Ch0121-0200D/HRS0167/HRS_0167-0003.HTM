[§167-3] Engineering program manager. The board of agriculture shall appoint a registered professional engineer who shall act as engineering program manager of the irrigation water development program and have such qualifications as the board may deem necessary. The appointment and removal of the engineering program manager shall be in accordance with chapter 76 and the engineering program manager shall perform the duties as set forth by the board. [L 1987, c 306, pt of §1; am L 2000, c 253, §150]

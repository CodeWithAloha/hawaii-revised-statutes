PART IV. VESSELS ABANDONED ON BUSINESS PREMISES OF PERSONS ENGAGED IN REPAIR BUSINESS, PRIVATE MARINAS, AND YACHT CLUBS

Note

Part heading amended by L 1999, c 232, §1.

§200-51 Disposition of vessels by persons in vessel repair business, private marinas, or yacht clubs. When any person abandons a vessel upon the premises of a vessel repair business, a private marina, or a yacht club, the owner of the business or private marina, or the owner's representative, or the designated representative of the yacht club, may sell or dispose of the vessel in accord with this part. [L 1991, c 272, pt of §2; am L 1999, c 232, §2]

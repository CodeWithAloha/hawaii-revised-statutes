§188-43 Hinana and oopu, taking of, prohibited. It is unlawful for any person to fish for, or attempt to take any of the fish known as hinana or oopu, by means of traps or weirs. [L 1937, c 41, §§1, 2; RL 1945, §1235; RL 1955, §21-76; HRS §188-43; am L 1981, c 85, §50]

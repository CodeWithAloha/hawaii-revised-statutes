§147-33 Prohibited acts. No commercial exporter shall ship any fresh or processed flowers or foliage to points outside the State unless such products meet the minimum export quality and condition requirements and are so packaged in containers with such packaging material and in such a manner as to meet the minimum packaging requirements established by rules and regulations promulgated under this part. [L 1959, c 267, pt of §2; Supp, §22-26.4; HRS §147-33]

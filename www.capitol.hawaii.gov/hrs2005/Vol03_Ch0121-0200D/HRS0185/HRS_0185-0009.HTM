§185-9 Obstructing the administrator or managers; penalty. Any person who obstructs or interferes with or prevents the administrator or any manager or any person in the performance of any duty authorized by this chapter shall be fined not less than $500 nor more than $2,000. [L 1953, c 100, §12; RL 1955, §19-60; HRS §185-10; ren and am L 1974, c 64, pt of §1; am L 1994, c 140, §9]

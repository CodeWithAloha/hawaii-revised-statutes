§145-14 Disposition of fees and charges. All fees, charges, expenses, and other moneys collected pursuant to this chapter shall be deposited with the state director of finance to the credit of the general fund. [L 1951, c 74, pt of §1; RL 1955, §23-14; am L 1963, c 114, §1 and c 193, §13; HRS §145-14]

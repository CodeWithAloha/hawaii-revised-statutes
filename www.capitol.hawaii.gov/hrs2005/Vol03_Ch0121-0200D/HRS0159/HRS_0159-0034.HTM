[§159-34] Prohibitions. The preparation, sale, offering for sale, or transportation or receipt for transportation in intrastate commerce of meat and meat products derived from exotic animals, unless inspected and passed as provided for under this part, is prohibited. Violators shall be subject to all requirements and penalties of this chapter. [L 1990, c 139, pt of §1; am L 1991, c 88, §1]

[§196-14] Annual report. Beginning January 1, 2004, each agency shall measure and report annually to the coordinator on its progress in meeting the requirements of this part.

The report shall include:

(1) How the agency is using each of the strategies described in this part to help meet energy and greenhouse gas reduction goals;

(2) A listing and explanation as to why certain strategies, if any, have not been used; and

(3) A listing and explanation of exempt facilities. [L 2002, c 77, pt of §9]

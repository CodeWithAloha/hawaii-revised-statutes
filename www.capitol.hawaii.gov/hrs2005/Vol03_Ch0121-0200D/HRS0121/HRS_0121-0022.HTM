§121-22 Warrants of noncommissioned officers. The warrants of noncommissioned officers and appointments of private first-class and rated specialists shall be continued in force after discharge by reason of expiration of term of service if they reenlist on the day following their discharge and the vacancy caused by their discharge has not been filled. [L 1967, c 196, pt of §1; HRS §121-22]

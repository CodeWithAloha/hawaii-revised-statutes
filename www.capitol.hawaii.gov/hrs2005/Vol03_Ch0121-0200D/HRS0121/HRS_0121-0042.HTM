§121-42 No pay without orders. No officer or enlisted member of the army or air national guard shall be entitled to receive the pay herein provided unless the orders detailing the officer or enlisted member for duty or subsequent orders, specify that pay is to be received for the service rendered. [L 1967, c 196, pt of §1; HRS §121-42; am L 1969, c 15, §1(c); gen ch 1985]

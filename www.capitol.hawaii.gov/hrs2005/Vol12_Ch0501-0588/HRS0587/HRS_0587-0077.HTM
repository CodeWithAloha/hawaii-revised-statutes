§587-77 Failure to comply with terms or conditions of an order of the court. If a party fails to comply with the terms and conditions of an order issued under this chapter, the court may apply the provisions of section 710-1077 and all other provisions available under the law. [L 1983, c 171, pt of §1; ren and am L 1986, c 316, §34]

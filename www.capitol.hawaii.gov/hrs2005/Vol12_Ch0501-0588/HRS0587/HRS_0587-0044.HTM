§587-44 Admissibility of evidence. The physician-patient privilege, the psychologist-client privilege, the spousal privilege, and the victim-counselor privilege shall not be available to exclude evidence of imminent harm, harm, or threatened harm in any proceeding under this chapter. [L 1986, c 316, §21; am L 1992, c 217, §4]

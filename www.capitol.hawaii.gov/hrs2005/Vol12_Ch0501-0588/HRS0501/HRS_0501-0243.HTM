[§501-243] Dual recording involving leasehold time share interests. Nothing in this part shall prevent or prohibit the registration of an instrument which assigns or affects both:

(1) One or more leasehold time share interests; and

(2) One or more interests in registered land other than a leasehold time share interest. [L 1998, c 219, pt of §1]

§516-111 Revenue bonds; special funds. A separate special fund shall be established in accordance with section 39-62 for each acquisition loan program or part thereof financed from the proceeds of the revenue bonds secured under the same trust indenture. [L 1983, c 270, pt of §1; am L 1987, c 337, §16; am L 1988, c 104, §2; am L 1993, c 280, §12]

[§514A-124] Costs, expenses and legal fees. Notwithstanding any provision in this chapter to the contrary, the declaration or the bylaws, the award of any costs, expenses, and legal fees by the arbitrator shall be in the sole discretion of the arbitrator and the determination of costs, expenses and legal fees shall be binding upon all parties. [L 1984, c 107, pt of §2; am L 1987, c 124, §2]

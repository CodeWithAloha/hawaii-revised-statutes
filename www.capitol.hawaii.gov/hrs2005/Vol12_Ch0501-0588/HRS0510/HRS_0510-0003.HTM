§510-3 Control of husband's separate property. The husband has the same right to manage, control, dispose of, and otherwise deal with his separate property as would be applicable with respect to his property generally were it not for the enactment of this chapter. [L 1945, c 273, pt of §1; RL 1955, §326-3; HRS §510-3]

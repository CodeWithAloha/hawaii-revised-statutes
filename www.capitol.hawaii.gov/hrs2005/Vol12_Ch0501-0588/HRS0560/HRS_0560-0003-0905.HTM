§560:3-905 Penalty clause for contest. A provision in a will or trust purporting to penalize any interested person for contesting the will or trust or instituting other proceedings relating to the probate or trust estate is unenforceable if probable cause exists for instituting proceedings. [L 1996, c 288, pt of §1; am L 2000, c 48, §3]

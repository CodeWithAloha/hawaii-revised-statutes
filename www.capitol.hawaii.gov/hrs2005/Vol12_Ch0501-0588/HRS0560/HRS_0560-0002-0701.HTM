PART 7. RULES OF CONSTRUCTION APPLICABLE TO WILLS

AND OTHER GOVERNING INSTRUMENTS

§560:2-701 Scope. In the absence of a finding of a contrary intention, the rules of construction in this part control the construction of a governing instrument. The rules of construction in this part apply to a governing instrument of any type, except as the application of a particular section is limited by its terms to a specific type or types of provision or governing instrument. [L 1996, c 288, pt of §1]

PART 4. POWERS OF TRUSTEES

§560:7-401 Powers of trustees. Trustees shall have, in addition to the powers set out in the trust agreement, the powers as provided in chapter 554 and otherwise as provided by law. [L 1976, c 200, pt of §1]

Case Notes

Power in a trustee to sell trust property held to include power to convey property free from pre-existing quasi-easement. 57 H. 577, 561 P.2d 1281.

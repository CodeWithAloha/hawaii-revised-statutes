§560:4-204 Proof of authority-bond. If no local administration or application or petition therefor is pending in this State, a domiciliary foreign personal representative may file with a court in this State in a judicial circuit in which property belonging to the decedent is located, authenticated copies of the representative's appointment and of any official bond the representative has given. [L 1996, c 288, pt of §1]

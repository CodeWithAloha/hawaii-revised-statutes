[§539-12] Application of chapter. This chapter applies to registrations of securities in beneficiary form made before or after April 29, 1998, by decedents dying on or after April 29, 1998. [L 1998, c 63, pt of §1]

Revision Note

"April 29, 1998" substituted for "the effective date of this Act".

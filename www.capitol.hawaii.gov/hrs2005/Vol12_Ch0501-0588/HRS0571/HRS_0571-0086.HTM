§571-86 Cooperation. Every public official or department shall render all assistance and cooperation within the official's or its jurisdictional power which may further the objects of this chapter. The court may seek the cooperation of organizations whose object is to protect or aid children and family life. [L 1965, c 232, pt of §1; Supp, §333-41; HRS §571-86; gen ch 1985]

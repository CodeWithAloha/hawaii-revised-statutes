§571-31.6 Informal adjustment, minor who may be both law violator and status offender. When a child is reasonably believed to come within section 571-11(1) and (2), the intake officer may exercise discretion to process informal adjustment under section 571-31.4 or 571-31.5. In making that determination, the officer shall be guided by the criteria set out in section 571-31.1(c)(1) to (5), taking into account the availability of suitable method, program, or procedure for the child. [L 1980, c 303, pt of §5(3)]

§576D-8 Transmittal of money collected to department of human services. The moneys collected by the agency on behalf of the department of human services shall be transmitted to the department of human services as required by Title IV-D of the Social Security Act. [L 1986, c 332, pt of §2; am L 1987, c 339, §4; am L 2002, c 72, §4]

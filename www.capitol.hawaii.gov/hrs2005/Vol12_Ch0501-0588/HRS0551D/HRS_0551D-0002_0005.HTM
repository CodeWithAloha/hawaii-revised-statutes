§551D-2.5 Durable power of attorney for health care decisions. A competent person who has attained the age of majority may execute a durable power of attorney authorizing an agent to make any lawful health care decisions pursuant to chapter 327E. [L 1992, c 208, §§2, 4; am L 1999, c 169, §2]

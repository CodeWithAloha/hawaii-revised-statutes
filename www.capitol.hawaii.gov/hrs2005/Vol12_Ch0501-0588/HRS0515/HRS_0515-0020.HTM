§515-20 Prima facie evidence. In a proceeding under this chapter, a written, printed, or visual communication, advertisement, or other form of publication, or written inquiry, or record, or other document purporting to have been made by a person is prima facie evidence that it was authorized by the person. [L 1967, c 193, §20; HRS §515-20; gen ch 1985]

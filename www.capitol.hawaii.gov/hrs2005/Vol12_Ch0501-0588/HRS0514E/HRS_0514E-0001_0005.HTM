§514E-1.5 Time share administrator; duties. The director shall appoint a time share administrator, who shall not be subject to chapter 76, to administer this chapter. The administrator shall be responsible for the performance of the duties conferred upon the director by this chapter. [L 1984, c 42, §1; am L 1992, c 202, §178; am L 2000, c 253, §150]

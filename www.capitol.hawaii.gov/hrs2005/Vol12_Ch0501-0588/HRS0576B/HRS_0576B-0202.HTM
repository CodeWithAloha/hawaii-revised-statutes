[§576B-202] Procedure when exercising jurisdiction over nonresident. A tribunal of this State exercising personal jurisdiction over a nonresident under section 576B-201 may apply section 576B-316 to receive evidence from another state, and section 576B-318 to obtain discovery through a tribunal of another state. In all other respects, Articles 3 through 7 shall not apply and the tribunal shall apply the procedural and substantive law of this State, including the rules on choice of law other than those established by this chapter. [L 1997, c 295, pt of §1]

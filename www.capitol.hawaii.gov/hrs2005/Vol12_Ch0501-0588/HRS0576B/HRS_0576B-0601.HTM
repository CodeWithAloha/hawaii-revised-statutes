ARTICLE 6. ENFORCEMENT AND MODIFICATION OF SUPPORT ORDER

AFTER REGISTRATION

PART I. REGISTRATION AND ENFORCEMENT OF SUPPORT ORDER

[§576B-601] Registration of order for enforcement. A support order or an income withholding order issued by a tribunal of another state may be registered in this State for enforcement. [L 1997, c 295, pt of §1]

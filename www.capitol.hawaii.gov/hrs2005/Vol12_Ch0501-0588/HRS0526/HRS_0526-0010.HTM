[§526-10] Disclaimer by appointee, object, or taker in default of exercise of power of appointment. (a) A disclaimer of an interest in property by an appointee of a power of appointment takes effect as of the time the instrument by which the holder exercises the power becomes irrevocable.

(b) A disclaimer of an interest in property by an object or taker in default of an exercise of a power of appointment takes effect as of the time the instrument creating the power becomes irrevocable. [L 2000, c 43, pt of §1]

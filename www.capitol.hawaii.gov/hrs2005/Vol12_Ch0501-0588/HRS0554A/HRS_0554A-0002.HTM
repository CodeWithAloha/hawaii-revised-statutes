[§554A-2] Powers of trustee conferred by trust or by law. (a) The trustee has all powers conferred upon the trustee by this chapter unless limited in the trust instrument and except as is otherwise provided by law.

(b) An instrument which is not a trust under this chapter may incorporate any part of this chapter by reference. [L 1985, c 61, pt of §1]

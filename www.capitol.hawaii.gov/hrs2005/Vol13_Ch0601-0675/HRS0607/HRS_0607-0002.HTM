§607-2 Fees to be accounted for. [Repeal and reenactment on June 30, 2007. L 2004, c 230, §6.] With the exception of fees prescribed by supreme court rule for the electronic filing, signing, serving, certification, and verification of documents, which shall be deposited into the judiciary computer system special fund, and fees intended to reimburse officers for actual expenditures made by them, all judges', clerks', sheriffs', and deputy sheriffs' fees provided for in this chapter and accruing from any action pending in any court shall be deposited to the credit of the general fund of the State.

[§612-9] Certificate for jury pay. At least once each month, the clerk shall certify the number of days each juror has attended court and the amount due to the juror. Each juror shall state on oath to the clerk the number of miles traveled for which the juror is entitled to mileage. [L 1973, c 191, pt of §1; gen ch 1985]

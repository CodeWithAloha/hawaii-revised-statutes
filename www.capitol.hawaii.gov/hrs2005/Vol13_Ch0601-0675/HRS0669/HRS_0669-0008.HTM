§669-8 Recording of judgment. The registrar of conveyances or the assistant registrar of the land court, as the case may be, shall receive and record or file and register every certified copy of judgment quieting title to property rendered by the circuit court under this chapter whenever the certified copy of judgment is presented to the registrar or assistant registrar for record or registration. [L Sp 1949, c 46, §1(c); RL 1955, §242-6; HRS §669-8; am L 1972, c 90, §12(e); gen ch 1985]

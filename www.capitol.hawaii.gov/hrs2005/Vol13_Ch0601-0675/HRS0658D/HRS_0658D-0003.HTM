[§658D-3] Policy. It is the policy of the State of Hawaii to encourage the use of arbitration, mediation, and conciliation to resolve disputes arising out of international relationships and to maximize private autonomy over such proceedings by limiting court involvement therein to:

(1) The enforcement of decisions, awards and settlement; and

(2) Ancillary matters in aid of such proceedings. [L 1988, c 186, pt of §1]

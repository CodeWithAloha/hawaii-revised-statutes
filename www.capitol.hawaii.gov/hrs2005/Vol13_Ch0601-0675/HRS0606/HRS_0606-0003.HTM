§606-3 Seal of court, attestation. Each court of record shall have a seal, the device of which shall be as approved by the supreme court. The seal shall be in the custody of the clerk of the court, and shall be impressed upon all processes and official certificates, accompanied by the clerk's official attestation. [L 1892, c 57, §65; RL 1925, §2293; RL 1935, §3692; RL 1945, §9723; RL 1955, §218-3; HRS §606-3; am L 1972, c 88, §4(c)]

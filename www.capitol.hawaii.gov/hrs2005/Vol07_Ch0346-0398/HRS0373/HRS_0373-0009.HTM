§373-9 Records and reports. Every employment agency shall keep records and make such reports with respect to the operation of the business as the director by rule may prescribe. Such records as required by rule shall be preserved by the agency and kept in the office of the employment agency for a period of at least two years. [L 1959, c 264, pt of §3; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §27; Supp, §88A-9; HRS §373-9; am L 1980, c 302, pt of §2; am L 1983, c 84, §1(4)]

§355-5 Contracts. The director of public safety may enter into such contracts on behalf of the State of Hawaii as may be appropriate to implement the participation of the State in the Western Interstate Corrections Compact pursuant to Article 3 thereof. No such contract shall be of any force or effect until approved by the governor and comptroller. [L 1965, c 118, §5; Supp, §83E-5; HRS §355-5; am L 1987, c 338, §10; am L 1989, c 211, §8]

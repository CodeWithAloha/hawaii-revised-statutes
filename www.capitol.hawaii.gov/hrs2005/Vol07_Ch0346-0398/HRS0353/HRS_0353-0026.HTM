§353-26 Removal of conservator. [2005 amendment retroactive to January 1, 2005. L 2005, c 22, §55(7).] The conservator may be removed, and another conservator appointed in the former conservator's place, whenever the judge thinks there is just cause for removal. [L 1987, c 338, pt of §3; am L 2004, c 161, §13; am L 2005, c 22, §22]

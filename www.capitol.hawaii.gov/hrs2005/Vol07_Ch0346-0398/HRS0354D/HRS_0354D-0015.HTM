[§354D-15] Sale of goods. It shall be unlawful to sell, or offer for sale, on the open market of this State, any articles or products manufactured or produced, wholly or in part, under this chapter. Goods or services produced by venture agreements under section 354D-13 shall be exempt from this limitation. [L 1990, c 341, pt of §1]

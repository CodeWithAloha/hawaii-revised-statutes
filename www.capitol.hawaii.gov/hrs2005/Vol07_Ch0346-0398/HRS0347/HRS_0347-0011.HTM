§347-11 Protection of records; divulging confidential information prohibited; penalties; payments to blind inalienable. Sections 346-10 and 346-11, relating to protection of records, divulging confidential information, and penalties, and section 346-33 concerning inalienability of payments to the blind, are made applicable in the same manner as if such sections were reenacted in this chapter. [L 1945, c 113, pt of §6; RL 1955, §109-12; HRS §347-11]

§346-274 Contracts. The department may contract with a public agency or private nonprofit organization to administer all or portions of this part in accordance with federal regulations and in accordance with chapter 42D. [L 1990, c 321, pt of §1; am L 1991, c 335, §11]

Note

Chapter 42D referred to in text is repealed. The amendment made by L 1997, c 190, §6 is not included in this Supplement.

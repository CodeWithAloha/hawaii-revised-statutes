§383-32 Filing of claim. Claims for benefits shall be made in accordance with such regulations as the department of labor and industrial relations may prescribe. [L 1939, c 219, §6(b); am L 1941, c 304, §1, pt of subs 18; RL 1945, §4233; RL 1955, §93-31; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §27; HRS §383-32]

Case Notes

Department is under no duty to maximize amount of benefits that applicant is entitled to by alerting applicant to possible alternatives. 55 H. 250, 517 P.2d 773.

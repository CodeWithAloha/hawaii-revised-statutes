§329-36 Records of registrants. Persons registered to manufacture, distribute, prescribe or dispense controlled substances under this chapter shall keep records and maintain inventories in conformance with the record-keeping and inventory requirements of federal law and with any additional rules the department of public safety issues. [L 1972, c 10, pt of §1; am L 1990, c 281, §10]

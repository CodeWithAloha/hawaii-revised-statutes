[§342F-31] Rules; specific. The director may establish by rule:

(1) The control of vehicular noise; and

(2) Other specific areas for control of excessive noise, thereby allowing for varying conditions. [L 1989, c 212, pt of §3]

Case Notes

Neither this chapter nor rules promulgated under §342-42 for "vehicular noise" extend to sounds reproduced by an automobile's stereo and regulated under Revised Ordinances of Honolulu section 41-31.1. 81 H. 156 (App.), 914 P.2d 549.

§328-76 Penalty. Any person who violates any of the provisions of sections 328-71 to 328-75 or the orders, rules or regulations of the department of health under authority thereof, shall be fined not more than $500, or imprisoned not more than one hundred days, or both. [L 1945, c 101, §6; RL 1955, §51-60; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §19; HRS §328-76]

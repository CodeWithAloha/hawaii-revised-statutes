§328-7 Remedies for violation of law. In addition to the remedies hereinafter provided the department of health may apply to a circuit judge for and the judge shall have jurisdiction upon hearing and for cause shown to grant, a temporary or permanent injunction restraining any person from violating section 328-6, irrespective of whether or not there exists any other remedy. [L 1941, c 318, §4; RL 1945, §2207; RL 1955, §51-7; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §19; HRS §328-7]

§338-28 Transmittal of certificates to department. Local agents of the department of health shall transmit all certificates filed with them to the department in accordance with regulations of the department. [L 1949, c 327, §32; RL 1955, §57-31; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §19; HRS §338-28]

[§342G-114.5] Redemption center and recycling infrastructure improvement program; standards. [Section repealed June 30, 2006. L 2005, c 228, §6(1).] (a) Notwithstanding chapter 103D, the department shall develop and implement a redemption center and recycling infrastructure improvement program using a request for proposals to contract with dealers and recyclers to improve redemption and recycling infrastructure statewide; provided that:

(1) One award only shall be allowed for each person; and

(2) An award under this program shall not be available to any person to whom a reverse vending machine rebate has been paid under section 342G-102.5;

provided further that the amount of awards shall not exceed $3,000,000 in the aggregate in any fiscal year.

(b) The director shall include in the deposit beverage container program annual report to the legislature a report on the redemption center and recycling infrastructure improvement program. [L 2005, c 228, pt of §2]

Note

Actions initiated pursuant to §342G-102.5(e) shall be allowed to continue until resolved. L 2005, c 228, §6(2).

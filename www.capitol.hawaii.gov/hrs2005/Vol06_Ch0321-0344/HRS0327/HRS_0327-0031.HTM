PART III. UNCLAIMED BODIES

§327-31 Medical, etc., use of unclaimed bodies authorized. A university, hospital, or institution within the State authorized to teach and conduct research in medicine, anatomy, or surgery or having a medical preparatory or medical graduate course of instruction may receive from the department of health the unclaimed body of any person required to be buried or cremated at public expense and to use any such body for medical education and research purposes. [L 1967, c 240, pt of §1; HRS §327-31; am L 2000, c 101, §3]

§325-75 Reporting recovery of patient. Upon the recovery of any person having tuberculosis, the attending physician shall make a report of this fact to the department of health or its agent, who shall record the same, and shall relieve the person from further liability to any requirement imposed by this part. [L 1911, c 118, §15; RL 1925, §952; RL 1935, §1112; RL 1945, §2330; RL 1955, §49-68; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §19; HRS §325-75]

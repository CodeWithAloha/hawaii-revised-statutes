§325-13 Regulations. For the purpose of carrying out this chapter, the director of health, with the approval of the governor, may make such regulations as the director deems necessary which, when adopted in accordance with section 321-10, shall have the force of law. [L 1911, c 118, §17; RL 1925, §954; RL 1935, §1114; RL 1945, §2341; RL 1955, §49-13; am L 1965, c 96, §35; HRS §325-13; gen ch 1985]

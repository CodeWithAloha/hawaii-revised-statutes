§203-3 County advisory committees. There shall be established in each county including the city and county of Honolulu, an advisory committee to the department of business, economic development, and tourism. Each committee shall consist of five members who shall be appointed by the mayor of each county with the approval of the city or county council. The county advisory committees, in order to effectuate tourist promotion and development in their respective counties, shall:

(1) Advise the department as to what provisions in addition to those specified in this chapter should be contained in the contract between the department and the Hawaii Visitors and Convention Bureau; and
  1. Advise the department as to other matters relating to tourism promotion and development. [L Sp 1959 1st, c 16, §4; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §25; Supp, §98G-4; HRS §203-3; am L 1987, c 336, §7; am L 1990, c 293, §8; am L 1997, c 2, §8]

[PART V.] HAMAKUA COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT DISTRICT

[§206E-171] District established; boundaries. The Hamakua community development district is established comprised of all land within the north Hilo and Hamakua judicial districts, from Kaiaakea in the south to the rim of Waipio Valley in the north, as provided in the Hamakua regional plan. [L 1993, c 311, pt of §2]

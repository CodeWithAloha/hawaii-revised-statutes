Attorney General Opinions

Counties must accept certifications approved by housing finance and development corporation (HFDC) pursuant to Act 15, L 1988, which sunsetted in 1993, as legal and binding, but the law did not require counties to certify, record, or accept into their records the certifications of HFDC. Counties may only require applications for matters subsequent to Act 15 certifications. Att. Gen. Op. 98-3.

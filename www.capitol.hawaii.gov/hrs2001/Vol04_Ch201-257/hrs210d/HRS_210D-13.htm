§210D-13 Exemption from chapter 42F. The provisions of chapter 42F shall not apply to the grants made pursuant to this chapter, but all grants made under this chapter shall be made only in accordance with the standards and conditions specified in section 210D-11. [L 1990, c 111, pt of §2; am L 1991, c 335, §4; am L 1997, c 190, §6]

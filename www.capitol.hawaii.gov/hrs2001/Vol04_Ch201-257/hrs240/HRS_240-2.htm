§240-2 Books, papers, etc. All books, papers, records, and accounts of such person shall at all reasonable times be open to inspection by the director of finance of such county, or the director's agents appointed for such purpose. [L 1937, c 105, pt of §1; RL 1945, pt of §5681; RL 1955, §131-2; HRS §240-2; gen ch 1985]

§206-22 Performance bond, procedure. Whenever the board of land and natural resources makes or enters into any contract as provided in section 206-21, it shall require a performance and payment bond conditioned, executed, and delivered as provided in chapter 103D. [L 1961, c 6, §20; Supp, §98J-20; HRS §206-22; am L Sp 1993, c 8, §54]

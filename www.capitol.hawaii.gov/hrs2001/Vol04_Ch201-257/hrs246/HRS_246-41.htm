§246-41 Returns by fiduciaries. Every personal representative, trustee, guardian, or other fiduciary shall make a return of the real property represented by the personal representative, trustee, guardian, or other fiduciary in such capacity in any taxation district in which returns shall be required to be made pursuant to this chapter.

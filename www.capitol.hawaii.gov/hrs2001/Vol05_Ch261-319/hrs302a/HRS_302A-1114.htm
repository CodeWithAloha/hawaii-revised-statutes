§302A-1114 Power of appointment, removal. The department, from time to time, may appoint and remove such teachers, educational officers, and other personnel as may be necessary for carrying out the purposes of sections 302A-201, 302A-301, 302A-401 to 302A-410, 302A-601, 302A-1001 to 302A-1004, 302A-1101 to 302A-1122, 302A-1301 to 302A-1305, 302A-1401 to 302A-1403, and 302A-1501 to 302A-1506, and regulate their duties, powers, and responsibilities, when not otherwise provided by law. [L 1996, c 89, pt of §2; am L 1999, c 62, §4]

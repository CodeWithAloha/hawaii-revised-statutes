§291-29 Lights for other vehicles. All vehicles other than those specified in section 291-25 shall during the time mentioned in such section when upon the public highway, carry a lighted light on the extreme width of each side so arranged that a light from the lamps shall be visible in every direction at least two hundred feet. [RL 1945, pt of §11707; RL 1955, §311-11; HRS §291-29; am L 1979, c 105, §29]

§266-23 Acceptance of gifts. The director of transportation may, with the approval of the governor, accept and receive gifts of personal property, which gifts shall be used for harbor purposes; provided that moneys received by the director, pursuant to the authority granted herein, shall be deposited into an appropriate fund or account before being used for harbor purposes. [L 1965, c 28, §2; Supp, §112-24; HRS §266-23]

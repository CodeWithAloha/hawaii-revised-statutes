§287-10 Application to nonresidents and unlicensed drivers. In case the driver involved in an accident within the State has no license or permit, the driver shall not be allowed a license or permit until the driver has complied with the requirements of this chapter to the same extent that would be necessary if at the time of the accident the driver held such a license or permit. [L 1949, c 393, pt of §1; RL 1955, §160-89; HRS §287-10; am L 1970, c 164, §3; gen ch 1985]

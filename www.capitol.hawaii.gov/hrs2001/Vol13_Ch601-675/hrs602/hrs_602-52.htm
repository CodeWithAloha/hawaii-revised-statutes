§602-52 Salary. [2000 amendmente retroactive to July 1, 1999. L 2000, c 2, §7.] Effective July 1, 1999, the salary of the chief judge of the intermediate appellate court shall be $101,321 a year and the salary of each associate judge shall be $99,656 a year. Effective July 1, 2000, the salary of the chief judge of the intermediate appellate court shall be $112,466 a year and the salary of each associate judge shall be $110,618 a year. [L 1979, c 111, pt of §3; am L 1982, c 129, §25(2); am L 1986, c 128, §23; am L 1990, c 72, §4; am L 1999, c 65, §5; am L 2000, c 2, §3]

§602-2 Salary, supreme court justices. [2000 amendment retroactive to July 1, 1999. L 2000, c 2, §7.] Effective July 1, 1999, the salary of the chief justice of the supreme court shall be $105,206 a year and the salary of each associate justice of the supreme court shall be $104,096 a year. Effective July 1, 2000, the salary of the chief justice of the supreme court shall be $116,779 a year and the salary of each associate justice of the supreme court shall be $115,547 a year.

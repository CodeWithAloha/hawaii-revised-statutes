§602-51 How constituted. The intermediate appellate court shall consist of a chief judge and five associate judges. The chief judge, who shall be specifically selected, shall supervise the administrative duties of the court. [L 1979, c 111, pt of §3; am L 1992, c 253, §2; am L 2001, c 248, §1]

[§667-39] Right to enforce this part. The foreclosing mortgagee, any other creditor having a recorded lien on the mortgaged property before the recordation of the notice of default under section 667-23, the borrower, and the mortgagor, may enforce this part by bringing an action in the circuit court of the circuit where the mortgaged property is located. [L 1998, c 122, pt of §1]

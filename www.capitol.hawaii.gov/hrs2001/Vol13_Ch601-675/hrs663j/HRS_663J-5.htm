[§663J-5] Damages. A person entitled to bring an action under section 663J-3 may recover all of the following damages:

(1) Economic damages proximately caused by coercion into prostitution;

(2) Noneconomic damages proximately caused by coercion into prostitution;

(3) Exemplary damages;

(4) Reasonable attorney's fees; and

(5) Costs of suit, including reasonable expenses for expert testimony. [L 1999, c 203, pt of §2]

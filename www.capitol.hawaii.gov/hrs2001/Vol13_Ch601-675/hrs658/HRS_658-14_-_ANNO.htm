Case Notes

Where, under §658-12, "judgment" was circuit court’s written order granting motion to confirm arbitration award, licensor was authorized to sue licensee under this section for unpaid portion of lease rent in any court that had relevant subject matter jurisdiction. 92 H. 209 (App.), 990 P.2d 107.

Mentioned: 74 H. 210, 847 P.2d 652.

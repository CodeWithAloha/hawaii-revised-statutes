§658-6 Application to court heard in summary way. Any application to the court hereunder shall be made and heard in a summary way in the manner provided for the making and hearing of motions, except as otherwise herein expressly provided. [CC 1859, §930; RL 1925, §2929; am L 1925, c 276, pt of §1; RL 1935, §4175; RL 1945, §8706; RL 1955, §188-6; HRS §658-6; am L 1972, c 90, §2(e)]

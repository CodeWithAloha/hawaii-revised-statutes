Case Notes

Powers of deputy clerks, in absence of clerk, to select jurors and issue process under former statutes. 7 H. 388; 9 H. 522, 540. Authority of clerks discussed. 8 H. 191.

Removal of deputy for misconduct, etc., requires opportunity to be heard, disloyalty to government is cause for removal. 9 H. 681. Where more judges than one in a circuit, appointment or removal of a clerk requires presence, actual or constructive, of all and concurrent action of majority. 22 H. 557, 568.

Cited: 32 H. 995, 997.

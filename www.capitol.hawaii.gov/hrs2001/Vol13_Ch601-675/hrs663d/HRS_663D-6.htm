[§663D-6] Illegal drug market target community. A person whose participation in the illegal drug market constitutes the following level offense shall be considered to have the following illegal drug market target community:

(1) For a level one offense, the area identified by the tax map section in which the defendant's place of participation is situated;

(2) For a level two offense, the area identified by the tax map zone in which the defendant's place of participation is situated;

(3) For a level three offense, the county, provided that in the case of Maui and Kauai counties the target community shall be any island in the respective county;

(4) For a level four offense, the entire State. [L 1995, c 203, pt of §2]

Case Notes

Where jury trial demanded, no jurisdiction to commit defendant for trial to circuit court in the absence of evidence tending to show commission of offense. 22 H. 614.

No jury, first instance, trivial offense. 27 H. 844, 847.

"Right" to jury trial refers to constitutional guarantee involving "serious" offenses, in absence of specific statutory right to jury trial. 51 H. 612, 466 P.2d 422.

Cited: 23 H. 91, 96, 767; 27 H. 237, 240; 45 H. 109, 110, 363 P.2d 790.

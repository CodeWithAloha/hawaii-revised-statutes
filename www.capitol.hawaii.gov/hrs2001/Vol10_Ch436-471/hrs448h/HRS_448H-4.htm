§448H-4 Meetings. The board shall meet not less than twice a year at a time and place as determined by the board. The board shall also meet but not later than thirty days prior to the licensing examination pursuant to section 448H-5(2) in order to evaluate applications therefor. Any board member who misses two consecutive meetings of the board or fifty per cent of the meetings in a year shall be removed from the board.

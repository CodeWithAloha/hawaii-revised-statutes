[§437D-11] Acknowledgment by lessee. No lessor shall rent a motor vehicle to a lessee until the lessee has acknowledged that the lessee understands the information described in section 437D-5(a)(1), (2), (3), (5), and (6), and that restrictions or conditions apply. The acknowledgment shall be written in plain language on the rental agreement and signed by the lessee. [L 1988, c 251, pt of §2]

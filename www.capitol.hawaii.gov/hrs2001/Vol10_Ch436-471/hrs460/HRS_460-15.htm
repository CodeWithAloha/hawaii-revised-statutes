§460-15 Remedies or penalties cumulative. Unless otherwise expressly provided, the remedies or penalties provided by this chapter are cumulative to each other and to the remedies or penalties available under all other laws in this State. [L 1949, c 120, pt of §2; RL 1955, §70-15; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §19; HRS §460-15; am L 1969, c 139, §8; am L 1986, c 211, §8]

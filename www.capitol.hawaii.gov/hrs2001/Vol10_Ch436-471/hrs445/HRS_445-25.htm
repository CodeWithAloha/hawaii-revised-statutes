§445-25 Description of goods sold considered warranties. Any person selling, disposing of, or offering for sale at public auction any stock of merchandise for the purpose of closing out the same as provided in section 445-22, shall, in describing the same, be truthful with respect to the cost, character, quality, kind, and description of the same, and such descriptions shall, for the purpose of the sale be considered as warranties. [L 1931, c 190, pt of §1; RL 1935, §2421; RL 1945, §7022; RL 1955, §155-22; HRS §445-25]

§440-17 Admission tickets. All tickets of admission to any boxing contest for which admission fees are charged and received shall have printed clearly upon the face thereof the purchase price of same, and no ticket shall be sold for more than the price as printed thereon. [L 1929, c 216, §20; RL 1935, §7007; RL 1945, §7568; RL 1955, §165-19; HRS §440-17; am L 1983, c 241, §14]

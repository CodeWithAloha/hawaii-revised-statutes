§440-5 Deputy commissioners. The director of commerce and consumer affairs may appoint and remove deputy commissioners. The boxing commission may direct deputy commissioners or any one or more of them to be present at any boxing contest and, in the absence of the commission or a member thereof, to superintend and control the boxing contest, in accordance with this chapter and the rules passed by the commission pursuant thereto. The deputy commissioners shall make a written report to the secretary in the manner and form prescribed by the commission of the conditions prevailing at every contest. [L 1949, c 264, §2; am L 1953, c 165, §1; RL 1955, §165-5; am imp L 1963, c 21, §1 and c 114, §3; HRS §440-5; am L 1983, c 241, §3]

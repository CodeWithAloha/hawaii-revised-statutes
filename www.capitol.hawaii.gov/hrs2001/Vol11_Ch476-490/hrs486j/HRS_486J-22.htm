[§486J-22] Duties of the council. The council shall:

(1) Advise the department of trends and activities in the retail petroleum industry that may require statutory consideration; and

(2) Take such other action as may be necessary to ensure that the department is informed of all relevant developments in the retail petroleum industry affecting the health, safety, and welfare of the people of this State. [L 1997, c 257, pt of §2]

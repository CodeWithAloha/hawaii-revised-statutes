[§200-31] Vessels required to be registered and numbered. Every undocumented vessel shall be registered and numbered before its use or operation on or in the waters of the State on an annual basis in accordance with the rules of the department except:

(1) Foreign vessels temporarily using the waters of this State;

(2) Public vessels of the United States;

(3) Ships' life boats; and

(4) Other vessels exempted by the department, if federal laws and requirements permit the department to exempt the vessels. [L 1991, c 272, pt of §2]

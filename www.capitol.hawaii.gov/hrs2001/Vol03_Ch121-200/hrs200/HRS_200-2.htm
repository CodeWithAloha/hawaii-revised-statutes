§200-2 Board of land and natural resources, powers and duties. The board shall have the primary responsibility for administering the ocean recreation and coastal areas programs and performing the functions heretofore performed by the department of transportation and the department of public safety in the areas of boating safety, conservation, search and rescue, and security of small boat harbor environs. [L 1991, c 272, pt of §2; am L 1996, c 296, §3]

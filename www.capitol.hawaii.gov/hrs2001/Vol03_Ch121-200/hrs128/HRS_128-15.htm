§128-15 Status of regular government employees. If any government officer or employee, state or county, is engaged in carrying out this chapter in lieu of the officer's or employee's regular office or employment, the amount of the officer's or employee's compensation shall not be adversely affected, and the officer's or employee's rights in or under the laws relating to vacations and leaves, the retirement system, civil service or the like, shall not be adversely affected. [L 1951, c 268, pt of §2; RL 1955, §359-15; HRS §128-15; gen ch 1985]

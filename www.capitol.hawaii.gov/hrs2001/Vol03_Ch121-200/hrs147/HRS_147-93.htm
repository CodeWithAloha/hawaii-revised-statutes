§147-93 Cooperating with federal authority. The department of agriculture may enter into a cooperative agreement with the meat grading service of the United States Department of Agriculture and may, to the extent funds are available in the division of animal industry special fund, pay the salaries, or portions thereof, of federal employees providing services under such agreement. [L 1957, c 172, §1(c); am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §22; am L 1961, c 132, §2; Supp, §22-92; HRS §147-93]

§190D-32 Rules. [Repeal and reenactment on July 1, 2004. L 1999, c 176, §11.] The board may adopt such rules as are necessary and appropriate to carry out the purposes and provisions of this chapter. The adoption of these rules shall be in accordance with chapter 91. [L 1986, c 91, pt of §1; am L 1999, c 176, §8]

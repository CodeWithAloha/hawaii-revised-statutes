§145-5 Reports of consignment sales. Every commission merchant shall make a written report to the producer for farm produce handled on consignment in behalf of said producer which shall be within such time and in such detail as may be prescribed by the department. Making a false or incorrect report shall constitute a misdemeanor under section 145-12. [L 1951, c 74, pt of §1; RL 1955, §23-5; HRS §145-5; am L 1972, c 39, §3]

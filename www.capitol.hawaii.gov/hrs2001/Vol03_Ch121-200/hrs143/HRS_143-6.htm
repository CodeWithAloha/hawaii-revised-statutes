§143-6 Lost dog tags. If any license tag is lost or stolen, the person to whom the tag was issued shall be entitled to receive a duplicate thereof by presenting to the director of finance who issued the license the original license and satisfactory proof that the tag was lost or stolen. [L 1941, c 268, §6; RL 1945, §7156; RL 1955, §156-6; HRS §143-6; am L 1987, c 201, §1]

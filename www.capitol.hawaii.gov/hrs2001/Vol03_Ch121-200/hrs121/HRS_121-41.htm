§121-41 Uniform maintenance allowance for enlisted personnel. Enlisted personnel of the army or air national guard while participating in annual training or year-round training, when federal pay is authorized, or when ordered to state active duty in accordance with section 121-30, shall, except when actually called or ordered into the service of the United States by or under the authority of the President, receive a uniform maintenance allowance of $1.50 per day from the State. [L 1967, c 196, pt of §1; HRS §121-41; am L 1969, c 15, §1(b); am L 1975, c 49, §2]

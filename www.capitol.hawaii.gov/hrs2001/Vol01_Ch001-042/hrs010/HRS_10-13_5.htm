§10-13.5 Use of public land trust proceeds. [This section supersedes the section printed in the HRS. By OHA v. State, 96 H. 388, and L 1990, c 304, §16, the amendment made by L 1990, c 304, §7 is deleted from this section.] Twenty per cent of all funds derived from the public land trust, described in section 10-3, shall be expended by the office, as defined in section 10-2, for the purposes of this chapter. [L 1980, c 273, §1; am L 1990, c 304, §§7, 16]

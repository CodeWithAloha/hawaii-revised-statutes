PART III. SALARIES, CERTAIN STATE OFFICERS

§26-51 Governor; lieutenant governor. Effective January 1, 1989, and January 1, 1990, the salary of the governor of the State shall be $90,699 and $94,780 a year, respectively. Effective January 1, 1989, and January 1, 1990, the salary of the lieutenant governor shall be $86,164 and $90,041 a year, respectively.

[§431:9B-109] Examination authority. (a) A reinsurance intermediary shall be subject to examination by the commissioner. The commissioner shall have access to all books, bank accounts, and records of the reinsurance intermediary in a form usable by the commissioner.

(b) A reinsurance intermediary-manager may be examined as if it were the reinsurer. [L 1992, c 176, pt of §6]

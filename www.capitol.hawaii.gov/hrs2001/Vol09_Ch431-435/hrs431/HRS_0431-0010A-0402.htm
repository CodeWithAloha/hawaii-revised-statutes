§431:10A-402 Definitions. Unless the context otherwise requires, for the purposes of this part:

(1) Association means a voluntary unincorporated association formed for the purpose of enabling cooperative action to provide disability insurance as defined in section 431:1-205, in accordance with this part in this or any other state having legislation enabling the issuance of insurance of the type provided in this part.

(2) Insurer means any insurance company authorized to transact disability insurance in this State.

(3) Extended health insurance means hospital, surgical, and medical expense insurance provided by a policy issued as provided by this part. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2]

PART II. GENERAL RULES

§431:10-201 Scope. The provisions of this part shall apply to all classes of insurance except:

(1) Ocean marine insurance, and

(2) Surplus line insurance, as defined in section 431:8-102, and

(3) Life or disability insurances, provided the contracts are neither issued for delivery in this State nor delivered in this State. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2]

§431:10C-203 Rate filings. The commissioner may accept from an advisory organization basic standards, manuals of classification, territories, endorsements, forms, and other materials, not dealing with rates, for reference filings by insurers. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2 as superseded by c 348, §17; am L 1989, c 207, §13; am L 1992, c 124, §4]

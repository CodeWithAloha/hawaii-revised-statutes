§431:10C-120 Prohibitions, penalty. (a) No insurer shall issue or offer to issue any policy which the insurer represents is a motor vehicle insurance policy unless such insurer meets the requirements of this article.

(b) [Subsection effective until June 30, 2002. For subsection effective July 1, 2002, see below.] Any insurer, any general agent, agent, solicitor, or representative of an insurer who violates subsection (a) shall be subject to section 431:10C-117.

(b) [Subsection effective July 1, 2002. For subsection effective until June 30, 2002, see above.] Any insurer, any producer, or any representative of an insurer who violates subsection (a) shall be subject to section 431:10C-117. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2; am L 1989, c 195, §34; am L 1997, c 251, §30; am L 1998, c 275, §11; am L 2001, c 216, §19]

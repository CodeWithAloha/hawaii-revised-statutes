[§431:9A-129] Penalty. [Section effective July 1, 2002.] (a) The commissioner shall sanction the license of any licensee who has submitted an invalid, false, or fraudulent certificate of completion, subject to the right of a licensee to have a hearing as provided in chapter 91.

(b) The commissioner shall revoke the approval of an approved course provider who has issued a certificate of completion to a licensee who has not attended the continuing education course [or] to a licensee who has not met the course requirements, subject to the right of an approved course provider to have a hearing as provided in section 431:2-308. [L 2001, c 216, pt of §2]

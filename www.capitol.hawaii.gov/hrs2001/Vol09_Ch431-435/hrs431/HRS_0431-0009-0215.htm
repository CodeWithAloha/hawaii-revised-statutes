§431:9-215 Number of licenses required. [Section repealed July 1, 2002. L 2001, c 216, §38.] A general agent, subagent or solicitor is required to have but one license inclusive of all classes of insurance the agent, subagent or solicitor is licensed to handle, regardless of the number of principals whom the agent, subagent or solicitor represents. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2]

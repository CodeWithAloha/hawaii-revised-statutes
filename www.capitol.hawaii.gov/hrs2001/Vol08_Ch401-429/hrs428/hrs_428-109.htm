[§428-109] Resignation of agent for service of process. (a) An agent for service of process of a limited liability company or a foreign limited liability company may resign by delivering to the director for filing a record of the statement of resignation.

(b) After filing a statement of resignation, the director shall mail a copy to the designated office and another copy to the limited liability company or foreign limited liability company at its principal office.

(c) An agency is terminated on the thirty-first day after the statement is filed in the office of the director. [L 1996, c 92, pt of §1]

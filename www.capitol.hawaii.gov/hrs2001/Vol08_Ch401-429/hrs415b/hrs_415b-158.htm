§415B-158 Penalties imposed upon directors and officers. Any director and officer of a foreign or domestic corporation who, within the time prescribed by this chapter, fails or refuses to truthfully and fully answer interrogatories initiated by the director pursuant to this chapter, or who signs any articles, statement, report, application, or other document delivered or delivered to and filed by the director which is known to be false in any material respect, shall be subject to a class C felony. [L 1985, c 270, pt of §1; am L 1987, c 135, §204]

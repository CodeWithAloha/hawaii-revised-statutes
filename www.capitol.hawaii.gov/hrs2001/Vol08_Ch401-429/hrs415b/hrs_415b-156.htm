§415B-156 Miscellaneous charges. The director shall charge and collect:

(1) For furnishing a certified copy of any document, instrument, or paper relating to a corporation, 25 cents a page and $20 for the certificate and affixing the seal thereto; and

(2) At the time of any service of process on the director as agent for service of process of a corporation, $25, which amount may be recovered as taxable costs by the party to the action causing the service to be made if that party prevails in the action. [L 1985, c 270, pt of §1; am L 1987, c 135, §202; am L 2001, c 129, §58]

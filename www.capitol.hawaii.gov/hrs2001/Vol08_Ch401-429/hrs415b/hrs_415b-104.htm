§415B-104 Decree of involuntary dissolution. In proceedings to liquidate the assets and affairs of a corporation, when the costs and expenses of the proceedings and all debts, obligations, and liabilities of the corporation have been paid and discharged and all of its remaining property and assets distributed pursuant to this chapter, or in case the corporation's property and assets are not sufficient to satisfy and discharge the costs, expenses, debts, and obligations, and all of the property and assets have been applied so far as they will go to their payment, the court shall enter a decree dissolving the corporation and the existence of the corporation shall cease. [L 1985, c 270, pt of §1; am L 1987, c 135, §178]

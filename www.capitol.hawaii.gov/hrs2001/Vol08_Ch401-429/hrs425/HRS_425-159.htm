§425-159 Correcting, amending, restating, and amending and restating the statement of foreign qualification; voluntary cancellation. (a) A foreign limited liability partnership shall correct, amend, restate, and amend and restate the statement of qualification in the same manner prescribed for domestic limited liability partnerships in section 425-154.

(b) A foreign limited liability partnership validly elected under this chapter may voluntarily cancel its limited liability status in an amendment to a statement of qualification. [L 2000, c 218, pt of §1; am L 2001, c 241, §6]

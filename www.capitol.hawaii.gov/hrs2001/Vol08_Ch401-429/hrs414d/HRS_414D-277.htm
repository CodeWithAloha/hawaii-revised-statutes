[§414D-277] Registered office and registered agent of foreign corporation. Each foreign corporation authorized to transact business in this State must continuously maintain in this State:

(1) A registered office with the same address as that of its registered agent; and

(2) A registered agent, who may be:

(A) An individual who resides in this State and whose office is identical with the registered office;

(B) A domestic business or nonprofit corporation whose office is identical with the registered office; or

(C) A foreign business or nonprofit corporation authorized to transact business in this State whose office is identical with the registered office. [L 2001, c 105, pt of §1]

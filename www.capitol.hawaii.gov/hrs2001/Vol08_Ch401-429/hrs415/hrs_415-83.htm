§415-83 Voluntary dissolution by consent of shareholders. (a) A corporation may be voluntarily dissolved by the written consent of all of its shareholders.

(b) Upon the execution of the written consent, a statement of intent to dissolve shall set forth:

(1) The name of the corporation;

(2) The names and addresses of its officers;

(3) The names and addresses of its directors; and

(4) A statement that the written consent has been signed by all shareholders of the corporation, or signed in their names by their attorneys thereunto duly authorized. [L 1983, c 167, pt of §1; am L 1985, c 270, §4; am L 1987, c 135, §58; am L 1988, c 371, §9; am L 1996, c 182, §5; am L 2000, c 219, §13]

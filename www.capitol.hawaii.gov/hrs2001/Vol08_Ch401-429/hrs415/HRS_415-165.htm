[§425-156] Foreign limited liability partnerships. (a) Before transacting business in this State, a foreign limited liability partnership shall register pursuant to part I of this chapter and file a statement of foreign qualification with the director.

(b) A foreign limited liability partnership shall not be denied a statement of foreign qualification by reason of any difference between the law under which the partnership was formed and the law of this State. [L 2000, c 218, pt of §1]

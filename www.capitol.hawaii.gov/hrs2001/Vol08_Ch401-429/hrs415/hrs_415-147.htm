§415-147 Application to existing corporations. The provisions of this chapter shall apply to all existing corporations organized under any general act of this State providing for the organization of corporations for a purpose or purposes for which a corporation might be organized under this chapter, where the power has been reserved to amend, repeal, or modify the act under which the corporation was organized and where the act is repealed by this chapter. [L 1983, c 167, pt of §1; am L 1985, c 270, §4; am L 1987, c 135, §103]

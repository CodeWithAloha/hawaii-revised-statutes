[§415-139] Powers of director. The director shall have the power and authority reasonably necessary to enable the director to administer this chapter efficiently and to perform the duties therein imposed upon the director. The director shall establish rules and regulations, subject to chapter 91, necessary to promulgate this chapter. [L 1983, c 167, pt of §1; am L 1985, c 270, §4; gen ch 1985]

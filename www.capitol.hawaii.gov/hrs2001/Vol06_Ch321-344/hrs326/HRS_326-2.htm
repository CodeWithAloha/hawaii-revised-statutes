§326-2 Equal treatment of patients. Every Hansen's disease sufferer at Hale Mohalu and Kalaupapa shall be accorded as nearly equal care and privileges as is practicable under the different operating conditions of the two institutions. [L 1953, JR 41, §7; RL 1955, §50-2; HRS §326-2; am L 1969, c 152, §1; am L 1981, c 185, §4]

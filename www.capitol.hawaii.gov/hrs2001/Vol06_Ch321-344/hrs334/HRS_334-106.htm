§334-106 License required. Facilities operated pursuant to this part shall be licensed under existing licensing categories, including provisional licenses, or accredited pursuant to section 321-193(10). The director shall review the appropriateness of these licensing and accreditation categories. If the director determines that new licensing or accreditation categories are necessary, the director shall issue a report and recommendation to the legislature. [L 1980, c 221, pt of §2; gen ch 1985; am L 1986, c 237, §3]

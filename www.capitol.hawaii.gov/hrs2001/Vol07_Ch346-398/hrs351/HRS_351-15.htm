§351-15 Medical examination. The commission may appoint an impartial licensed physician or licensed psychologist to examine any person making application under this chapter, and the fees for the examination shall be paid from funds appropriated for expenses of administration. [L 1967, c 226, pt of §1; HRS §351-15; am L 1988, c 305, §1; am L 1998, c 240, §5]

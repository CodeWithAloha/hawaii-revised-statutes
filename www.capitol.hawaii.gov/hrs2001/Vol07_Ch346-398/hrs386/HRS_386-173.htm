§386-173 Time for giving notice, etc. Any time fixed for giving of notice of injury or for any other substantive purpose as to any injuries within the purview of section 386-171 which may have occurred prior to May 25, 1945, but subsequent to December 7, 1941, shall be construed to run from May 25, 1945. [L 1963, c 116, pt of §1; Supp, §97-162; HRS §386-173]

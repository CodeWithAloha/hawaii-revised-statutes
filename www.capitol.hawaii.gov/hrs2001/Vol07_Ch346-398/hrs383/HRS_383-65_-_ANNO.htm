Case Notes

"Left his work voluntarily" as used in §§383-30(1) and 383-65 have the same meaning; striker not deemed to have left work voluntarily; strike of itself, does not sever employer-employee relationship. 46 H. 164, 377 P.2d 932.

Overpayment of benefits chargeable to employer's account where overpayment due to employer's error in reporting claimant's earnings. 68 H. 349, 714 P.2d 520.

Cited: 44 H. 93, 94, 352 P.2d 856; 46 H. 140, 143, 377 P.2d 715.

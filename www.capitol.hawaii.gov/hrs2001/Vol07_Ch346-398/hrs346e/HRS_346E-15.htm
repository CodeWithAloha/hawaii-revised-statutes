[§346E-15] Health care revolving fund. The department shall collect the tax and pay all tax revenues into the state general fund for deposit into the health care revolving fund which is hereby created in the state treasury. Amounts deposited in the health care revolving fund, and any interest earned on these amounts, shall be used only for section 346-14 Medicaid purposes. Any federal Medicaid matching funds to expenditures made from funds deposited in the health care revolving fund shall not become part of the health care revolving fund. The funds in the health care revolving fund shall be expended by the department. [L 1993, c 315, pt of §1; ree L 1994, c 230, pt of §1]

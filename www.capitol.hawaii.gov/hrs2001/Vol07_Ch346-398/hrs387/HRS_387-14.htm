§387-14 State-federal cooperation. In the administration of this chapter, the department of labor and industrial relations shall cooperate to the fullest extent consistent with this chapter with the Administrator of the Wage and Hour Division, United States Department of Labor. [L Sp 1941, c 66, §12; RL 1945, §4365; RL 1955, §94-15; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §27; HRS §387-14]

§347D-3 Responsibility of council for communication access. The council shall establish guidelines for the utilization of interpreter or other communication access services by deaf, hard-of-hearing, or deaf-blind persons in state programs and activities, including the qualifications of persons who may receive the services and the qualifications and recommended fee schedule of persons who may provide communication access services. The council shall maintain a list of providers of communication access services and their level of qualification. [L 1987, c 289, pt of §2; am L 1991, c 270, §3; am L 1996, c 163, §5]

[§392-92] Penalties. (a) Any person who, after twenty-one days written notice and the opportunity to be heard by the director, is found to have violated any provision of this chapter or rule adopted thereunder for which no penalty is otherwise provided, shall be fined not more than $250 for each offense.

(b) All fines collected pursuant to this chapter shall be deposited into the special fund for disability benefits created by section 392-61. [L 1991, c 107, §2]

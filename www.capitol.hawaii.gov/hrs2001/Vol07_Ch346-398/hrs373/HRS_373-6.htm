§373-6 Issuance of license. Upon receipt of an application for a license to conduct an employment agency, the director may order the issuance of the license provided that the application is complete and in proper form.

Every license issued shall be valid only as to the employment agency and premises named therein. The location of an employment agency shall not be changed without the written consent of the director and such change of location shall be endorsed on the license.

The license shall not be transferable except on approval of the director. [L 1959, c 264, pt of §3; Supp, §88A-6; HRS §373-6; am L 1980, c 302, pt of §2]

§347-12 Blind shop revolving and handicraft fund. The department of budget and finance shall create and maintain a revolving fund entitled "blind shop revolving and handicraft fund". This fund may be used by the department of human services for workshop purposes or home labor purposes for the blind or others, who, in the opinion of the department of human services, will be benefited by such experience and all moneys in the fund may be expended for materials, machinery, and other facilities and for the erection, operation, and conduct of such workshops and for the payment of such compensation as the department of human services authorizes. All proceeds derived from the sale of products of the workshops or the home labor shall be deposited in the fund.

This section shall be subject to any federal policies, rules, or regulations, which may be applicable in order to obtain federal aid or the cooperation of any federal agency concerned.

§712-1222 Promoting gambling in the second degree. (1) A person commits the offense of promoting gambling in the second degree if the person knowingly advances or profits from gambling activity.

(2) Promoting gambling in the second degree is a misdemeanor. [L 1972, c 9, pt of §1; am L 1973, c 201, pt of §1; am L 1987, c 83, §2; gen ch 1992]

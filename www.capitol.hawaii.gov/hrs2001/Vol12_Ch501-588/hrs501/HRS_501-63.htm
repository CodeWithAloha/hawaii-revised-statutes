§501-63 Appeal to supreme court. Pursuant to section 641-1(a) and the Hawaii rules of civil procedure, in all cases an appeal to the supreme court shall lie from the final decree of the land court on behalf of any party aggrieved by the decree. The aggrieved party shall file a notice of appeal and pay the fees for appeal as prescribed by section 501-218, together with the deposit of costs on appeal pursuant to section 607-7, to the registrar of the land court; provided that the registrar of the land court shall thereafter pay the costs on appeal so deposited to the clerk of the supreme court.

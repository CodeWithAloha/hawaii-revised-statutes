Case Notes

Child support enforcement agency was authorized, under §584-6(a), §576D-4, and this section, to bring action to establish paternity of a child, "born out of wedlock" for purposes of subsection (b), where mother, though married to husband at time child was born, alleged that appellant, and not husband, was child’s natural father. 88 H. 159 (App.), 963 P.2d 1135.

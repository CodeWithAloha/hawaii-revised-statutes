§560:5-105 Compensation of guardian ad litem, etc. Persons appointed by the family court to serve as guardians ad litem or to perform such other duties as may be requested by the family court in guardianship and protective proceedings shall be compensated in such amounts, if any, as the family court deems appropriate and reasonable. Any such compensation shall be paid from such source under the jurisdiction of the family court as it shall order. [L 1977, c 144, §40]

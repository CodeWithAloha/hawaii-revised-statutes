§80-21 Judiciary and the Hawaii health systems corporation; powers and duties. All of the powers and duties assigned in this chapter to the governor or the department or director of human resources development, with respect to the judiciary, shall be assigned to the chief justice of the supreme court or the administrative director of the courts, and with respect to the Hawaii health systems corporation, shall be assigned to the chief executive officer of the Hawaii health systems corporation or designee. [L 1977, c 159, §11; am L 1994, c 56, §21; am L 1996, c 262, §16]

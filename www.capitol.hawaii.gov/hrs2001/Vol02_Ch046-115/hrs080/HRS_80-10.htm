§80-10 Police; court appearance on day off, compensation. Any police officer who is required to appear in court on the police officer's day off to testify in a matter connected with the police officer's official duties shall receive compensation for the time spent in court on such day at the rate of time and one-half. [L 1964, c 63, §2; Supp, §5-79; HRS §80-10; gen ch 1985]

§78-6 Private employment or practice prohibited; officers. [Section repealed July 1, 2002. L 2000, c 253, §83.] No full-time officer of the State or of the counties or city and county may, during the officer's term of office, engage in other gainful occupational employment or the private practice of any profession. [L 1957, c 170, §5; Supp, §5-19; HRS §78-6; gen ch 1985]

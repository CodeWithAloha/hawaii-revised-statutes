Attorney General Opinions

Withholding of salary for indebtedness to federal government not authorized. Att. Gen. Op. 71-11.

Case Notes

This scheme of collecting indebtedness, as applied to one determined after hearing to be liable on parking violations at the University of Hawaii, is not unconstitutional. 379 F. Supp. 65.

As a rule, salary follows title, irrespective of amount of service performed. 8 H. 19.

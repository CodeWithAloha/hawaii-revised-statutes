§88E-8 Deferred funds. [Section effective until June 30, 2002. For section effective July 1, 2002, see below.] Sums deferred under the plan, as well as property and rights purchased with such amounts and income attributable to such amounts, shall be held in trust in accordance with section 457 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended, for the exclusive benefit of participants and their beneficiaries. [L Sp 1981 1st, c 7, pt of §1; am L 1997, c 171, §1]

§88E-8 Deferred funds. [Section effective July 1, 2002. For section effective until June 30, 2002, see above.] Sums deferred under the plan, as well as property and rights purchased with such amounts and income attributable to such amounts, shall be held in trust outside the state treasury in accordance with section 457 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended, for the exclusive benefit of participants and their beneficiaries. [L Sp 1981 1st, c 7, pt of §1; am L 1997, c 171, §1; am L 2000, c 253, §127]

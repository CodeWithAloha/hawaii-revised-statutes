[§109-6] Stadium special account. The stadium authority is authorized to set up a special account into which shall be deposited all receipts collected by the authority from the sale of admission tickets for events held at the stadium, including any money deposited with the authority by users to assure the payment of charges for the use of the stadium. Money in the account shall be kept in a depository as defined in section 38-1. Disbursements from the account shall be made in accordance with procedures adopted by the authority and approved by the director of finance. [L 1978, c 73, §1]

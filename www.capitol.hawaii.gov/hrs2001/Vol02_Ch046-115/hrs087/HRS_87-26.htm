§87-26 Information and enrollment. (a) The board of trustees shall make available to each employee-beneficiary information which will help each employee-beneficiary exercise an informed choice among the approved health benefits plans. The information on all plans shall be distributed to each employee at the same time and in the same manner.

(b) The board shall establish conditions under which employee-beneficiaries may transfer enrollment from one health benefits plan to another. [L 1961, c 146, pt of §1; am L 1965, c 235, §1(L); Supp, §5A-16; HRS §87-26]

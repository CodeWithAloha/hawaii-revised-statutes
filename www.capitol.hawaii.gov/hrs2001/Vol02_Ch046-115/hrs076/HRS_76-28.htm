§76-28 Forms required of appointing authorities. [Section effective until June 30, 2002. For section effective July 1, 2002, see below.] The director of human resources development shall maintain records of all appointments, terminations of employment, transfers, resignations, suspensions, demotions, and dismissals. Appointing authorities shall file necessary forms of such personnel actions as the director may request. §76-28 Forms required of appointing authorities. [Section effective July 1, 2002. For section effective until June 30, 2002, see above.] Each director shall develop and administer an employment records management system and require appointing authorities to transmit such records as the director may request. Appointing authorities shall maintain records of all appointments, terminations of employment, transfers, resignations, suspensions, demotions, and discharges, other employment records and forms deemed appropriate by the director.

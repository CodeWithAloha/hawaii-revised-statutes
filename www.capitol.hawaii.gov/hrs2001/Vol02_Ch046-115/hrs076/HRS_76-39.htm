§76-39 Use of official class titles. [Section repealed July 1, 2002. L 2000, c 253, §59.] Official class titles established by the director of human resources development shall be used for personnel, budget and fiscal purposes, but this requirement shall not prevent the use of other titles for internal administration, public conveniences, law enforcement or similar purposes. [L 1955, c 274, pt of §1; RL 1955, §3-21(s); HRS §76-39; am L 1994, c 56, §21]

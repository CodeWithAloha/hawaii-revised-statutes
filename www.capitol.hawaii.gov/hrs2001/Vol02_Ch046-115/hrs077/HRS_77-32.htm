§77-32 Provisions of previous sections applicable. All of the provisions of this chapter not by their terms inapplicable apply with equal force to the city and county of Honolulu as the same apply to the State. The term "State" wherever it appears means the city and county; the term "governor" means the mayor of the city and county; the term "legislature" means the council of the city and county; and the term "fiscal officer" means the director of finance. [L 1955, c 274, pt of §2; RL 1955, §4-21; HRS §77-32]

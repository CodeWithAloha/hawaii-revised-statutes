§77-8 Functions of the commission. The commission shall hear and decide appeals by employees and department heads from actions taken by the director under this part, except as to appeals concerning the compensation plan.

The appeal shall be made within twenty days after notice of the action has been sent to the person and shall be heard in the manner provided in chapter 91. [L 1955, c 274, pt of §2; RL 1955, §4-5; am L 1961, c 188, §4; am L 1967, c 58, §1; HRS §77-8]

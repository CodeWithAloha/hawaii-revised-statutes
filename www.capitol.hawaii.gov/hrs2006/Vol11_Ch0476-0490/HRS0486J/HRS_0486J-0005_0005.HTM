[§486J‑5.5]  Petroleum industry monitoring, analysis, and reporting program.  The commission shall establish the petroleum industry monitoring, analysis, and reporting program that  includes development and maintenance of an automated petroleum industry information reporting system that meets the requirements of government, industry, and the public while promoting sound policy making and consumer information and protection.  The purpose of the petroleum industry monitoring, analysis, and reporting program is to conduct and facilitate the efficient analysis and reporting of all information and data provided by the petroleum industry pursuant to this chapter.  The commission shall develop the petroleum industry monitoring, analysis, and reporting program in a manner that will result in greater market transparency and provide useful information to the general public and those agencies that are authorized to conduct oversight of the petroleum industry and ensure compliance with all relevant laws. [L 2006, c 78, pt of §2]

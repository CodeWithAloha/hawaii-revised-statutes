[§138-3]  Wireless enhanced 911 fund.  There is established outside the state treasury a special fund, to be known as the wireless enhanced 911 fund, to be administered by the board.  The fund shall consist of amounts collected under section 138-4.  Moneys paid into the fund are not general fund revenues of the State.  The board shall place the funds in an interest-bearing account at any federally insured financial institution, separate and apart from the general fund of the State.  Moneys in the fund shall be expended exclusively by the board for the purposes of ensuring adequate cost recovery for the deployment of phase I and phase II wireless enhanced 911 service and for expenses of administering the fund. [L 2004, c 159, pt of §2]

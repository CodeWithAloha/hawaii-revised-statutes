§290-5  Waiver of public auction requirements.  The requirements of public auction may be waived when the value of any vehicle is less than $1,000 as determined by the director of finance or authorized representative.  In that event the vehicle, after public advertisement has been made pursuant to section 1-28.5, may be disposed of in the same manner as when a vehicle is put up for public auction and for which no bid is received. [L 1949, c 194, pt of §1; RL 1955, pt of §138-32; am L 1967, c 267, pt of §1; HRS §290-5; am L 1989, c 158, §4; am L 2006, c 62, §1]

PART II.  ORGANIZATION

 

     §432:1-201  Incorporation by charter.  Any mutual benefit society may be or become incorporated by charter, notwithstanding any limitations under the corporation law as to the purposes for which charters may be granted.  Any society so chartered as a corporation shall be subject in all respects to this article. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2; am L 2001, c 217, §1]

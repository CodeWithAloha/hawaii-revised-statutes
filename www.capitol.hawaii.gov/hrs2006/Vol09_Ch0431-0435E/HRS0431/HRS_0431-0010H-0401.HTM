PART IV.  UNIVERSAL AVAILABILITY OF

LONG-TERM CARE INSURANCE

 

     [§431:10H-401]  Publicizing of policies.  For purposes of section 371-16, upon request by an employer, labor organization, retiree organization, or other entity specified under the definition of "group long-term care insurance" in section 431:10H-104, an insurer that is subject to this part shall be allowed, if it chooses, to publicize a long-term care policy and may sell and underwrite that policy. [L 1999, c 93, pt of §2]

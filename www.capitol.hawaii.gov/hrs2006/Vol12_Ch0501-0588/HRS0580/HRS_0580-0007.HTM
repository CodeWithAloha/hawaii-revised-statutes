§580-7  Examination of parties to prevent collusion.  Upon the hearing of any complaint for a divorce or separation, the court may examine either or both of the parties, upon oath, in order to prevent collusion. [CC 1859, §1341; am L 1903, c 22, §2; RL 1925, §2992; RL 1935, §4489; RL 1945, §12241; RL 1955, §324-66; HRS §580-7; am L 1973, c 211, §5(c)]

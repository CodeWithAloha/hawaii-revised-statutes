[PART VIII.]  RETIREMENT FOR CLASS H PUBLIC OFFICERS

AND EMPLOYEES

 

A.  Applicability of Part II

 

     §88‑301  Applicability.  [2006 amendment retroactive to July 1, 2004.  L 2006, c 169, §43.]  The following provisions of part II of this chapter shall apply to this part:

     (1)  Subpart A;

     (2)  Subpart B, except sections 88‑45, 88‑46, 88‑48, 88‑52, 88‑59, 88‑59.5, 88‑59.6, 88‑61, and 88‑62;

     (3)  Subpart C, except sections 88-71, 88‑72, 88‑73, 88‑74, 88‑74.6, 88‑75, 88‑76, 88‑79, 88‑80, 88‑83, 88‑84, 88‑85, 88‑88, 88‑89, 88‑96, 88‑97, and 88‑98;

     (4)  Subpart D; and

     (5)  Subpart E. [L 2004, c 179, pt of §1; am L 2006, c 169, §31]

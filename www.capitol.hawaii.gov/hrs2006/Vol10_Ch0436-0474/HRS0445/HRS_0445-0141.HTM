PART VI.  PEDDLERS

 

Note

 

  Section 445-141 designated as Part VI by L 1990, c 164, §28.

 

     §445-141  Licensing.  Each council shall have the power to enact ordinances providing for the licensing and regulation of any person who peddles merchandise.

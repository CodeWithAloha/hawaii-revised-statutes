§469-8  Penalty.  Any person who practices as an embalmer or undertaker without being registered with the department of health as herein provided shall be fined not less than $50 nor more than $250 or imprisoned not less than thirty days nor more than ninety days, or both. [L 1933, c 36, §7; RL 1935, §1456; RL 1945, §3058; RL 1955, §72-8; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §19; HRS §469-8]

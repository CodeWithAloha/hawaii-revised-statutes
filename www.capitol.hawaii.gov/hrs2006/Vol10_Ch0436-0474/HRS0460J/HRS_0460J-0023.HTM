[§460J-23]  Pest control.  A pest control operator may secure pest control work, identify infestations or infections, make inspections, and submit bids or otherwise contract for pest control work.  A pest control operator may hire or employ individuals who are not licensed under this chapter to perform work on contracts. [L 1972, c 134, pt of §1]

[§468M-8]  Requirements for offering discounts.  When a discount offered for any activity is contingent upon any requirement that the recipient of the discount first attend a sales presentation of any kind, the activity desk shall disclose the requirement in writing to the recipient of the discount before any payment is made.  Failure to do so shall constitute a false and deceptive business practice subject to a civil fine of not less than $1,000 per day for each occurrence.

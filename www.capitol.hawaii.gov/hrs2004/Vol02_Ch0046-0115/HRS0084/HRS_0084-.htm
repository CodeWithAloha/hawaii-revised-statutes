PREAMBLE

The purpose of this chapter is to (1) prescribe a code of ethics for elected officer and public employees of the State as mandated by the people of the State of Hawaii in the Hawaii Constitution, Article XIV; (2) educate the citizenry with respect to ethics in government; and (3) establish an ethics commission which will administer the codes of ethics adopted by the constitutional convention and by the legislature and render advisory opinions and enforce the provisions of this law so that public confidence in public servants will be preserved. [L 1972, c 163, pt of §1; am L 1979, c 91, §2; am L 1981, c 82, §10]

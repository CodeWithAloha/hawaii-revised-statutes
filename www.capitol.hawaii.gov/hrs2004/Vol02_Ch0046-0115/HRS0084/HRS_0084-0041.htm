[PART V.] MANDATORY ETHICS TRAINING

[§84-41] Applicability of part. This part applies to legislators, elected members of the board of education, trustees of the office of Hawaiian affairs, the governor, the lieutenant governor, and executive department heads and deputies. This part does not apply to any other officer or employee of the State. [L 2004, c 198, pt of §1]

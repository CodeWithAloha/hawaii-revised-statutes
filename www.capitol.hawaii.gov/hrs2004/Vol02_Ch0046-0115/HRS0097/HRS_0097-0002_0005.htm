§97-2.5 Renewal of registration. Each registered lobbyist shall renew the lobbyist's registration biennially by filing a registration and authorization form with the state ethics commission within ten days of the opening of the 1983 regular session of the legislature and on every odd-numbered year's session thereafter. [L 1980, c 129, §1(c); am L 1982, c 105, §3; gen ch 1985]

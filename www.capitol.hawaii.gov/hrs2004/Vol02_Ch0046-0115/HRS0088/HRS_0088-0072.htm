§88-72 Refund of additional contributions. A member may withdraw at any time prior to the member's retirement, all the member's voluntary contributions made to provide an annuity in addition to the retirement allowance provided under section 88-74. [L 1959, JR 23, §1; am L 1963, c 127, §17; Supp, §6-84.5; HRS §88-62; am L 1969, c 110, pt of §1; gen ch 1985]

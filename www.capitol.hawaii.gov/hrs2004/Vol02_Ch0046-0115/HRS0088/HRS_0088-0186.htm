§88-186 Meetings. Each pension board shall meet at least once each quarter. No board shall defer action upon any application for a pension beyond the second quarterly meeting following the date of the filing thereof. [L 1937, c 237, §1; am L 1939, c 112, pt of §1(a); RL 1945, pt of §6184; am L 1947, c 87, §1; RL 1955, §6-165; HRS §88-186]

[PART VIII.] RETIREMENT FOR CLASS H PUBLIC OFFICERS

AND EMPLOYEES

A. Applicability of Part II

[§88-301] Applicability. The following provisions of part II of this chapter shall apply to this part:

(1) Subpart A;

(2) Subpart B, except sections 88-45, 88-46, 88-52, 88-59, 88-59.5, 88-59.6, 88-61, and 88-62;

(3) Subpart C, except sections 88-71 to 88-76, 88-79, 88-80, 88-83, 88-84, 88-85, 88-88, 88-89, 88-96, 88-97, and 88-98;

(4) Subpart D; and

(5) Subpart E. [L 2004, c 179, pt of §1]

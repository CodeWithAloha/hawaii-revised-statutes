§54-62 Disbursement of funds. All moneys expended by the board of water supply pursuant to this part shall be disbursed by the county treasurer on vouchers signed by the chairperson or acting chairperson of the board. [L 1949, c 86, §12; RL 1955, §146-111; HRS §54-62; gen ch 1993]

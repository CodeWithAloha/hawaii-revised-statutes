§92-25 No fees for copies of pleadings, etc. No fees shall be charged for the certification of copies of any pleadings, order or other paper or document filed in any court, or process thereon, or any transcript of testimony, upon the original filing or issuance thereof, nor for the certification of records on appeal in any proceeding in any court. [RL 1925, pt of §166; am L 1929, c 166, pt of §1; RL 1945, pt of §458; RL 1955, §7-2; HRS §92-25]

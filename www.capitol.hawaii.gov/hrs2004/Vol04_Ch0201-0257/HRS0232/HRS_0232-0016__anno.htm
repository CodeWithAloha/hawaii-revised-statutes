HRS 0232-0016 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Scope of review. 34 H. 515.

Where this section provides that only a taxpayer may appeal directly to the tax appeal court, Revised Ordinances of Honolulu §8-12.1 rejected as void insofar as it extended the right to appeal to an "owner" pursuant to this section. 102 H. 440, 77 P.3d 478.

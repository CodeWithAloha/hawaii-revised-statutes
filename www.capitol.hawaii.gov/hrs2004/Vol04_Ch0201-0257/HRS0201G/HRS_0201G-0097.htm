[§201G-97] Powers, etc., supplemental. The powers conferred upon the corporation by sections 201G-91 to 201G-95 shall be in addition and supplemental to the powers conferred by any other law, and nothing in the sections shall be construed as limiting any other powers, rights, privileges, or immunities of the corporation. [L 1997, c 350, pt of §2]

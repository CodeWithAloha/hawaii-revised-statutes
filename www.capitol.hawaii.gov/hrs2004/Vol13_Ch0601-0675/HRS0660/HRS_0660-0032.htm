§660-32 Remanded, when. If the person is lawfully imprisoned or restrained, and is not entitled to be admitted to bail, he shall be remanded to the person or officer having lawful authority to detain him. [L 1870, c 32, §24; RL 1925, §2755; RL 1935, §4340; RL 1945, §10381; RL 1955, §239-32; HRS §660-32; am L 1972, c 90, §5(t)]

Case Notes

Statement of juror though not sworn may be relied upon. 9 H. 622.

Prospective juror must be challenged for cause before juror is sworn. 5 H. 634. Examination of prospective jurors upon voir dire party entitled to ask questions which will aid judge in determining whether juror should be excused for cause and also, within reasonable limits, all questions which may enlighten attorney upon question whether attorney should exercise peremptory challenges. 32 H. 543. Refusal to sustain challenges for proper cause may result in reversible error. 23 H. 792.

Court's refusal to allow voir dire inquiries into specific possible prejudices of prospective jurors upheld as within discretion. 57 H. 492, 559 P.2d 728.

Cited: 20 H. 7, 15.

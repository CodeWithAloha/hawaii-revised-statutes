HRS 0632-0003 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Cited as authorizing the grant of ancillary equitable relief, including relief from forfeiture of lease for breach of covenant. 58 H. 606, 575 P.2d 869.

Where the court did not adjudicate the rights of any party because the declaratory judgment was voluntarily dismissed, section inapplicable. 103 H. 26, 79 P.3d 119.

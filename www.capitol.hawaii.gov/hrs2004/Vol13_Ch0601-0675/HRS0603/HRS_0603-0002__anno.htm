HRS 0603-0002 ANNOTATIONS

Rules of Court

Acts or proceedings outside the circuit, see HRCP rules 77(b), 82.

Case Notes

As to whether extra-territorial jurisdiction may be exercised, see 46 H. 197, 377 P.2d 609. Prior to statehood, circuit court not a court of the United States within meaning of Norris-La Guardia Act. 37 H. 404.

HRS 0634J- ANNOTATIONS

Cross References

Nonconsensual common law liens, see chapter 507D.

Case Notes

This chapter and §634J-7 are applicable where a party seeks to maintain litigation in an existing case, such as by the filing of a motion, as well as to the filing of an entirely new case. 102 H. 289, 75 P.3d 1180.

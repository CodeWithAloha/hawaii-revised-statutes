[§671-20] Annual report. The director of commerce and consumer affairs shall prepare and submit to the legislature annually, twenty days prior to the convening of each regular session, a report containing the director's evaluation of the operation and effects of this chapter. The report shall include a summary of the claims brought before the medical claim conciliation panel and the disposition of such claims, a description and summary of the work of the panel under this chapter, an appraisal of the effectiveness of this chapter in securing prompt and fair disposition of medical tort claims, a review of the number and outcomes of claims brought under section 671-12 and recommendations for changes, modifications or repeal of this chapter or parts thereof with accompanying reasons and data. [L 1976, c 219, pt of §2; am L 1982, c 204, §8; am L 1983, c 124, §17; gen ch 1985]

Supp. 1411.

In granting immunity to certain persons, section prior to 1974 amendment was declared violative of equal protection guaranty. 55 H. 7, 514 P.2d 568.

Homeowner's suit not barred because rights matured before 1972 amendments took effect. 64 H. 80, 636 P.2d 1348.

Section held violative of equal protection. 65 H. 26, 647 P.2d 276.

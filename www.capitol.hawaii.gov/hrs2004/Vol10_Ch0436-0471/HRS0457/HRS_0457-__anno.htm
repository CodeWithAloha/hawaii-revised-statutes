HRS 0457- ANNOTATIONS

Note

Additional fee (repealed July 1, 2009). Upon issuance of new license or at license renewal period, each nurse shall pay an additional fee of $40 which shall be deposited to the compliance resolution fund established under §26-9(o) for quarterly credit to the center for nursing special fund under §304D-5. L 2003, c 198, §§3, 6, and 10.

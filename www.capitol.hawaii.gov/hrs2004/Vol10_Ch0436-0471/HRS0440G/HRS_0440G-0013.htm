§440G-13 Cable advisory committee. There is established the cable advisory committee. The committee shall consist of five members appointed by the governor as provided in section 26-34.

The committee shall advise the director and cable operators on matters within the jurisdiction of this chapter at the request of the director or any cable operator.

The members of the committee shall serve without pay but shall be entitled to reimbursement for necessary expenses while attending meetings and while in discharge of their duties. [L 1970, c 112, pt of §1; gen ch 1985; am L 1987, c 301, §15]

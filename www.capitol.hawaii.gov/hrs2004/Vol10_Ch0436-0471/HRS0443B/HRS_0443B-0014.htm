[§443B-14] Penalties. (a) Violation of this chapter by an individual is punishable by a fine of not more than $500.

(b) Violation of this chapter by persons other than an individual is punishable by a fine of not more than $1,000. Any officer, agent, or employee of a collection agency who personally participates in any violation of this chapter by the collection agency is subject to penalties prescribed in subsection (a) of this section. [L 1987, c 191, pt of §1]

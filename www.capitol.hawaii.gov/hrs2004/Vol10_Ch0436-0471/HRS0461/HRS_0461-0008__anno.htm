HRS 0461-0008 ANNOTATIONS

Note

The 2004 amendment applies to license renewals for the licensing biennium beginning on January 1, 2008. L 2004, c 165, §7.

L 2004, c 165, §5 provides:

"SECTION 5. The board of pharmacy shall provide written notification of the contents of this Act to all persons licensed under chapter 461, Hawaii Revised Statutes, within ninety days of the effective date of this Act [July 2, 2004]."

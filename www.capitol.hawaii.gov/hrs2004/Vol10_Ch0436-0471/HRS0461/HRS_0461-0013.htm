§461-13 Prescription record. A pharmacy shall keep prescription records of each prescription compounded or dispensed at the pharmacy for a period of not less than five years. The prescription records shall at all times be open to inspection by the board of pharmacy and other law enforcement officers. [L 1949, c 175, pt of §1; RL 1955, §71-13; HRS §461-13; am L 1998, c 70, §2]

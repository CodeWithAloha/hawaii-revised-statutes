§440-24 Number of rounds. The commission, with respect to any boxing contest or with respect to any class of contestants, may limit the number of rounds of a boxing contest within the maximum of twelve rounds. [L 1929, c 216, §27; RL 1935, §7014; RL 1945, §7575; am L 1949, c 264, §13; RL 1955, §165-26; HRS §440-24; am L 2004, c 135, §20]

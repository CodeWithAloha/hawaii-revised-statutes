§437-12 Legal ownership certificates. (a) Possession or proof of possession of legal ownership certificate. No dealer shall sell or advertise for sale a motor vehicle unless the dealer has in the dealer's possession or proof of possession of the legal ownership certificate of the subject motor vehicle.

(b) Delivery of legal ownership certificate. The legal ownership certificate shall be delivered within the period as provided in section 286-52(b). [L 1963, c 199, §1(i); Supp, §160-167.5; HRS §437-12; gen ch 1985]

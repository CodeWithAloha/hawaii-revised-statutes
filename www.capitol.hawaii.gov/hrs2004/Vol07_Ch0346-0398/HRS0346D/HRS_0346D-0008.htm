§346D-8 Personnel exempt. The department of human services may employ civil service and non-civil service personnel to service the waiver programs. The personnel employed for the waiver programs may be exempt from chapter 76, as deemed appropriate by the department of human services. [L 1983, c 192, §10; am L 1985, c 207, pt of §1; am L 1987, c 134, pt of §1; am L 1988, c 208, pt of §3; am L 2000, c 45, §1 and c 253, §150]

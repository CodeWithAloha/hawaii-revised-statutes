HRS 0386-0006 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Claimant not entitled to rely on foreign law where in proceedings below, she not only failed to rely on foreign law but affirmatively relied on Hawaii law. 54 H. 98, 503 P.2d 434.

Employee "hired in the State" construed. 59 H. 551, 584 P.2d 119.

Not exclusive remedy where emotional distress is caused by wilful and wanton conduct of corporate officer. 720 F. Supp. 829.

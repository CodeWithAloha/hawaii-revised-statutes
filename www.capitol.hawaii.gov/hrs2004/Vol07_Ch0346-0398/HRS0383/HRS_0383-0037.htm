§383-37 Appeal tribunal. Appeals from determinations and redeterminations with respect to benefits shall be heard by an impartial referee for unemployment compensation appeals, who shall serve as the appeal tribunal. The referee shall be appointed as provided by section 383-98. [L 1941, c 304, §1, pt of subs 18; RL 1945, §4238; RL 1955, §93-36; HRS §383-37]

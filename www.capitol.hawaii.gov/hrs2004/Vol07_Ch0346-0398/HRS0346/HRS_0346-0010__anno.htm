HRS 0346-0010 ANNOTATIONS

Note

Part IX referred to in subsection (a)(9) is repealed. For similar provisions, see chapter 346, part XI.

Attorney General Opinions

Dissemination of data in DSSH files is limited to officials and employees, therefore HMSA is entitled only to information essential to its fiscal functions. Att. Gen. Op. 77-5.

Case Notes

Confidentiality requirement insufficient to prevent administrative inspection procedures from unconstitutionally intruding into patient's freedom to decide whether to seek aid of psychiatrist and communicate personal information. 481 F. Supp. 1028.

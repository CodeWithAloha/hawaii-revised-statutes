§346-39 Legal representative. The attorney general and the county attorneys and corporation counsel shall act as legal representatives of the department of human services when so requested. [L 1941, c 296, pt of §1; RL 1945, §4845; RL 1955, §108-25; HRS §346-39; am L 1970, c 105, §5; am L 1987, c 339, §4]

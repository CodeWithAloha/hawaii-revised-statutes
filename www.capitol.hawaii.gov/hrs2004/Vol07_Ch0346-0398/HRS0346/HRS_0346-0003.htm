§346-3 Compensation of employees. The compensation of all personnel in the department of human services shall be fixed by the director of human services pursuant to any applicable classification law. [L 1941, c 296, §1(4); RL 1945, §4804; am L 1951, c 264, §2; RL 1955, §107-4; HRS §346-3; am L 1970, c 105, §5; am L 1987, c 339, §4]

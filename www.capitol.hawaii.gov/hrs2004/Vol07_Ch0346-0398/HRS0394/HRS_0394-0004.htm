§394-4 Compensation. The State shall pay to each person who is enrolled in a manpower development and training program a weekly compensation equal to the average weekly benefit amount provided under chapter 383. It may pay to each person who is employed on a public service employment project instituted under section 394-2 the prevailing wages of persons employed in similar public employment. [L 1969, c 251, §4; am L 1971, c 188, §4]

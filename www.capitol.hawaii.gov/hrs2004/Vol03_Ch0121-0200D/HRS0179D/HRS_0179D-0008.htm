[§179D-8] Violations; penalties. Any person violating any provision of this chapter or any permit condition or limitation established pursuant to this chapter or negligently or wilfully failing or refusing to comply with any final order of the board issued as provided herein, shall be liable for a civil penalty not to exceed $500 for each day during which said violation continues. [L 1987, c 199, pt of §1]

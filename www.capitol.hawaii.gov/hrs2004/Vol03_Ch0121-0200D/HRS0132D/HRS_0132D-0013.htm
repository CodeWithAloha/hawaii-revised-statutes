[§132D-13] Liability of parents or guardians. The parents, guardian, and other persons having the custody or control of any minor, who knowingly permit the minor to possess, purchase, or set off, ignite, or otherwise cause to explode any fireworks, shall be deemed to be in violation of this chapter and shall be subject to the penalties thereunder, except that the parents or guardian may allow the minor to use fireworks while under the immediate supervision and control of the parent or guardian, or under the supervision and control of another adult. [L 1994, c 180, pt of §1]

PART III. ABANDONED VESSELS ON PUBLIC AND

PRIVATE PROPERTY GENERALLY

[§200-41] Disposition by chairperson of certain abandoned vessels. Any vessel which:

(1) Has been left unattended for a continuous period of more than thirty days; and

(2) Is within the waters of the State or on public property, or is on private property without authorization of the owner or occupant of the property,

may be caused by the chairperson to be taken into custody and disposed of pursuant to this part. [L 1991, c 272, pt of §2]

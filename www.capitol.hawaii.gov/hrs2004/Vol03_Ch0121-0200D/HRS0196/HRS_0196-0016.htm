[§196-16] Agency energy teams. Each agency shall form a technical support team consisting of appropriate procurement, legal, budget, management, and technical representatives to expedite and encourage the agency's use of appropriations, energy-savings performance contracts, and other alternative financing mechanisms necessary to meet the goals and requirements of this part. Agency energy team activities shall be undertaken in collaboration with each agency's representative to the interagency energy policy committee. [L 2002, c 77, pt of §9]

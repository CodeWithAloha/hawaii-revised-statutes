§183-18 Penalty. Any person who violates section 183-17, upon conviction thereof, is guilty of a misdemeanor and shall be fined not more than $1,000 or imprisoned not more than one year, or both. [L 1919, c 83, §2; RL 1925, §596; RL 1935, §185; RL 1945, §1020; RL 1955, §19-9; HRS §183-18; am L 1981, c 85, §6]

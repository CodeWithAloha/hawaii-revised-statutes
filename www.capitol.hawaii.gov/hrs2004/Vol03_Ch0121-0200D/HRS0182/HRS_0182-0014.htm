§182-14 Rules and regulations. Subject to chapter 91, the board of land and natural resources may make, promulgate and amend such rules and regulations as it deems necessary to carry out this chapter and to perform its duties thereunder, all commensurate with and for the purpose of protecting the public interest. All such rules and regulations shall have the force and effect of law. [L 1963, c 11, pt of §1; Supp, §99A-14; HRS §182-14]

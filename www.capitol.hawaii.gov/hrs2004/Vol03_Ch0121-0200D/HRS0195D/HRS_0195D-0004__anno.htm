HRS 0195D-0004 ANNOTATIONS

Note

No new safe harbor agreements, habitat conservation plans, or incidental take licenses shall be approved or issued subsequent to July 1, 2007. L 1997, c 380, §13; L 2001, c 3, §1.

Revision Note

Subsection (j) redesignated pursuant to §23G-15(1).

Cross References

Interim rules, see §195D-6.5.

Case Notes

Destruction of habitat not covered by statute. 538 F. Supp. 149.

§128-31 Civil defense personnel; law enforcement powers, when. The governor may authorize appropriate members of the civil defense agency and civil defense organizations, during a civil defense emergency period, to make arrests and exercise other powers of police officers for the purpose of enforcing this chapter, rules and regulations issued under this chapter, and such laws as the governor may specify. [L 1951, c 268, pt of §2; RL 1955, §359-28; HRS §128-31]

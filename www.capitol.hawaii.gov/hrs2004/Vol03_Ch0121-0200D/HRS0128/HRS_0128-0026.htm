§128-26 Proclamations, how made; service of papers. Every proclamation of the governor for which provision is made by this chapter, shall be promulgated by publication thereof, or when immediate promulgation is necessary in the opinion of the governor, who shall be the sole judge thereof, by official announcement thereof by means of radio broadcast or such other means as may be available.

Any process, notice, or order, service of which is provided for by this chapter, may be served by any police officer or person authorized by the governor, any other provision of law to the contrary notwithstanding. [L 1951, c 268, pt of §2; RL 1955, §359-23; HRS §128-26]

§708-827 Criminal tampering in the second degree. (1) A person commits the offense of criminal tampering in the second degree if the person intentionally tampers with property of another person, without the other person's consent, with intent to cause substantial inconvenience to that person or to another.

(2) Criminal tampering in the second degree is a petty misdemeanor. [L 1972, c 9, pt of §1; gen ch 1993; am L 1996, c 256, §3]

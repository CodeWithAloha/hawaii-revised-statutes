§706-649 Probation services special fund. (1) There is established in the state treasury a special fund to be known as the probation services special fund. All probation services fees collected under section 706-648 shall be deposited into this fund.

(2) Moneys in the probation services special fund shall be used by the judiciary to monitor, enforce, and collect fees, fines, restitution, other monetary obligations owed by defendants, and other terms and conditions of probation. [L 2000, c 205, pt of §2; am L 2004, c 10, §11]

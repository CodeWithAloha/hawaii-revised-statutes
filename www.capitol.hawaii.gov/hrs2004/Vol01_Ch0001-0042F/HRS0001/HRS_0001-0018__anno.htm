HRS 0001-0018 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Applied: 17 H. 135, 141; 34 H. 935, 941; 230 F. Supp. 455. "And" deemed conjunctive, not alternative. 33 H. 98, 101. "Or" deemed alternative, not conjunctive, penal statute. 44 H. 601, 604, 359 P.2d 289.

Applied in substituting "and" for "or" in public accountancy statute. 51 H. 80, 452 P.2d 94.

"Or" deemed "and". 54 H. 356, 507 P.2d 169.

Use of word "and" is not necessarily decisive. 56 H. 675, 548 P.2d 268.

Cited: 5 H. 52, 53; 19 H. 393, 397; 52 F.2d 411, 414.

HRS 0576D-0004 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Child support enforcement agency was authorized, under §584-6(a), §576D-3, and this section, to bring action to establish paternity of a child, "born out of wedlock" for purposes of §576D-3(b), where mother, though married to husband at time child was born, alleged that appellant, and not husband, was child's natural father. 88 H. 159 (App.), 963 P.2d 1135.

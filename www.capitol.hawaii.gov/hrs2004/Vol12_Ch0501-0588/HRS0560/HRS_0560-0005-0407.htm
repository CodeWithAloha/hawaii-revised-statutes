§560:5-407 Confidentiality of records. The written report of a kokua kanawai and any professional evaluation are confidential and shall be sealed upon filing, but shall be available to:

(1) The court;

(2) The respondent without limitation as to use;

(3) The petitioner, the kokua kanawai, and the petitioner's and respondent's lawyers, for purposes of the proceeding; and

(4) Other persons for any purposes that the court may order for good cause. [L 2004, c 161, pt of §1]

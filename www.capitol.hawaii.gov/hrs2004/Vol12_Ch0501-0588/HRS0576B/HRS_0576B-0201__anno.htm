HRS 0576B-0201 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

As Hawaii retains its continuing jurisdiction to enforce a child support order as long as the order has not been modified by a tribunal of another state pursuant to chapter 576B or a law substantially similar to it, this section authorized the family court to enforce the child support order even though the father, mother, and children were no longer residents of this State. 98 H. 168 (App.), 45 P.3d 368.

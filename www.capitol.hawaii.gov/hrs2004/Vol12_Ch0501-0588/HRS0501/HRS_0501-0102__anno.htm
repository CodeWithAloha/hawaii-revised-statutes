HRS 0501-0102 ANNOTATIONS

Rules of Court

Filing liens, see RLC rule 62

Case Notes

Stipulated judgment and order not a final judgment as it did not dispose of all claims in lawsuit nor was certified as final; non-final damage determination thus not judgment lien upon real property under this section. 85 H. 398 (App.), 944 P.2d 1341.

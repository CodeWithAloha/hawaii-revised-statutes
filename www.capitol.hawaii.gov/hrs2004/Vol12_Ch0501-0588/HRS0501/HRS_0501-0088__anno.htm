HRS 0501-0088 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Issuance of new certificate to reflect acquisition by condemnation may be compelled. 31 H. 781, aff'd 61 F.2d 896.

Conclusive character of certificate considered. 35 H. 816, 822.

Certificate does not freeze seaward boundary, it being subject to erosion. 55 H. 176, 517 P.2d 57.

Matters contained in transfer certificate of title were not conclusive. 8 H. App. 273, 800 P.2d 618.

HRS 0572D-0010 ANNOTATIONS

Revision Note

"July 1, 1987" and "chapter" substituted for "the enactment of this Act" and "Act".

Case Notes

If the premarital agreement is a valid contract, the agreement must be enforced. The agreement will not be enforced if party did not freely and voluntarily enter into the agreement or the agreement is unconscionable. Issue of unconscionability of provision governing division of property should be evaluated at time agreement was executed. 69 H. 497, 748 P.2d 1362.

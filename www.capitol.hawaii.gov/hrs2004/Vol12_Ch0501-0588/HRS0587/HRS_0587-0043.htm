§587-43 Recording a statement or the testimony of a child. The recording of a statement of a child is admissible into evidence in any proceeding under this chapter if:

(1) The recording is visual, or oral, or both and is recorded on film, tape, or videotape or by other electronic means;

(2) The recording equipment was capable of making an accurate recording, the operator of the equipment was competent, and the recording is accurate and has not been altered; and

(3) Every person on the recording is identified. [L 1986, c 316, §20]

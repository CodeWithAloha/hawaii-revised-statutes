Case Notes

This section does not prohibit use of juvenile record in presentence report under §706-602. 56 H. 75, 527 P.2d 1269.

Defendant did not have standing under this section to seek suppression of statements defendant made in family court because the purpose of this [section] was not to protect adults involved in a family court dispute. 14 F. Supp. 2d 1194.

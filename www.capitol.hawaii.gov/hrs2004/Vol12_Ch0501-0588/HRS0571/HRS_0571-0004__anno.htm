HRS 0571-0004 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Even assuming that, for purposes of §571-14, defendant did not have physical custody of complainant during the times of the alleged offenses, and the family court was, therefore, without jurisdiction over those counts of the indictment, the judge, in the judge's capacity as a circuit court judge, properly exercised jurisdiction over those counts under this section. 103 H. 214, 81 P.3d 394.

§490:1-108 Relation to electronic signatures in global and national commerce act. This article modifies, limits, and supersedes the federal Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act, Title 15 United States Code section 7001 et seq., except that nothing in this article modifies, limits, or supersedes section 7001(c) of that Act or authorizes electronic delivery of any of the notices described in section 7003(b) of that Act. [L 2004, c 162, pt of §1]

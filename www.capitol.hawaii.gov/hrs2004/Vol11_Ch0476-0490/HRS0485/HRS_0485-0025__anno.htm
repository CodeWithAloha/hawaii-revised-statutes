HRS 0485-0025 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Former employees could establish that interest in pensions were securities. 501 F. Supp. 830.

Shares transferred in connection with reorganization were not "securities". 567 F. Supp. 609.

Applicability of appropriate statute of limitations in securities fraud claims discussed. 758 F. Supp. 1357.

Section requires a showing of scienter and reliance. 250 F. Supp. 2d 1254.

Section 485-21 is an essential element of criminal charges brought pursuant to subsection (a)(2) and (a)(4). 78 H. 373, 894 P.2d 70.

HRS 0431-0010C-0302 ANNOTATIONS

Case Notes

Inter-policy stacking of applicable wage loss coverages must be permitted for each covered accident; insurer's "non-duplication of benefits" clause, which purported to limit wage loss benefits of insurer's two policies, was invalid to the extent that it impaired coverage of actual wage loss. 103 H. 181 (App.), 80 P.3d 1002.

Cited: 77 H. 39, 881 P.2d 526.

HRS 0431-0010A-0521 ANNOTATIONS

Note

Derivation. L 1987, c 253, §2 and L 1989, c 276, §3.

This part is not intended to supersede the obligations of entities subject to this part to comply with the substance of other applicable insurance laws insofar as they do not conflict with this part, except that laws and rules designed and intended to apply to medicare supplement insurance policies shall not be applied to long-term care insurance. L 1989, c 335, §4.

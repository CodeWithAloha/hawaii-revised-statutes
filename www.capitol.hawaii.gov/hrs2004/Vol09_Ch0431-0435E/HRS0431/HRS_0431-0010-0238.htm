§431:10-238 Validity of noncomplying forms. Any insurance policy, rider or endorsement hereafter issued and otherwise valid, is not rendered invalid by the inclusion of any condition or provision not in compliance with the requirements of this part, but shall be construed and applied in accordance with such conditions and provisions as would have applied had the policy, rider or endorsement been in full compliance with this code. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2]

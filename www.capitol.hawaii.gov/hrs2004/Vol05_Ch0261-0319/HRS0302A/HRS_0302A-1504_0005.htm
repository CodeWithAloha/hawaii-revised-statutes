§302A-1504.5 School-level minor repairs and maintenance special fund. There is established within the state treasury a special fund to be known as the school-level minor repairs and maintenance special fund, into which shall be deposited all moneys collected pursuant to section 235-102.5(b), and any other moneys received by the department in the form of grants and donations for school-level minor repairs and maintenance. The special fund shall be administered by the department and used to fund school-level minor repairs and maintenance. [L 2001, c 311, §2; am L 2002, c 16, §23]

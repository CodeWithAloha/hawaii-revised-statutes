§302A-202 Student assessments. [Section repealed July 1, 2005. L 2004, c 51, §§23, 62(2).] Any school electing to participate in school/community-based management may develop and implement its own student assessment mechanisms; provided that the board shall include the results of the assessments in the educational status report to the legislature and the governor required in section 302A-1004. [L 1996, c 89, pt of §2]

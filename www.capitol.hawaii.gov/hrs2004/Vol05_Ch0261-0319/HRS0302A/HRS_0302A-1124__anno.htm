HRS 0302A-1124 ANNOTATIONS

Note

Transition to school community councils. L 2004, c 51, §20 provides:

"SECTION 20. School/community-based management councils shall prepare for the transition to school community councils pursuant to this section in the 2005-2006 school year. All policies or bylaws adopted, and all decisions made, by a school/community-based management council shall remain in effect until repealed or revised by members of the new school community council. The department of education shall ensure that schools not participating in the school/community-based management system are prepared for the implementation of school community councils in the 2005-2006 school year."

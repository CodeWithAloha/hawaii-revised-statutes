§308-1 Parking; control by board of regents. The board of regents may make rules and regulations governing the traffic and parking conditions on the roadways and other areas under the jurisdiction of the university.

The board may assess fees for parking on roadways and in the parking areas under the jurisdiction of the university and may install parking meters on roadways and in parking areas and may make regulations relating to the assessments of fees for parking and the installation of parking meters. The rules and regulations shall be adopted and promulgated pursuant to chapter 91.

For the purposes of this chapter, parking facilities shall be considered as university projects and the board shall possess all powers conferred by chapter 306. [L 1964, c 3, pt of §2; Supp, §44-80; HRS §308-1]

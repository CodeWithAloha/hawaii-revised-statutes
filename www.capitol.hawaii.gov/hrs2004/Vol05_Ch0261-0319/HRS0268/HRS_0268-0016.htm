§268-16 Report. The director of transportation shall prepare a report in writing of the nature and extent of the director's activities under this chapter whenever the director deems it necessary or appropriate. The report shall be delivered to each house of the legislature. [L 1963, c 186, §15; Supp, §114-15; HRS §268-16; am L 1979, c 55, §1; gen ch 1985]

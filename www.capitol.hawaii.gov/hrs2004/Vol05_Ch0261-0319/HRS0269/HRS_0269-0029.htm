§269-29 Perjury. Any person who wilfully and knowingly makes under oath any false statement in connection with any investigation by or proceeding before the public utilities commission shall be guilty of perjury and, upon conviction, shall be subject to the penalty prescribed by law for the offense. [L 1913, c 89, §16; RL 1925, §2206; RL 1935, §7964; RL 1945, §4725; RL 1955, §104-26; HRS §269-29]

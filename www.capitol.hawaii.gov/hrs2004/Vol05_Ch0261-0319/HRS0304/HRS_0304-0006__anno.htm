HRS 0304-0006 ANNOTATIONS

Cross References

Suits against State, see chapters 661, 662.

Case Notes

University of Hawaii, law school, and board of regents immune from suit brought by unsuccessful law school applicant. 791 F.2d 759.

Based on language of contracts, university held to have consented to suit in federal court. 512 F. Supp. 889.

On and after July 1, 1998, the date this section [as amended] took effect, the university continued to be an arm of the State for purposes of Eleventh Amendment immunity. 159 F. Supp. 2d 1211.

The university can be sued only under chapter 661 or 662. 63 H. 117, 621 P.2d 957.

HRS 0304-0007 ANNOTATIONS

Attorney General Opinions

The board's responsibility is that of an administrative representative of a corporate trustee, not that of a personal trustee. Att. Gen. Op. 78-1.

Applicable standard of care for regents' investment policy; permissibility of divestiture of investment for unacceptable corporate policies. Att. Gen. Op. 85-26.

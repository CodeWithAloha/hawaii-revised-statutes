§304-66.5 Residency program; defined. For the purpose of this subpart, "residency program" means a graduate medical education program in a hospital in this State which is accredited as a medical school residency program by the school of medicine of the University of Hawaii. The school of medicine may develop a special residency program for the purpose of this subpart; provided that the program, upon completion by the person, qualifies the person to take the licensure examination under chapter 453 or 460. [L 1986, c 334, §5; am L 1996, c 202, §5]

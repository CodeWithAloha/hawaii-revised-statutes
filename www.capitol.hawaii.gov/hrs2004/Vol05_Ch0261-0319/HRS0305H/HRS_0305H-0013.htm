[§305H-13] Agreement with the federal government; federal reimbursement of the State's postsecondary review costs. The commission is authorized to enter into agreements with the federal government to carry out the purposes of the state postsecondary review program. To the extent authorized by law, the commission shall seek reimbursement from the federal government for the costs of performing the functions required by such agreements with the federal government. [L 1994, c 189, pt of §1]

§321-2 General office. The department of health shall maintain its general office in Honolulu and such other offices throughout the State as it, in its discretion, may deem to be necessary for the proper performance of its functions. [L 1937, c 122, pt of §2; am L 1941, c 16, §1; RL 1945, §2003; RL 1955, §46-3; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §19; HRS §321-2]

[§323F-11] Executive branch; noninterference. Notwithstanding any other law to the contrary, the governor and executive branch agencies shall limit their responsibilities to that of review and oversight when the corporation receives general funds from the State to subsidize the operating budgets of deficit facilities. The governor and executive branch agencies shall not interfere with the systemic change, capacity building, advocacy, budget, personnel, system plan development, or plan implementation activities of the corporation. The governor and executive branch agencies shall not interfere with the ability of the corporation to function as a multiple facility public hospital system delivering health care services to the residents of the State. [L 1996, c 262, pt of §2]

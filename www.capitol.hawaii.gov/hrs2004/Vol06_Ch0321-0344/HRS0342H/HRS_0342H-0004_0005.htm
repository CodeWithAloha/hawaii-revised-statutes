[§342H-4.5] Transporting prohibited without permit. No person shall transport any petroleum-contaminated soil, as defined in section 342G-1, without a permit issued under section 342H-4; except that no permit shall be required for the transport of petroleum-contaminated soil to a soil remediation site, as permitted by the department if the transporter provides written notification to the department at least forty-eight hours in advance of any proposed transportation of petroleum-contaminated soil and abides by any transportation guidelines set by the department. [L 1993, c 190, §2]

[§343D-6] Prohibition of further purchases. In the event that any person refuses to file as required by section 343D-3, to participate in a hearing or fails to produce relevant documents requested by the office in accordance with section 343D-5, the office may in its discretion recommend to the attorney general that action be taken to prohibit such person from purchasing more than five per cent of the voting securities or the assets which are the subject of the filing within any subsequent twelve-month period. The prohibition shall not preclude such person from seeking to purchase additional securities or assets pursuant to a subsequent filing with the office. [L 1982, c 53, pt of §2]

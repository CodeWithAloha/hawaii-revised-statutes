§338-3 Registration districts. The department of health shall divide the State from time to time into public health statistics registration districts, which shall conform to political subdivisions, or combinations thereof, or such other subdivision as the department may deem advisable. [L 1949, c 327, §5; RL 1955, §57-4; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §19; HRS §338-3]

§412:10-108 Denial of charter. If the commissioner is not satisfied that the applicant meets all the criteria set forth for approval, the commissioner shall issue a written decision denying the applicant's application for charter. An applicant who is denied approval may request a hearing before the commissioner in accordance with chapter 91. Any final decision of the commissioner denying an applicant a charter may be appealed to the circuit court as provided in chapter 91. [L 1993, c 350, pt of §1]

[§466-41]  Oversight of sponsoring organizations.  (a)  The board shall retain oversight of sponsoring organizations through the peer review oversight committee described in section 466-42.

     (b)  The board shall periodically publish a list of sponsoring organizations that have been approved by the board. [L 2012, c 324, pt of §1]
